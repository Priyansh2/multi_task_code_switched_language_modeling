Olive Oil ka istemal na Sirf khane mein, Balki aur bhi kai prakar se kiya jane laga hain. Isme kai prakar ke aise minerals maujood hain jo sharir ke liye bahut hi jaroori hain. Na sirf sehat, balki iske istemal se skin aur hair ko bhi kai saare fayde milte hain.  Aaiye jante hain Olive Oil (Jaitun ke tel ) ke fayde ke baare mein:-\n\n\n\n\n1. Haddiyo ki majbooti ke liye \n\n\n\nOlive oil Haddiyo se calcium observe karne ki capacity ko badhaata hain, Jo nayi haddiya banane ke liye bahut hi jaroori hain. Iske istemal se haddiya majboot hoti hain. Iske saath hi umar badhne ke saath hone wali kai bimariya jaise osteoporosis , Peeth dard aur arthritis se bhi bacha ja sakta hain. Haddiyo ki majbooti ke liye Olive Oil (Jetun ka tel ) bahut hi upyogi hain. Isse aap jodo ke dard ya kisi bhi haddi ke dard par laga sakte hain.  \n\n\n\n\n\n2. Liver sahi rakhta hain. \n\n\n\nAnti-oxidants ka khazana olive oil , kharab khan-pan ke karan hone wale liver damage ko rok kar usay healthy banaata hain. Saath hi yeh free Radicals se bhi bachata hain. Olive Oil mein kai saare aise minerals mauzood hote hain hain jo liver ki androoni saaf safaai kar usay healthy rakhne ke liye bahut hi jaroori hote hain. \n\n\n\n3. Diabetes mein faydemand\n\n\n\nIs oil mein aise healthy chemicals paye jate hain jo sharir mein blood sugar level ko control karte hain. Olive oil pachne ke baad insulin ke taur par kaam karta hain, Jiski diabetes ke rogiyo ko bahut jyada jaroorat hoti hain.\n\n\n\n \n\n \n\n4. Alzheimer ke khatre ko kam kare \n\n\n\nOlive oil mein Oleocanthal naam ka tatv paya jata hain jo dimaag ke kaam karne ki capacity ko sahi rakhta hain, Saath hi yaddasht ko bhi badhaata hain. Isse badhti umar ke saath bhoolne ki bimari Alzheimer ki sambhavna kafi kam ho jati hain. \n\n\n\n5. Dryness door kare \n\n\n\nSkin ki dryness ko bina koi cream lagaye door karna ho, to Olive oil ek behtar option hain. Raat mein sone se pahle ise chehre ke aas-paas ki skin par laga le aur subah thande paani ke saath dho le. Lagatar kuch dino tak aisa karne se Skin ki dryness door ho jati hain. Saath hi isse tavcha ki komalta ko barkarar rakha ja sakta hain. \n\n\n\n6. Motapa door kare \n\n\n\nKhane mein Olive oil ki matra ko shamil kar kuch hi dino mein motape ko control kiya ja sakta hain. Ismein calorie ki matra na ke barabar hoti hain, Jo motape ka mukhya karan hain. Sirf 1 chammach tel ka istemal hi khane mein kafi hota hain. \n\n\n\n  \n\n7. Makeup remover ke liye \n\n\n\nShaadi, Party ya office ke liye jab aap taiyaar hote hain , to chehre par kai tarah ke makeup products use karte hain, Jinhe baad mein hatane ke liye kafi mashkkat karni padti hain. Kai tarah ke mehnge makeup remover ka sahara lena padta hain. Lekin in jhanjhato se bachne aur paise bachane ke liye rui se olive oil lagaye aur baad mein ise thande paani se dho le. Makeup asaani se hat jayega. \n\n\n\n8. Stretch Marks hatane ke liye \n\n\n\nMotape aur Exercise ki wajah se pet aur seene ke aas-paas stretch marks pad jate hain , Jinhe door karna thoda mushkil hota hain, Lekin Olive ke dwaara iske nishaan ko halka jaroor kiya ja sakta hain. Yeh skin ke lacheelepan ko banaye rakhta hain. Iske istemal se stretch mark se bacha ja sakta hain. \n\n\n\n   \n\n9. Chehre ki jhurriyo ko door kare \n\n\n\nOlive oil mein vitamin E ke saath Hydroxytyrosol naam ka anti-oxidant hota hain. Jo chehre par padne wali jhurriyo ko door karke tavcha ke lachkeenepan ko banaye rakhta hain. Yeh chehre par budhape ke karan padne wali baarik rekhaon ko door karta hain aur usay lambe samay tak jawan banaye rakhne mein madad karta hain. Badhti umar ke saath aankhon ke aaspaas wrinkles bhi dikhne lagte hain aur chehre ki chamak gayab si hone lagti hain. In sabhi prakar ki problems se chhutkara pane ke liye Olive Oil ka istemal shuru kare aur kuch hi dino mein fark dekhe. \n\n\n\n\n\n10. Tanning se rahat dilaaye. \n\n\n\nGarmiyo ki dhoop ka sabse jyada asar hamari skin par dikhta hain. Tanning ke chalte skin 2 rango ki dikhne lagti hain. Sunscreen ka istemal isi tanning se bachne ke liye kiya jata hain. Jankar hairaani hogi ki Olive oil iske liye bhi bahut kargar hain. Dhoop mein nikalne se pahle Olive oil ki thodi si matra skin par lagakar Dhoop ke hanikarak prabhavo se bacha ja sakta hain. \n\n\n\n   \n\n11. Damakti tavcha paye \n\n\n\nDamakti tavcha ke liye Blood circulation ka sahi hona bahut hi jaroori hain. Iske liye facial aur massage bahut hi jaroori hain. Hafte mein sirf ek baar Olive oil ka massage Blood circulation ko sahi rakhne ke saath skin ke glow aur moisturizer ko banaye rakhta hain.  Iske liye garm olive oil ki kuch boonde chehre par lagaye. \n\n\n\n12. Palko ko ghana aur lamba banaye\n\n\n\nAankhe chehre ka wah hissa hoti hain, jinka khyaal rakhna utna hi jaroori hain jitna sharir ke baaki hisse ka. Palko ko lamba aur ghana dikhana hain to un par halka sa olive oil lagana shuru kar de.
