Aapko gale ki kharash se bhi rahat milegi. \n\n\n\n\n\n• Tulsi ke patte :- Tulsi ke patte ko pees kar uska ras nikaal le aur phir is ras mein Adrak ka ras aur Shahad mix karke piye. Yeh sookhi khansi ka Ramban upchar mana gya hain. \n\n\n\n• Shahad :- Honey mein aise enzymes paye jate hain jo cough se kafi rahat dilate hain. Sookhee khansi ko door karne ke liye aap 1 chammach Shahad Din mein 3 time khaye. Yakinan yeh aapko sookhi khansi mein aaram dilayega. \n\n\n\n• Haldi :- Ek cup paani mein 1 chota chammach Haldi aur 1 chota chammach peesi huyi Kali Mirch mix kare aur usay ubaale. Jab tak aapko sookhi khansi se chhutkara na mil jaye, is nuskhe ka istemal karte rahe. \n\n\n\n• Pyaaz :- Aadha chammach Pyaaz ke ras mein 1 choti chammach Shahad mix karke din mein 2 baar chaatne se bhi fayda hota hain. \n\n\n\n• Garm paani se garara kare :- Jab aap paani mein namak mila kar garara karte hain toh aapko Gale ke dard aur sookhi khansi se turant hi rahat mil jati hain. Ek glass garm paani mein Namak mila kar Subah-shaam garara kare, isse aapko kafi fayda hota hain. \n\n\n\n• Nimbu :- 2 Chammach Nimbu ke ras mein 1 chammach Shahad mix kijiye aur ise din mein kai baar pijiye. Isse gale ki kharash door ho jayegi. \n\n\n\n• Kali Mirch :- Kali mirch ko pees kar desi ghee mein bhoon kar khane se gale ko kafi aaram milta hain. \n\n\n\n• Adrak :- Adrak ko paani mein ubaal kar usme 2 chammach Shahad mila kar din mein 3 baar peene se bhi fayda hota hain. \n\n\n\n• Herbal Tea :- Tulsi, Adrak aur Kali mirch ki Chaay ko khansi ke liye sabse accha ilaj mana jata hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPosted by\n\n\nHinglishpedia\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nShare to:\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nNextNewer Post\nPreviousOlder Post\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nLoading...\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPopulars\nComments\nArchive\n\n\n\n\nFeature Post\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPopular\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nRukhe baalo ko Silky banane ke gharelu upay aur nuskhe (Tips for Dry Hair to make Silky)\nKya aap Rukhe baalo (Dry Hair) se preshan hain?
