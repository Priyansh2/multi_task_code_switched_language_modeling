Kam calcium hone ki wajah se neend na aana, darr lagna, tension mein rahnaaam baat hoti hain. Jis wajah se aap stress aur thakaan jyada mahsoos karte hain. Calcium ki kami hone ka lakshan yeh bhi hain ki aap hamesha thakey huye aur alasya se bhare huye nazar aayenge. \n\n\n○ Haddiya Kamjor ho jati hain :- Calcium ki kami hone ka sabse pahle asar haddiyo par padta hain. Isse haddiya kamjor ho jati hain. Agar aapme Calcium ki kami ho gyi ho to haddiya asaani se fracture ho sakti hain. Muscles mein akadan aur pain hamesha bana rahta hain. Kai mamlo mein aapko recats naam ki bimari bhi ho jati hain. \n\n\n○ Daant kamjor ho jate hain :- Calcium ki kami hone ka symptom yeh bhi hain ki aapke daant saaf nahi dikhayi dete hain. Calcium ki kami hone par daanto mein sadan ho jati hain. Agar chhote bacche mein Calcium ki kami hain to uska sabse pahla sanket yeh hain ki uske daant kafi der baadnikalte hain. \n\n\n○Teen girls mein periodproblems :- Calcium ki kami hone par kishoriyo mein yauvan deri se aata hainaur calcium ki kami hone se periods mein bhi gadbadi ho sakti hain. Kai teen age girls mein periods ke pahle kafi dard bhi ho sakta hain. \n\n\n○ Baalo ka jhadna aur kharaab hona ;- Sahi calcium ka dose na milne par baal buri tarah se jhadne lagte hain. Baalo mein hardness aur dryness aana ek aam baat ban jati hain. \n\n\n○ Nakhoono ka tootna :- Nakhoon ko majboot banane ke liye calcium ki jaroorat padti hain.
