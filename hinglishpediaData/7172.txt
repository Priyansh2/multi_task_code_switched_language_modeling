Purusho mein dikkat hone par :-  Aadiwasiyo ke anusaar, Kali Musali (5-10 gram), Badam (1 ya 2), Chiraunji ki daane (2 gram) aur 3 elaichi ko milakar paste bana kar roj raat ko sone se pahle khane par Purusho mein napunskta dheere-dheere khatm ho jati hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n6. Urine Problems hone par :- Jin logo ko peshab hone ke samay dard ya kathinaai ka samna karna padta hain, Unhe Vidarikand, Laung aur Elaichi ki barabar matra mila kar din mein 3 baar chabana chahiye. Isse yeh samsya dheere-dheere khatm ho jati hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n8. Peshab se judi anya samsyaon mein rahat ke liye aadiwasi Mithi Neem ( Kadi Patta) ke jado ka ras taiyaar karte hain.
