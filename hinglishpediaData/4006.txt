Iske liye aap chahe toh self assessment se bhi exam clear kar sakte hain. Khas karke samay ki kami ke liye jaroori hain ek strategybanaye aur usi keaccording apni taiyaari kare. Aisa dekhte huye kuch questions ko solve karne ki practise kare. Aaiye jante hain kuch aise tips jo IAS ke exam ko crack karne ke liye aapke liye helpful honge. \n\n1. Regularly Newspaper padhe :- Candidatesko exam mein success paane ke liye acche se rojana 2-3 Newspaper ko padhna chahiye. Akhbaar se desh-duniya, Sports, Cinema jagat ki jankari to milti hi hain, Saath hi editor’s choice se zyadatar subjects ke bare mein vistaar se pata chalta hain.\n\n\n2. Notes ki help le :- Topics ki acche tarikese padhai karne ke liye jaroori hain aap usse related notes banaye. Notes ki help se aap syllabus ko ek nazar mein revised kar sakte hain. \n\n3. Ratne se accha hain subject ko samjhe :- Agar aap kisi subject koratt rahe hain toh kuch time ke baad aap usay bhool jayenge. Isliye ghatnao ko analysis karne ki aadat dale.Isse aap hamesha usay yaad rakhenge. Iske liye aap Internet ki madad le sakte hain aur ghatnao ke aage aur peeche ke bare mein puri jankari hasil karke analyse kar sakte hain.\n\n4. Iconology, Environments, Arts aur Culture par karna hoga focus :- Candidates ko Iconology, environments,arts aur culture par focus karna hoga. In sabhi topicski acchese taiyaari karni hogi, khas karke inki study ke liye aise notesbanaye, jinme subject ke bare mein full details ke saath samjhayagya ho. \n\n5. Apnebasicclearrakhe:- Candidates ko badle huye pattern ke saath apne basic clear karnehonge. Civil Services Pre Exam ke candidates ko basic par kafi mehnat karni hogi.\n\n6. Mock test paperse karetaiyaari :- Students mock testpaperaur previousyearske papers setaiyaari kar sakte hain. Mak test se taiyaari kar aapapni speed toh badha hi sakte hain, balki iski madad se questions ko jaldi solve karne ki samajh bhi badhti hain. \n\n7. NCERT ki books jaroor padhe :- Civil Services Exam ki taiyaari kar rahe students ko NCERTki books jaroor padhni chahiye. Aap in kitaabo ki short notes bhi bana sakte hain. \n\n8.Sahi books ka chayan kare ;- Kai saari books padhneseaccha hain ki ek aise quality ki kitaabpadhe, Jisse aapke basicconcepts clear ho jaye. \n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPosted by\n\n\nHinglishpedia\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nShare to:\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nNextNewer Post\nPreviousOlder Post\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPost a Comment\n\n\n\naprieztmkrdezign\n218168578325095\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nSubscribe to:\nPost Comments (Atom)\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nLoading...\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPopulars\nComments\nArchive\n\n\n\n\nFeature Post\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPopular\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nRukhe baalo ko Silky banane ke gharelu upay aur nuskhe (Tips for Dry Hair to make Silky)\nKya aap Rukhe baalo (Dry Hair) se preshan hain?
