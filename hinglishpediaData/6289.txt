Why Eat Orangesin Pregnancy period in Hindi/Urdu? In swaalo ka jawaab janne ke liye puri post jaroorpadhe.\n\nPregnancy Period mein mahilaon ko kai tarah ki cheeze khane ki iccha hoti hain. Is period mein kai pregnant woman ko santrakhane ki iccha hoti hain.\n\nSantra ek aisa phal hain, Jise Pregnancy ke liye sabse safeaur surakshit phal mana jata hain. Vitamin C ke guno se bharpoor Santra garbhavati mahilaon aur uske garbh mein pal rahe bacche ke liye bahut hi faydemand hota hain. Vahi, Isme kai poshak tatv hote hain jo maa aur bacche dono ko fayda pahuchate hain.\n\nVitamin C se bharpoor hone ki wajah se yeh maa ke immune system yani ki rog-pratirodhak kshamta ko behtar banane ka kaam karta hain. Iske alawa yeh sharir mein Iron aur Zinc ki matra ka level banaye rakhne mein madad karta hain.\n\nEk research ke mutabik, Pregnancy period ke dauran Vitamin C ke bharpoor sewan se Infection aur dusri bimariya hone ka khatra kam ho jata hain. Isse hone wale bacche ka dimaagbhi healthy rahta hain.\n\nEk pregnant (garbhvati) mahila ko rojana ke hisaab 80 se 85 Milligram Vitamin C ka sewan karna chahiye. Halaki harekmahila ki garbhavstha ki situation alag-alag hoti hain. Aise mein apne Doctor se ek baarSalah jaroorle leni chahiye. Vitamin C ke sewan se bacche ka maansik vikas hota hain. Aise mein pregnancy period mein Orange ka sewan karna maa aur bacche dono ke liye bahut hi faydemand rahta hain.\n\nPregnancy mein Orange (Santra) khane ke kuch bemisal fayde aur benefits :- \n\n\n1. Folate ka accha source hain :- Yeh folate ka bahut accha source hain. Pregnant woman ke liye folate ek importanttatv hain. Yeh Red Blood Cells ke production ke liye jaroori hota hain. Saath hi yeh naye cells ke nirmaan ko bhi protsahit karta hain. \n\n2. Kabzki samsya ko door kare :- Santre mein bharpoor matra mein Fiberspaye jate hain, Jisse kabz ki samsya nahi hoti hain. Pregnancy mein Kabz ki samsya ho jana ek aam problem hain. Agar aapke ghar mein kisi pregnantwomanko yeh problem hain toh aap usay Santra khane ki salah de sakte hain. \n\n3. Kidney mein stone ke liye:- Pregnancy mein niyamit roop se santre ka sewan karnese Urine ka PH level badh sakta hain. Aise mein Kidney ke stone ke upcharke liyebhi iska sewan kiya jana behadfaydemand hota hain. \n\n4. Sharir mein paani ki matra barkararrakhe :- Pregnancy mein kisi bhi haalatmein sharir mein paani ki kami nahi honi chahiye. Is dauranyeh jaroori hain ki pregnancy mein woman prayapt matra mein paani peetirahe. Aise mein Santre ka sewan karne se sharir mein paani toh milta hi hain, Saath hi yeh Fluid mein Sodium aur Potassium ki matra ko bhi balanced banayerakhti hain. \n\n5. Tension door karne mein madad karta hain :- Pregnancy mein Santra khane se tanaav door hota hain, Kyonki isme Potassium paya jata hain, Jiski wajah se Blood pressure control mein rahta hain.
