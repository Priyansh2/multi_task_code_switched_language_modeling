Sirke (Vinegar) mein 4% se lekar 8% tak Acetic Acid hota hain. Sirka sirf khane ke liye hi upyogi nahi hain, balki ise aur bhi kai tariko se istemal kiya ja sakta hain. Aaiye aaj jante hain Sirke ke kuch aise hi gharelu nuskhe aur upayo ke baare mein jinse aap aur bhi kai kaam kar sakte hain. Sirke ke gharelu nuskhe (Home remedies of vinegar  in Hindi/Urdu.) :- \n\n\n\n• Khane mein mirch zyada hone par :- Khane mein kabhi-kabhaar mirch zyada ho jati hain. Aise mein khana phenkne se accha hain, Usme thoda sirka daale. Khane se mirch ka swaad khatm ho jayega. \n\n\n\n• Gale ki kharash door kare  :- Gale ki kharash ko door karne ke liye seb ke sirke (Apple cider Vinegar) ka istemal kare. Ek Cup garm paani mein ek chammach Seb ka sirka milaye aur  is paani se garare kare. Isme aap chahe toh kuch chammach shahad bhi daal sakte hain, isse aur bhi jaldi fayda hota hain. \n\n\n\n• Chintiya (Ants) ko bhagane mein kargar :- Agar aapke ghar mein zyada  Chintiya (Ants) ho gyi ho toh Safed Sirke (White Vinegar) aur Paani ko barabar matra mein milaye aur pure ghar mein chhidke, Kuch ki der mein chintian ghar se bhaag jayengi. \n\n\n\n• Kharpatvaar khatm karta hain :- Sirka khatpatwaar ka dushman hain. Baagwaani ke liye istemal kiye jane wale khatpatwaar  nashak ko 25% Sirke ke saath mila kar paudhe par chhidkaav karne se khatpatvaar se aur bhi jaldi mukti milti hain. \n\n\n\n• Safai karne ke liye :- Agar aap apne fridge aur floor ko smell free (durgandh mukt) banana chahte hain toh Paani mein Sirka mila kar fridge aur fursh ki safai kare. Badboo door  ho jayegi. \n\n\n\n• Wajan kam karne ke liye :- Seb ke Sirke ko wajan kam karne mein kargar mana jata hain. Apple cider vinegar se blood sugar  level control mein rahta hain. Isliye wajan kam karne ke liye seb ke sirke ka istemal kare. \n\n\n\n• Naali saaf karne ke liye :- Agar naali jam ho jaye yani ki kachra phas jaye toh 3/4 cup baking soda le usme aadhaa cup safed sirka daale. Is mishran ko naali mein daalne se naali saaf ho jayegi. \n\n\n\n• Jung ko saaf karne mein :- Sirke mein Acetic acid  mauzood  hota hain, Jo iron oxide se kriya karke jang ko hatata hain. Lohe ke  kisi chote-mote  samaan mein zung lagne par sirke ko ek bartan mein garm  kare. Is bartan mein jung lagi cheezo ko daale. Kuch der tak  rahne  de, Phir paani se acchi tarah dhoye. \n\n\n\n• Phoolo ko fresh rakhne  ke liye :- Phoolo ko taro-taza rakhne ke liye phoolo par paani mein sirka  daal kar chhidkaav kare. Isse phool lambe samay tak taro-taza  bane  rahte hain. \n\n\n\n• Dard kam karne ke liye :- Agar exercise ya kisi  dusri wajah se muscles mein pain ho toh ek cup paani mein kuch chammach sirke ko mila kar dard  wali jagah par kapde se 20 minutes taka lagaye. Dard se bahut jaldi rahat mil jayegi. \n\n\n\n• Hichaki se chhutkara dilaye :- Agar kisi ko lagatar hichaki aa rahi ho toh sirka usay fayda pahucha sakta hain. Hichaki se chhutkara paane ke liye 1 chammach sirka nigal le. \n\n\n\n• Sticker nikaalne ke liye :- Kai baar sticket hatane ki koshish karte hain toh veh puri tarah se nikal nahi pata hain. Aise mein bartan accha nahi dikhayi  deta hain. Isliye  sticker ko asaani se nikaalne ke liye Sirka garm kar le. Phir usme ek kapde ka tukda bhigo de aur usay sticker par thodi der ke liye laga  de. Iske baad sticker hatane  par koi daag-dhabaa nahi rahega. \n\n\n\n•  Kamre se Jale khane ki badboo mitane ke liye :- Agar khana jal jaye toh kamre mein 1 katore  mein 3/4 bhaag sirka aur paani ke saath mila kar rakhe, Kamre se jale khane ki badboo door ho jayegi. \n\n\n\n• Baalo ko shiny banane ke liye :- Baalo ko shiny banane ke liye conditioner ki bajaye Vinegar ka istemal  jyada kargar hain. Ek cup paani mein aadhaa chammach sirka mila kar baalo par lagaye. Yeh baalo ke liye behtarin kaam karta hain. \n\n\n\n• Ghar se badboo door  karne ke liye :- Agar aap kamre se aane wali badboo se preshan hain toh sirke ka istemal kare. Sirke mein mauzood  Acetic  Acid badboo ko shokh leta hain. Isliye  ise kamre mein chhidkne se badboo door ho jati hain. \n\n\n\n• Kapdo ko naya banaye  rakhe  :- Kapdo ko mulayam banaye rakhne ke liye kapdo ki dhulaai se pahle washing machine mein safed sirka daale. Isse saabun ke ansh hat jayenge aur  kapdo ki chamak bani rahegi. \n\n\n\n• Fungal Infection door karne ke liye :- Feet, Pair ke naakhoon aur roosi ki samsya mein sirka  dawaai ki tarah kaam karta hain. Iske liye infection wali jagah par sirka lagaye, fafoondi khatm ho jayegi. \n\n\n\n• Kapdo se daag hatane ke liye :- Kapdo se paseene ke kharaab  daago ko hatane ke liye sirka lagaye aur kuch der ke liye chhod de. Phir kapdo ko dhoye. Daag gayab ho jayenge. \n\n\n\n\n\n\nPosted by\n\n\nHinglishpedia\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nShare to:\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nNextNewer Post\nPreviousOlder Post\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPost a Comment\n\n\n\naprieztmkrdezign\n218168578325095\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nSubscribe to:\nPost Comments (Atom)\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nLoading...\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPopulars\nComments\nArchive\n\n\n\n\nFeature Post\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPopular\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nRukhe baalo ko Silky banane ke gharelu upay aur nuskhe (Tips for Dry Hair to make Silky)\nKya aap Rukhe baalo (Dry Hair) se preshan hain?
