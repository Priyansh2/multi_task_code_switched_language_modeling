Research mein bataya gya hain ki agar garbhvati mahilayein sea food ka sewan kare toh unke bacche ka IQ level sabse tez hoga. Saath hi sea food khane se budhape mein Alzheimerki problem nahi hogi.\n\n\n• Protein ka good source :- Protein ka high source Chicken aur Meat hote hain, Lekin machhali bhi kuch kam nahi hain. Yeh blood pressure,tavcha ki dekhbhaal aur aankho ki roshini badhati hain.\n\n\n• Pregnancy mein :- Pregnancy mein Omega-3 Fatty Acid ko bahut faydemandmana gya hain. Garbvati aurato ko pregnancy ke teesre mahine se paki huyi machhali ka sewan karna chahiye. Macchali khane se hone wale shishu ka dimaag tezhota hain. Lekin yeh accha hoga ki pahle aap apne doctor se salah le le ki kaun si machhali khani chahiye aur kitni matra mein khani chahiye.\n\n\n• Muscles ke liye:- Workout karne ke baad musclesko protein ki khas jaroorat hoti hain. Iske liye aap Fish try kar sakte hain. Yeh maanspeshiyo ko majboot karti hain aur unhejald se jald recover karne ka kaam karti hain.\n\n\n• Omega -3 Fatty Acid:- Sharir keliye Omega-3 Fatty Acidbahut jaroori hain, Kyonki yeh sharir par jald budhapa nahi aane deta hain. Veh log jo macchali khate hain, Unki skin hamesha jawan rahti hain aur glow karti hain.\n\n\n• Heart ke rog ke liye :- Sea food ko khane se dil ki bimariya honeka khatra kamho jata hain.
