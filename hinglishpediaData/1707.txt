Iske dwara Earth-quake ki trashdi mein fanse logo ki sthiti ke baare mein pata kiya ja sakta hain. Pahla "I am looking for someone" (main kisi ko dhoondh raha hoon), aur doosra "I have Information about someone" (Mujhe kisi ke baare mein suchna chahiye) . Agar koi vyakti is trashadi mein phansa hua hain aur aapko unki jankari chahiye to "I am looking for someone" me jakar us vyakti ka naam likh sakte hain. Isi tarah agar kisi ke parijan ki safety ke baare mein agar unhe suchana chahiye to " I have Information about someone" tool mein jakar apne parijan ka naam likhna padega. \n\n\n\n\n\nGaurtalab hain ki yeh tool English aur Nepali dono language mein available hain.
