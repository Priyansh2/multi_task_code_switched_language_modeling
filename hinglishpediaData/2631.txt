Ise khana sirf sehat ke liye nahi balki skin aur baalo ke liye bhi bahut faydemand hota hain. Low Calorie wali fish mein bahut sara protein maujood hota hain, jo Dimaag ke liye bhi accha hain. Iske saath hi polyunsaturated Omega 3 Fatty Acid maujood hote hain, jo healthy life ke liye faydemand hain. Hafte mein 1 baar Fish (Machhali) kha kar bhi active raha ja sakta hain. Salmon Fish ke sirf 2-3 tukdo mein hi 1.5 gram Fatty aicd maujood hota hain. Iske alawa aur bhi kai tarah ki fish mein pryapt matra mein protein aur Jaroori tatv paye jate hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n\n1. Pregnant Mahilaon ke liye :-  Kai Garbhavati mahilaon ki bahut sari problems aur andekhi ke chalte samay se pahle unki delivery karni padti hain, Iska sidha asar unke saath-saath bacche par bhi padta hain. To is samsya se door rahna hain to machhali khana shuru kar de. Kyonki aisa karke pr-mature birth ki sambhavnaye puri tarah se khatm ho jati hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n2. Dimaag ke liye faydemand :- Salmon aur aisi hi kai prakar ki fishes mein EFA Omega 3 maujood hota hain, jo dimaag ke liye bahut hi jaroori hain. Khas taur par chhote shishuon ke liye. 75% Brain Cells ka nirmaan janm ke samay hota hain aur 25% janm ke baad. Isliye doctor fish khane ki salah bachcho ke saath-saath pregnant ladies ko bhi dete hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n3. Alzheimer ka khatra kam karti hain :- Fish ko brain food bhi kaha jata hain, kyonki isme aise tatv maujood hote hain jo brain ke liye bahut hi faydemand hote hain. Hafte mein 1 baar baked aur bhuni hui fish khane se brain ke kaam karne ki kshamta badh jati hain. Isse yaddast sahi rahti hain aur Alzheimer bimari ki sambhavna ko ise kha kar kam kiya ja sakta hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n4. Dil ki bimari se bachati hain :- Fish mein maujood Omega 3 Fat ko kam kar deti hain, jisse dil ki bimariyo ka khatra kafi had tak kam ho jata hain. Research mein yeh baat saamne aayi hain ki 50% mahilaye dil ki bimari se preshan thi, Lekin jo Mahila hafte mein 1 hi din hi sahi, Machhali khaya karati thi, vo healthy rehti thi. Body ko 2 types ke fatty acids ki jaroorat hoti hain vo hain Omega-3 aur Omega 6. Isme Omega-3 hi hearts ki problems ko door rakhta hain. Yeh artis mein blood ke saath oxygen aur nutrients ke circulation ke liye bhi jimmevar hain.   \n\n\n\n\n\n5. Skin aur balon ke liye faydemand :-  Fish mein maujood protein ki bharpoor matra skin ke saath saath balo ke liye bahut faydemand hain. Fish mein sabse jyada Omega-3 ki maujoodagi hoti hain, aur yahi sabse kargar bhi hain. Sehat ke saath-saath balon ki chamak, unhe lamba, ghana, majboot banana aur skin ki glow ke liye yeh faydemand hain. Hafte mein kewal 1 baar iska sewan karke bahut saare fayde paye ja sakte hain aur skin ko kai tarah ke rogo  aur infection se bachaya ja sakta hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n6. Depression door karta hain : -  Bahut saare research  se yeh baat samne aayi hain ki dawaaiyo ke saath khane mein fish shamil karne se depression ki problem ko door kiya ja sakta hain. Bhaag-daur bhari zindagi, kaam ka pressure, pregnancy ke dauraan hone wale depression ko kam karne ke liye machhali khana ek behtar option hain. Omega-3 Fatty acid depression ko kam karne ke saath mood ko bhi sahi rakhta hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n7. Vitamin D ka Source: -  Haddiyon ke vikas aur majbooti ke liye vitamin D ki sabse jyada jaroorat hoti hain, jo kai prakar ki bimariyo ko door rakhne mein madad karta hain. Fish ke sirf 5-6 tukde body ke 75% Vitamin D ki matra ko pura karte hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n8. Purusho ke liye faydemand :-  Fruits, Vegetables , Sabut Anaaz ke saath machhali ka sewan purusho ke liye faydemand hota hain. Smoking, Drinking, Drugs ka sidha asar purusho ki fertility par padta hain, Lekin iska asar kam karne ke liye fish bahut hi faydemand hoti hain. Jitna jyada iska sewan karoge, utna hi fayda yeh pahuchayegi.\n\n\n\n9.  Aankhon ki roshini ke liye :-  Garbhvati mahilaye agar fish ka sewan pryapt matra mein karti hain, unke saath-saath unke bachhe ki aankhon ki roshini tej hoti hain, Unhe chashma lagane ki jaroorat nahi padti hain. Yaha bhi Omega-3 Jimmevar hain jo Retina ki majbooti ke saath kai prakar ki aankho ki bimariyo se bachata hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n10. Arthritis mein aaram :- Arthritis ke karan jodo mein hone wale dard aur sujan ko machhali kha kar kam kiya ja sakta hain.
