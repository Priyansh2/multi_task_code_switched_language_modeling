Diabetes mein labhdayak :- Bael mein laxative ka   level adhik hota hain. Yeh body mein blood sugar level ko control mein rakhta hain. Luh ki samsya ko khatm karta hain :- Looh lagne par Bael ke taaze patto ko pees kar mehndi ki tarah pair ke taluye par lagaye. Iske alawa sar, chhati, Haath aadi par iski malish kare. Mishri dalkar Bael ka sharbat bhi piye, Turant rahat milegi. \n\n\n\n\n\n4. Khoon ki kami ko door kare :-  Niyamit roop se Bael ka sharbat peene se Hemoglobin ki kami door hoti hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n5. Saans ke rogo mein faydemand :- Ayurveda mein Bael se nikalne wale tel ka istemal dama aur saans se jude rogo ke upchar mein kiya jata hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n6. Dil ke liye labhdayak :- Bael ke ras ko ghee ke saath milakar thodi matra mein niyamit roop se le. Iske niyamit sewan se dil se jude rogo se bacha ja sakta hain. Iske juice ke pryog se stroke ke khatre se bacha ja sakta hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n7. Infection se bachav :-  Bael mein kai tarah ke Anti- Microbial gun hote hain. Jo body ko infection se bachane mein madadgaar hote hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n8. Ulcer mein faydemand : -  Bael mein Phenolic tatv ke saath Anti-Oxidant mauzood hote hain. Yahi karan hain ki bael ka sharbat Gastric Ulcer ko theek karne mein madadgar hota hain. Itna hi nahi iske sewan se pet mein acid ka balance bhi bana rahta hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n9. Cholesterol par control :- Ayurveda mein iski madad se Cholesterol kam kiya jata hain.
