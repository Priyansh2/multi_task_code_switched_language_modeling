Abaise mein powerbank saath mein lekar chale ya phir kuch asaan tips apna kar battery ko jaldikhatm honese bachaye.Aaiye jantehain 10 aise upay aur tarike jisse aap apne smartphone kibattery ko jald khatm hone sebacha sakte hain.\n\n\n1) Turn down display brightness :- Yehtips smartphone aur Laptop dono users ke liye common hain. Jyadatar devices mein brightness ki setting ko asaani ke saathaccess kiya ja sakta hain. Yani ki aap chahe toh kisi bhi device ki brightness ko kam ya jyada karsaktahain. Agar battery ko bachana hain toh brightness ko thodakam hi rakhetoh behtarhain. Iseaap display settings mein jakar change karsaktehain. \n\n\n\n2) Don’t use vibrate :- Aksar dekhne ko milta hain kismartphone’s user ringtone ko mute karkephone vibratepar rakhtehain. Lekin hum aapko bata de ki phone ke vibratemodepar hone se battery ki jyada khapat hoti hain. \n\n\n\n3) Keep it cool :-Aaspaas ka mahaul battery par khasa asar daalta hain. Yani ki temperatureka battery power par seedhaasar padta hain. Agar aap apna Smartphoneaisijagahrakhtehain jaha ka temperature 35 degree ke upar ya 0 degree ke neeche hain, toh batteryki capacity kafi jaldi ghatne lagti hain. Aise mein smartphoneko direct sunlight se door rakhna chahiye. Iske saath hi bahut hi thandijagah se bhi smartphoneko bacha kar rakhna chahiye.Jyada garmi aur thandidono hi smartphone ki battery ki capacity ke liye haanikarak hain. \n\n\n\n4) Change your settings :- Aksar dekhajatahain ki users Smartphonepar live wallpaper ya 3d wallpaper laga kar rakhte hain, Iski wajah se battery jaldi dischargehoti hain. Aisemein jab battery kam ho jaye toh inki settings ko aap change kar sakte hain. Iske alawaBluetooth, GPS jaisi servicesko kuch samay ke liye off kiya ja sakta hain. \n\n\n\n5) Schedule app updates over Wi-Fi : - Agar aap kisi app ko updatekar rahehain tohusayWi-Fi ke jariye updatekare. Iske alawa Wi-Fi ko hamesha on na rakhe, jab iski jarooratho tabhi iska istemalkare.Batatechaleki Wi-Fi on rahne seyeh aas-paas keareamein signal ko dhoondtarahtahain, Jisse batteryjyadakharch hoti hain. Iske alawa agar aapke mobile mein strong signalaa raheho, toh 4g LTE service ko off karke 3Gpar kaam karsakte hain. Vahi backup meinchalne wali sabhi apps ko band karde. \n\n\n\n 6) Go forpaid appsinstead offree ones :- Ads supported apps aapke Smartphone battery lifeko lagbhag 2.5 ghantekhatm kar detehain. US based research kemutabik, Phone Processor dimaag ki tarah hotahain. Aisemein Adssupported appsbrain powerkam kar dete hain. Jiska seedha asarbattery life par padta hain. Aise meinjahatak possible ho toh free apps se bache. Iskeliyepaid apps sabse behtarin option hain. Halaki researchesyeh manana hain ki sabhi free appbatterypar buraasarnahi daltehain. Lekin jis app meinadschalte hainvehbattery ko bahut jaldi dischargekar dete hain. \n\n\n\n7) Keep the screen timeout short :- Smartphone ki display jitni kam chalegi, Battery utni kam kharch hogi. Iske liye aapko display settings par jana hoga. Jaha par aapko ‘screen timeout’ ka option dikhaai dega.Is option se yeh nishchit kiya jata hain ki phone screen par koi bhi app use karneke baad uski screenkitani der takjalegi. \n\n\n\n8) Turn Off location tracking :- Ek newsreport ke mutabik, Iphone users agar facebook app ko accesskarte hain, toh unke phone ki batteryjaldi dischargeho jatihain. Kyonki yeh GPSmodule ke chalteuser ki location ko lagatar track karta rahtahain. Aise mein agar location trackko off kar diya jayetoh battery drain ki problem se niptaja saktahain. Agar aap Androiduser hain toh Settings > Location mein jakar location tracking ko disable kar sakte hain. Vahi iOS user ke liye Settings > Privacy > Location services option mauzoodhain. \n\n\n\n 9) Turn Off push notifications :- Agar aapko lagta hain ki battery 30% ke aas-paasbachi hain toh sabhi push notificationsko offkarde.Jaise ki Facebook ya kai messenger apps aadike pop-ups on rakhne par yeh energy ko concede karte hain. Aapke phone meinjitni baar pop-up notificationaayega, vehkuch na kuch battery jaroor khayega. Aise mein agar aap kanhi travel kar rahe ho aur batterylagbhag khatam hone wali ho toh sabhi notifications ko off karde. \n\n\n\n10)Particular dischargeVS Full discharge : - Aksar aapne suna hoga ki phone ko tabhi charge par lagana chahiye jab vehpuri tarah se discharge ho chuka ho. Yani ki 0-5 percent ke beech meinaane par hi battery ko chargekarna chahiye. Halaki ki yeh bilkul hi galat fact hain. Expertskahte hain kibattery ke30 se 40 percent tak aatehi phone ko chargekarlena chahiye. Isse batterylife badh jati hain. Kyonki mana jatahain kiparticular discharge full dischargecycle se behtarhain.
