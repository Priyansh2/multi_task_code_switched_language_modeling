Fat Tummy ki problem aaj common ho gyi hain. Pet par Fat ke jama hone se pet ka motapa jyada badh gya hain. Lekin diet mein kuch changes karke aap fat tummy se chhutkara pa sakte hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nBadhe huye Pet (Fat tummy) ko kam karne ke tarike :- \n\n\n\n1.  Alsi (Flex Seeds) \n\n\n\nAlsi mein good quantity mein Omega-3 Fatty Acid aur Iron hota hain. Daily 1 Spoon Alsi khane se pet kam hone lagta hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n2. Saunf wala paani \n\n\n\n\n\nRegular Ek cup paani mein ek chammach Saunf ubaal kar pijiye. Isse digestion sudharta hain aur pet ka fat kam hone lagta hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n3. Badam \n\n\n\n\n\nRojana 4 se 5 Badam khaye. Isme Vitamin E, Polyunsaturated aur Monounsaturated Fat bhookh ko control karte hain. Isse pet ka motapa kam karne mein help milti hain. \n\n\n\n4. Kalonji \n\n\n\n\n\nEk glass paani mein kalonji tel ki kuch boonde aur shahad mila kar pijiye. Aisa aap din mein 2 baar kare. Isse pet ka fat kam hone lagta hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n5.  Dahi \n\n\n\n\n\nRojana 1 kaotori Dahi khani chahiye. Isme Calcium hota hain jo tummy fat ko badhaane wale cortisol hormone level ko control kar pet ko ghataane ka kaam karta hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n6.  Chana aur Jau \n\n\n\n\n\nGenhu ki roti ko khana kam kar de. Iski jagah par aap Chana aur Jau ke aate se bani roti ko khaye. Isme Calorie aur Carbohydrates bahut hi low quantity mein hote hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n7. Nariyal Paani \n\n\n\n\n\nNariyal paani mein Electrolytes paye jate hain. Yeh motapa aur pet ke fat ko kam karta hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n8. Gunguna paani \n\n\n\n\n\nDin bhar mein kam se kam 3 ya 4 baar ek glass gunguna paani jaroor piye. Isse pet ka fat tezi ke sath burn hone lagta hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n9.  Honey \n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nRegular ek glass hot water mein 1 chammach Shahad mila kar piye. Isse fat tezi ke sath kam hone lagta hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n10.  Green tea \n\n\n\n\n\nGreen tea mein Thiamine naam ka amino acid hota hain. Yeh bhookh ko control karta hain. Jisse Stomach Fat kam hone lagta hain. \n\n\n\n\n\nDaily routine mein yeh aadatein ajmaye :- \n\n\n\n• Khana khate samay paani na piye \n\n\n\n\n\nKhana khate waqt paani nahi peena chahiye. Isse khana sahi tarah se digest nahi ho pata hain aur pet ka fat badhne lagta hain. Khane ke 10 ya 15 minutes baad hi paani peena chahiye. \n\n\n\n\n\n• Morning Walk jaroor kare \n\n\n\nRegular 2 se 3 Kilometer Morning walk par jaye. Isse kafi matra mein calorie burn hoti hain aur fat kam karne mein madad milti hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n • Tummy Exercise \n\n\n\n Peeth ke bal let jaye aur dono pairo ko upar uthaye. Phir dono pair ek saath ghunto se mode. 5 seconds tak haatho se pairon ko jakad kar rakhe. Pairon ko waapis seedha kare. 8 se 10 baar ise dohraye. \n\n\n\n\n\n• Breakfast \n\n\n\nSubah ke nashte mein Protein wali diet de. Isme Anda, doodh  aur Kela jaisi healthy cheeze khaye. Isse din mein baar-baar bhookh lagne ki problem kam hoti hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n• Ek saath khana na khaye \n\n\n\nKhane ko ek saath na kha kar 2 se 3 ghante ke gap mein khaye. Isse tummy ka fat kam hone lagta hain. \n\n\n\n\nPet badh gya hain to follow kare yeh tips. \nKharab lifestyle ki wajah se in dino Pet ka motapa kafi jyada badh raha hain. Kyonki raat ko mobile use karne se health par bahut hi bad effect padta hain. \n\n\n\nAaj ke time mein Smartphone life ka important part ban chuke hain. Agar koi aadmi mobile nahi istemal karta hain to usay bekaar aur old fashion wala maana jata hain. Lekin jaroorat se jyada mobile ka istemal bhi sehat ko nuksan pahuchata hain. Kai sari studies mein paya gya ki mobile health ko nuskan pahuchate hain. \n\n\n\n\n\nRaat ko sone se pahle mobile kyon nahi use karna chahiye? \n\n\n\n1. Neend ki kami hona \n\n\n\n\n\nRaat ko sone se pahle mobile ka istemal karne par body mein melatonin hormone ka level kam hone lagta hain. Jisse aapko neend der se aati hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n2. Eyes sight par bad effect \n\n\n\n\n\nRaat ke time sone se pahle mobile use karne par eyes ki retina par bad effect padta hain. Jisse aankhon ki roshni kam hone lagti hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n3. Stress mein badhotri \n\n\n\n\n\nDer raat tak mobile ke istemal se Melatonin Hormone level kam hone lagta hain, jiski wajah se aapka stress badh jata hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n4. Brain par bura asar \n\n\n\n\n\nDer raat tak mobile ke use se brain tumor hone ka khatra kafi jyada badh jata hain. Isse aapki brain memory kamjor ho sakti hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n5. Thakaan hona \n\n\n\n\n\nRaat ko jyada mobile chalaane se neend puri nahi ho pati hain aur agle din aapko thakaan mahsoos hota hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n6. Glaucoma \n\n\n\n\n\nLate night tak mobile use karne se brain tak signal le jani wali optic nervs par bura asar padta hain, Isse aapko Glaucoma (Kaala Motiya) ki problem ho sakti hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n7. Focusing muscles par effect \n\n\n\n\n\nRaat ko der tak mobile istemal karne se aankhon ki focusing muscles par bura effect padta hain. Isse kisi bhi cheez par focus karne mein kafi hi muskhil aati hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n8. Aankhon mein redness hona \n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nLate night tak Mobile ya Tablet use karne se Eyes mein redness ki problem paida ho sakti hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n9. Dark circles hona \n\n\n\n\n\nDer raat tak mobile use karne se eyes par stress padta hai. Isse aapko dark circles ki problem ho sakti hain. \n\n\n\n\nSone se pahle Mobile Use kyon nahi karna chahiye? \nKya aap bhi raat ko sone se pahle mobile ka istemal karte hain? to aapko yeh article jaroor padhna chahiye.
