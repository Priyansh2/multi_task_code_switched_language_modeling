Iske baad jo bhi member baat karega, veh group ke sabhi logo ko sunayi degi. Halaki yeh baat dhyaan rahe, jo member call ko disconnect kar dega veh calling ke dauran khud hi bahar ho jayega. \n\n \n\n \n\n\n200 million users hain target :- \n\n\n\nHike messenger ke founder aur CEO Kevin Bharti Mittal ka kahna hain ki, Kai Hike users group calling ko lekar complain kiya karte thay. Philhaal Hike mein yeh naya free calling feature company ki marketing ka ek hissa hain. Company ka target 200 million Hike users banana hain.
