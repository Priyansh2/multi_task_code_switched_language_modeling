Highgean veh point hain jiske liye diaper ho ya gharelu napkin, dono ke istemal ke  samay alertness rakhna jaroori  hain. Kuch diaper  ethylbenzene, toluene aur xylene jaise Volatile Organic Compounds yani environment mein bhaap  ban kar pahuchane  wale  organic compound ko  release karte  hain.
