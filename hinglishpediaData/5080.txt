Home remedies for remove pimples problem in Hindi/Urdu. \n\n\n\n• Honey :- Shahad mein kaccha doodh mila kar pest banaye aur ise chehre par lagaye. Yeh chehre ke pimples aur scars remove karne mein help karta hain. Iske alawa Honey & milk dono hi face ko moisturize karke smooth bhi banate hain. Isse aapka chehra chamakne lagta hain aur chehre ka glow bhi badh jata hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n• Masoor ki daal :- Masoor ki daal ke face pack pimples remove karne ka best tarika mana jata hain. Aap is face pack ko 2 tariko se bana sakte hain. Pahle tarike mein aap Masoor ki dal ko raat bhar ke liye bhigo de aur subah ise dahi ke sath peeskar chehre par lagaye. Dusra tarika yeh hain ki aap masoor ki dal ko bina bhigoye huye hi baarik peeskar powder bana le aur ise dahi ke saath mila kar chehre par lagaye. Yeh skin ko glowing karne mein madad karta hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n• Pyaaz ka ras :- Pyaaz ka ras bahut hi useful remedy hain, Ise Olive oil ke saath mix karke face par lagane se daag-dhabbe khatm ho jate hain. Ise aap hafte mein 2 baar apne chehre par laga sakte hain. Lekin ise lagane se aapke chehre par thodi se jalan mahsoos hoti hain, Agar aapke chehre par jalan jyada ho rahi hain to turant chehre ko paani se dho le aur agli baar se is nuskhe ko na ajmaye. \n\n\n\n\n\n• Papita :- Papita na sirf health ke liye faydemand hota hain, balki chehre se pimples mitaane mein bhi madad karta hain. Papite ko shahad ke saath mash karke chehre par lagaye. Is pest ko 15 minutes ke liye face par laga rahne de. Iske baad chehre ko gungune paani se dho le. Isse aapko pimples ki problems se nijaat to milta hi hain, saath hi aapki skin soft & shining bhi ban jati hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n• Kheera :- Kheere ko peeskar iska paste bana le aur ise chehre par lagaye. Isse face ke daag-dhabbe door ho jayenge. Iske liye Kheere ko kaatkar isey aap mixture mein pees le. Agar aapko yeh thoda massy lage to peese huye kheere ko nichodkar iska ras nikaalkar bhi aap ise chehre par laga sakte hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n• Multani Mitti :- Pimples se chhutkara dilaane mein Multani mitti sabse accha upay hain. Multani mitti mein Gulabjal aur Nimbu ka ras mix karke chehre par lagaye. Isse pimples jald se jald khatm hone lagte hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n• Nimbu ka ras :- Nimbu ka ras face ke liye kisi beauty products se kam nahi hain. Nimbu ke ras mein Honey mix karke face par lagaye, isse pimples jaldi theek hone lagte hain. Iske alawa is paste ko lagane se aapki skin shine hone lagti hain. Aap chahe to Nimbu ke chhilke sookha kar pees le aur iska face pack bana kar use kar sakte hain. Face par Nimbu ka ras aur Shahad ko ek saath lagane se chehre par halki se jalan bhi mahsoos ho sakti hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n• Pudina :- Pudine ke pattiyon ko pees kar usme shahad mix kare. Is paste ko chehre par lagaye aur sookhne ke baad gungune paani se chehre ko dho le. Isse na sirf chehre ko thandak milti hain, balki pimples bhi door hote hain aur rang bhi nikharta hain. Yeh face pack chehre ke liye best mana jata hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n• Aaloo ka ras :- Aaloo ke ras mein Kheera ka ras mix kare aur cotton ki help se ise chehre par lagaye. Ise 15 minutes tak face par laga rahne de, phir gungune paani se chehre ko dho le. Isse bhi moohanso se chhutkara milta hain. Iske alawa isse chehre ki rangat nikhaarne mein bhi help milti hain. Aap chahe to is ras ko fridge mein store karke bhi rakh sakte hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n• Haldi :- Haldi mein Gulabjal mila kar pest banaye aur ise face par apply kare. Isse pimples jaldi se theek hone lagte hain. Kyonki haldi mein antioxidants paye jate hain jo pimples ko theek karta hain.
