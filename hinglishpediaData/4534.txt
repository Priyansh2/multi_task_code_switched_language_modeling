Iske beejo mein Iron, Potassium, Minerals aur Vitamin paye jate hain. Tarbooz ke beej ko chaba kar khaye ya phir iske tel ka istemal kare. Dono hi roop mein yeh faydemand hain. Tarbooz ke beej khane se skin healthy ho jati hain. Baal chamakdaar aur ghane hote hain aur sehat ban jati hain. \n\n2. Tarbooz mein mauzood Magnesium Dil ke liye bahut hi accha hota hain. Yeh karyapranali mein sudhaar lata hain aur metabolism system ko support karta hain. Iske alawa yeh Cardiovascular rogo aur Hypertension mein bhi faydemand hain. \n\n3. Agar aap diabetes ke patient hain aur aapki sugar hamesha ghatati-badhti rahti hain toh tarbooz ke beej ko thode se paani mein ubaale. Is paani ko rojana chai ki tarah piye. Isse blood sugar control mein rahta hain. \n\n4. Tarbooz ke beej ke tel se chehre par massage karne se chehra chamakne lagta hain. Yeh chehre se gandagi aur sebum ko hata kar pores ko khol deta hain aur skin ko beautiful banata hain. \n\n\n5. Tarbooz mein un-saturated fatty acid hota hain, jo tavcha mein nami ko barkarar rakhta hain. Isme mauzood Anti-Oxidants tatv jhurriyo ko door karta hain. \n\n6. Tarbooz aur uske beejo ki giri sharir ko pusht banati hain. Giri ki thandai bana kar rojana subah niyamit roop se peene se smaran shakti badhti hain. Tarbooz ke beej khane se high blood pressure bhi control hota hain. \n\n7. Skin infection mein bhi Tarbooz ek behatarin dawa ki tarah kaam karta hain. Agar aapko acne (Pimples) ki problem ho toh Tarbooz ke beej ka tel chehre par lagaye. Yeh samsya khatm ho jayegi. \n\n8. Protein aur Jaroori matra mein Amino Acid hone ki wajah se iske beej baalo ki problems mein ramban hain. Tarbooz ke beej ko chaba kar khane se baal jad se majboot hote hain. \n\n9. Purane seerdard ko door karne ke liye tarbooz ke beejo ki giri ko paani mein pees kar lep taiyaar kare aur seer par lagaye. Aaram milega. \n\n10. Tarbooz mein Lycopene paya jata hain, Lycopene hamari skin ko hamesha jawan banaye rakhta hain. Yeh hamare sharir ko cancer se bhi bachata hain. \n\n11. Garmiyo mein tarbooj ka sewan karne se dil ki bimariya nahi hoti hain. \n\n12. Tarbooz hamare sharir ki prati-raksha pranali ko bhi majboot karta hain. \n\n13.
