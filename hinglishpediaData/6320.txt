Subah Shaam acchi tarah se brush karna chahiye. \n\n\n\n2. Neem ya babool ki naram daali ka brush bana kar daant saaf karne se moonh ki durgandh (Badboo) door hoti hain. \n\n\n\n3. Elaichi aur Pudine ka bana Paan chabane se mooh ki badboo door hoti hain. \n\n\n\n4. Elaichi chabane se mooh se badboo nahi aati hain. \n\n\n\n5. Dalchini bhi mooh ki durgandh (Badboo ya smell) ko door karne mein madadgar hoti hain.\n\n\n\n6. Ek cup paani mein Jeere ke tel ki 2-3 boonde dal kar garare karne se labh hota hain. \n\n\n\n7. Din bhar mein kam se kam 8 se 10 glass paani piye. \n\n\n\n8. Anaar ki chhal ko paani mein ubaal kar isse kulla kare. Labh hoga. \n\n\n\n9. Sukha dhaniya chabane se bhi mooh ki badboo se chhutkara paya ja sakta hain. \n\n\n\n10. Din mein 1 baar sarso ke tel mein thoda Namak mila kar daanto aur masoodho ki malish kare. Dhyaan rahe lar thookte rahe. Isse mooh ekdum saaf ho jata hain.\n\n\n11. Tulsi ki chaar pattiya roj chabakar kha kar upar se paani peene se mooh ki badboo door ho jati hain.\n\n\n12. Ek Laung mooh mein rakhkar choosne se bhi labh hota hain.\n\n13. Bhojan ke baad dono samay aadha chammach Saunf chabane se kuch hi dino mein mooh ki badboo khatm ho jati hain aur hajma bhi sahi rahta hain.\n\n14. Subah Neem ya babool ka datun sabse behtar hain.
