Aam taur par log bimar hone par soup ka jyada sewan karte hain, lekin soup ko niyamit roop mein apni diet mein shamil karne se kai labh hote hain. Soup peene se pet jaldi bhar jata hain aur bharipan bhi nahi hota hain. \n \n\n• Pachne mein asaan :- Soup ka sewan bimariyo mein isliye bhi kiya jata hain, kyonki yeh asaani se pach jata hain aur kisi tarah ki preshani paida nahi karta hain. Isse bimarike baadsust pada pachan tantra bhi sahi tarike se kaam karne lagta hain.\n\n • Mucus ko patla kare :- Kamjori hone par mucus mota ho jata hain, jiske karan bacteria aur virus ka khatra badh jata hain. Soup ka niyamit upyog karne se mucus patla ho jata hain, jisseinfection nahi hota hain. \n \n\n• Kamjori karedoor :- Sharir mein kamjori mahsoos hone par soup ka sewan karna bahut hi faydemandhota hain. Iske alawatabiyat kharab hone parsoup ka sewan sekisi bhi tarah kipreshanibhi nahi hoti hain.\n\n • Taste badhaaye :- Agar aapke mooh ka swaadbadal raha hain aur aapko har cheez beswaadlagne lagi hain toh phir soup pijiye. Isse aapko apna swaad vaapas lane mein madadmilegi, kyonki yeh swaadbadhaane mein madadkarta hain. \n \n\n• Paushtik :- Soup koi bhi ho , paushtik tatvo se bharpoor hota hain. Darasal jis sabzi ya khadya padarth ka soup banta hain, uska pura satv soup mein hota hain. Iske alawa kaitatvo se bharpoorsoup androoni takat dene wala hota hain. \n \n\n• Hydration :- Jab aap bimarhote hainya fever se sharir dehydrate ho jati hain. Isliye aisewaqt mein body ko hydrate rakhne ke liyesoup pijiye. Isse sharir mein paani ki matra aur poshak tatv daakhil ho jatehain. \n \n\n• Energyke liye ;- Sharirik kamjori mein soup ka sewan aapko energy deta hain aur aap pahle sehealthy feel karte hain.
