Amrood ko tamam guno ka khazana mana jata hain. Isme Folate ki acchi matra hain, Jisse yeh mahilaon ki fertility ko badhata hain. Maa banane ki chahat rakhne wali mahilaon ko amrood ko jaroor khana chahiye.\n\n\n\nIsse sharir ke hormone ka balance bana rahta hain. Amrood mein santre ke mukable 4 guna jyada Vitamin C hota hain. Amrood mein mauzood Potassium sharir mein sodium ke asar ko kam karta hain, Jisse blood pressure ka balance sahi bana rahta hain. \n\n\n\nIsme paya jane wala Vitamin C aur A health ke liye bahut hi faydemand hain. Pregnancy ke dauran Amrood khane se bahut jyada fayde hote hain, Aaiye jante hain Pregnant woman ko Amrood khane se kya kya fayde hote hain.\n\n1) Blood pressure balance rakhta hain :- Garbhavati mahilaon mein high blood pressure kafi khatarnaak saabit ho sakta hain. Isliye garbhvati mahilaon ko roz ek paka hua amrood khana chahiye, isse blood pressure control mein rahta hain.\n\n2) Kabz se rahat milti hain :- Amrood mein bharpoor matra mein fiber paya jata hain, Jisse anto par dabaav banta hain aur mal tyagne mein asaani hoti hain. Roj ek Amrood khane se kabz theek rahta hain.\n\n3) Cancer se bachata hain :- Garbhavati mahilaon mein breast cancer hone ka khatra rahta hain. Isliye inhe aise fruits khane chahiye, Jinme Antioxidants ho, jaise Amrood. Amrood mein Lycopine aur Vitamin C hota hain jo zehreele tatvo ko sharir mein jama nahi hone deta hain.\n\n4) Immunity ko majboot karta hain :- Garbhvati mahilaon mein rogo se ladne ki shakti honi chahiye. Amrood mein Vitamin C bharpoor matra mein paya jata hain, Jo kai tarah ke rogo se ladne mein madad karta hain.\n\n5) Mann ko shant rakhta hain :- Tanaav pregnancy ke dauraan sharir mein cortisole banane lagta hain, Jisse garbhashya ko nuksaan pahuch sakta hain. Isliye roj amrood khane se mann shant rahta hain aur tension se mukti milti hain.\n\n6) Jyada Poshak tatv le :- Garbhvati mahila ko aur uske hone wale bacche wajah se jyada poshak tatvo ki jaroorat padti hain, Isliye usay jyada se jyada poshak tatvo wala aahaar lena chahiye. Is dauran amrood khane se poshak tatvo ki kami puri hoti hain.\n\n7) Nervous System  ;- Shishu ke mashtik aur tantrika tantar ke vikas ke liye folic acid bahut hi jaroori hain. Amrood mein folic acid paya jata hain jo pregnancy ke dauran khane se bacche ke liye bahut hi jyada faydemand hota hain.\n\n8) Aankho ki roshini ko badhata hain :- Amrood mein Vitamin A paya jata hain jo Maa aur bacche dono ki aankho ki roshini ko badhata hain.\n\n9) Pachan mein faydemand :- Garbhavati mahilaon ko aksar pet ki kharabi aur acidity se preshan dekha gya hain. Isliye pregnancy ke shuruvat mein Amrood khaye, isse pachan ki prakriya acchi hogi aur acidity se bhi aaram milega.\n\n10) Blood sugar level :- Garbhavshta ke dauran Diabetes khatranaak sabit ho sakti hain, Yeh Pregnancy ya delivery ke time preshani paida kar sakti hain.
