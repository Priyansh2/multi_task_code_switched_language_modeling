Is camera par aapko milega Company ka Exmor R censor, LED flash aur 25mm Wide-angle lens ke saath.\n\n\n Battery aur storage bhi hain jabardast \n\nIs Xperia C4 dual mein par aapko milega 16 GB ki inbuilt storage. Company ki aor se is battery mein 12 hours 47 minutes ka talk time dene ka dawa kiya gya hain.
