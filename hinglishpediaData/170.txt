Isse aankho par screen kam se kam chamakti hain. Saath hi Television ka screenjitnabada hoga, veh aankho ke liye utna hi behtar hoga. Isse aankho ko choti screen par focusnahi karna padta hain aur aankho par zor kam padta hain. \n\n \n\n\n• Breaklete rahe:- Lagatar TV se chipke rahneki bajaye thodi-thodi der mein usse break lete rahe. Isse aankho par dabaav kam padega.T.V. dekhneke dauranaankhein jhapkaaterahe aur jab bhi ads break aaye toh kuch derke liye tahalaaye. Isse na kewalaankho ko aaram milega, balki back ki problem serahatmilegi. Har din 8 hours ki neend jaroor le taki aankho ko aaram mil sake. \n\n \n\n\n• Lightrakhe ON :- T.V. dekhna har kisi ko accha lagta hain, Chahe bade ho ya bacche. Kai baar hum Bed par baithe-baithe, sote-sote bhi T.V. dekhte hain, jo ki aankho par bura asardaalta hain. Jabbhi T.V. dekh raheho toh kamre ki light chaaloo rakhe, Isse aapki aankhein nahi thakengi. \n\n \n\n\n• Screen se doori banaye rakhe :- T.V. dekhne ke dauran is baat ka khyaal rakhe ki veh aapki aankho se 8 se 10 feet ki doori par ho. Veh sahi uchaai par ho, Chahe veh aankho ke level ki baatki jaye toh T.V. ki uchaai aankho ke neeche ya aankho ke uparnahi honi chahiye. Uski screen ko dekhne ke liye aapki aankho ko upar-neeche karne ki jaroorat nahi honi chahiye.
