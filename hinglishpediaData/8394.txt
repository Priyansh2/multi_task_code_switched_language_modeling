Yeh level  naakhoon kaatne  ki badhit aadto ke saath  badhta jata  hain. Agar  aap khud ko nakhoon chabane se rokte  hain toh  tension feel hoti hain, Nakhoon kaatne  ka negative  asar life ki  quality par bhi padta hain. \n\n\n\nDaant kharab ho jate hain :- Jab hum mooh band karte hain toh upar se neeche daant iss kataar  mein hote hain  ki khaane ko sahi tarah  se chaba sake. Lekin jin logo ko nakhoon chabaane ki aadat hoti hain, unke daant apne sahi position se hut jate hain aur samay se pahle  unke  girne  ka khatra  rahta hain. \n\n\n\nDaanto se nakhoon chabaane  ke time  ko pahchane  :- Nail biting ke triggers ko pahchane , Jaise boring, television dekhte huye nakhoon chabana aadi. Is dauran  apne  haatho ko band ya electric  tape  se  baandh kar  rakhe.  \n\n\n\nNakhoono ka infection :- Lagatar  nakhoon chabaane se uske aas-paas infection phail jata hain. Jab aap nakhoono ko chabaate  hain toh bacteria, yeast aur dusre virus, toote-phoote  nakhoono ke jariye jism mein daakhil ho jate hain, Jisse nakhoono ke aas-paas lalima, sujan aur puss ho jate hain. Is takhleefdeh situation ko  surgery ke jariye  theek kiya jata hain. \n\n\n\nDaanto se nakhoon chabaane  ya kaatne  se  rokne  ke  liye  apnaye  yeh  upay ;- \n\n\n\n • Ungliyo par  kuch aise  tatv daale  jo aapki jeebh ke liye acche na ho jaise vinegar, hot sauce ya dusre  kuch khatte  swaad wali cheeze. \n\n\n\n• Nail biting ki aadat ko door karne ke  liye  behavioral therapy ka bhi sahara le  sakte hain.\n\n\n\n• Nakhoono ko hamesha chhote rakhe  ya manicure  karwaye.  \n\n\n\n• Apne  haatho  ko kuch activities mein busy rakhne ki koshish kare  jaise seelai, bunaai aur  bhi kai kaam jo haatho se kiye jate ho. \n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPosted by\n\n\nHinglishpedia\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nShare to:\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nNextNewer Post\nPreviousOlder Post\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPost a Comment\n\n\n\naprieztmkrdezign\n218168578325095\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nSubscribe to:\nPost Comments (Atom)\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nLoading...\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPopulars\nComments\nArchive\n\n\n\n\nFeature Post\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPopular\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nRukhe baalo ko Silky banane ke gharelu upay aur nuskhe (Tips for Dry Hair to make Silky)\nKya aap Rukhe baalo (Dry Hair) se preshan hain?
