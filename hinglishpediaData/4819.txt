Isse aap pairo mein jyada paseena aane ki shikayat ko khatm kar sakte hain. \n\n\n\n• Garmiyo mein Shoes ki jagah chappal pahle :- Hamesha hi Cotton Juraab pahne aur apne mozo ko har dusre din baar dhoye. Pairo ko paseene se bachaane ke liye aap Shoes ki jagah Sandel ya chappal pahne. \n\n\n\n• Fitkari ka istemal :- Pairo ko acchi tarah se saaf kare. Jab bhi baahar se ghar par aaye toh pairo ko kisi acche Antibacterial saabun se dhoye. Isse pair acchi tarah se saaf ho jate hain aur paseena bhi door ho jata hain. Phir is par Fitkari ka baarik peesa hua powder daalkar shoes ko pahan le. \n\n\n\n• Namak ke paani se pairo ko dhoye :- Pair mein infection na ho iske liye pairo ko saaf rakhna bahut hi jaroori hain. Paseene ke jariye bacteria paida hote hain, Jisse skin aur foot infection hone ka khatra badh jata hain. Ek tub mein gungune paani mein 5-7 chammach namak daal kar pairo ko aadhe ghante tak dubo kar rakhe. Phir pairo ko namak wale paani se bahar nikaalneke baad turant na pochhe, balki inhe sookhne de. Phir moze pahne. Namak wala paani skin ko dry banata hain aur paseena aane se rokne mein madad karta hain. \n\n\n\n• Iontophoresis Treatment :- Iontophoresis treatment ek aisi new technology hain, Jisme tub mein paani bhar kar ek machineke jariye charge karke paani ke positive aur negative ions ko activate kar diya jata hain. Phirusme kuch time ke liye haath-pairo ko duboya jata hain, jisse sweat gland ki activity kam ho jati hain. \n\n\n\n• Pumice stone ka istemal :- Paanv ki hard skin ko saaf kare. Hard ho chuki skin, dead skin hoti hain jise ragad ka nikaal dena chahiye. Jyada paseena aane se dead skin mein bacteria panap sakte hain, Isliye har din nahate waqt apne pairo ko ragadna chahiye. Pumice stone ka istemal karke aap apne aidiyo aur paanv ko clean kar sakte hain. \n\n\n\n• Surgical Spirit ka istemal :- Cotton ball le kar usay surgical spirit mein duboye aur phir ise pairo ko pocchiye.
