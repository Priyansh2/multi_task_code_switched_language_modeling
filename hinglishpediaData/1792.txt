Lekin\nticket par likhi har baat ka matlab aapko pata hona  bahut jaroori hain. Rail ticket par likhi code\nbhasha ka matlab janiye. \n\n\n\nKitni tarah ke reservation \n\n\n\nYeh janana interesting hain ki Indian\nrailway mein reservation kitni tarah ka hota \nhain. Is samay aap Indian railway mein 3 tarah ke reservation pa sakte\nhain.\n\n\n\n-        \nTicket\nwindow par jakar traditional tarike se reservation\n\n-        \nOnline\nE-reservation \n\n-        \nOnline\nI-Reservation\n\n\n\nE aur I Ticket\n\n\n\nAajkal\nE-ticket yani ki electronic ticket ka zamana hain. Har koi ticket reservation\nke liye computer ka istemal kar raha hain. Yaha reservation ke 2 option hain.\nE-Reservation aur I-Reservation. Bhale hi dono reservation ghar baithe  computer se karwaye jate ho, Lekin inme kuch\nantar bhi hain. \n\n\n\n-        \nAgar  aap physical ticket (Original print ticket)\nchahte hain aur ticket window par jane se bhi bachna chahte  hain toh aapko I-ticket lena chahiye. Yeh\nticket Indian Railway post office ke jariye aapke address  par bhejti hain. Kahne ka matlab yeh hain ki\nI-Ticket ko aap online reserve karwa sakte \nhain aur jaisa ki normal reservation ticket jo ki reservation window par\nmilti hain, Vahi ticket aapko milegi, lekin yeh aapke ghar ke address  par post \nke jariye aayegi. \n\n\n\n-        \nI-Ticket\ndiye gaye  pate par post ke jariye\npahuchta hain. I-ticket ke  liye\nyatra  ke \n3 din pahle booking karwani padegi, jabki E-Ticket yatra ke kuch ghanto\npahle bhi book ki ja sakti hain. \n\n\n\n-        \nI-Ticket\nke liye aapko daak kharch bhi bharna padega, Jabki E-ticket mein aapka kaam\nprint out ya sms ke jariye  bhi chal\njata  hain. \n\n\n\n-      \nE-Ticket\nke aap kai saare print nikaal sakte \nhain. I-ticket lene ke liye aapko phir se  dobara \ncharge dena padega. Dobara I-Ticket pane ke liye aapko apna PNR\nnumber  bhi yaad hona chahiye.\n\n\n\nPassenger  ki\npahchaan PNR No.\n\n\n\nTicket window\nse liye gye  aam reservation ticket ke\nleft side  ke  upar ki aor yeh number  hota \nhain. Yeh  number  unique \nhota  hain aur ise passenger name\nrecord  kaha jata hain. Yeh ticket aapka\nhain aur aap hi safar  kar  rahe hain iska pata  is pnr no. se hi chalta hain. Rail ka ticket  yunki non-transferable hota hain isliye aapke\nticket par koi aur safar karne ka adhikari \nnahi hain. Is lihaaz se  yeh  number aapke aur railway dono ke liye bahut\nhi important hain. Internet ya phone , kisi bhi madhyam se apne ticket  par yatra \nki jankari  aapke PNR no. ke\njariye hi milegi. Accha hoga ki safar ke \nshuruvaat se pahle PNR  number ko\nkanhi likh le ya mobile mein save karle. Kyonki ticket kho jane ki haalat mein\nyeh  aapki madad karega.  PNR number ki madad  se aap train number, train ka naam,\ndestination ki jankaari, safar ka class, ticket ka status, passenger coach aur\nseat ka number pata kar sakte hain. \n\n\n\nRoute  ki\njanam-kundali hain ‘train number’\n\n\n\nReservation\nticket par jo dusra  important number\nnazar aata hain veh  hain train number .\nAisa nahi hain ki yeh  number  bas \naise hi likh diya gya hain. Train number \n5 digit ka hota hain aur iska har number kuch na kuch kahta hain. Pahla\nnumer  yeh  batata \nhain ki aap jis train mein safar \nkar rahe  hain, veh kis tarah ki\npassenger  train hain. Aapko thoda  idea \ndete  hain – Agar aapke  reservation ticket par  likhe train number ki pahli digit  0 hain toh aap summer special, holiday\nspecial ya anya  special  train mein safar karne  wale hain. Agar pahli digit 1 ya 2 hain toh\nlambi doori ki train hain, jisme rajdhani aur shatabdi jaisi trains  shamil hain. 3 Number se  shuru hone wale train number Kolkata  ki local train ka hota  hain. 4 number se shuru hone wala train\nnumber Delhi, Chennai, Secundrabad jaise \nmetro city ki rail services  ka\nhota  hain. 5 number  traditional tarike ki train ka hota  hain. 6 number se shuru hone  wali train memu hoti hain aur 7 number  se shuru hone wali demu. Haal hi mein\nreserve  ki gyi train ka number 8 hota\nhain aur 9 number se  shuru hone wali\ntrain Mumbai ki local hoti hain. Isi tarah aage \nke  chaar digit  bhi alag-alag tarike se  train ke bare mein puri jankari dete  hain. Indian railways ke expert  train number \ndekh kar train ke route  ki puri  jaankari de \nsakte  hain. \n\n\n\n Iske \nalawa reservation ticket par asaani se samajh aane  wali jankari \njaise ki train yatra ki doori, taarikh, Adult aur bachho ki jankari  aapko asaani mein samajh aa jayegi. Iske  alawa ticket ka ek number bhi hota hain,\njisse jaroorat padne par yeh  pata lagaya\nja sakta hain ki yeh kahan se  liya gya\nhain. E-ticket par aapko ticket number ki jagah transaction  number milta \nhain. Iske neeche aapko aapki \njourney  ka class aur aane-jaane\nki jagah  ka naam milega.  E-Ticket par toh aapko apna phone number\naur  pata likhne ka option bhi milta hain\njo printout mein saaf  dikhta hain. Agar\naapka ticket confirm hain toh koi baat \nnahi lekin agar kanhi waiting ka chakkar hain toh railway ki English ki\nclass phir se join karni padegi.\n\n\n\nWL  ka chakkar \n\n\n\nApne ticket ke\nconfirm na hone ki tension to aapko hogi, saath mein short form mein likhe\nangreji ki kuch axar aapke dil ki dhadakne aur badhayenge. Aaiye nazar\ndalte  hain waiting list ke kuch aise hi\nshort  form aur unke matlab par.\n\n\n\nRLWL :- Iska matlab hain remote location waiting list. Agar  2 bade \nstations ke beech ka koi aisa station hain, jaha se jyada trains mauzood\nnahi hain toh vaha ke yatri ko kisi cancellation ki stithi mein pahle seat di\njayegi. Agar is tarah ki waiting  list\njyada bhi hain phir bhi seat milne ke chance jyada hote hain.\n\n\n\nPQWL :- Kai chhote stations ko jaroorat ke hisaab se kote mein se\nkuch seat di jati hain. Agar aap in stations se \napni yatra shuru kar  rahe hain\naur aapka ticket waiting mein hain to aap pulled  quota \nwaiting  list mein aa jate  hain. Iska matlab hain pulled quota  ke saare \nticket reserve  ho chuke hain\naur  aap oos quota ke waiting  list mein hain.\n\n\n\nCQWL :-  Jab aap tatkaal quote mein ticket\nlete hain aur aapki ticket waiting mein hoti hain, toh ise current quota\nwaiting list mein rakha  jata hain.\nAamtaur  par is  tarah ki waiting  10 tak \nhone  par confirm ho jati hain. \n\n\n\nRQWL :- Ise request waiting \nlist kahte hain. Agar route mein koi pulled quota nahi toh is tarah ki\nwaiting list ko banaya jata hain. \n\n\n\nQuota tere kitne naam :-\n\nLD :- Ladies\nquota\n\n\n\nHQ :- High official ya Headquarter\nQuota\n\n\n\nDF :- Defence Quota\n\n\n\nOS :- Outstation Quota\n\n\n\nRS :- Road side quota, Bade stations\nke beech ke aise stations jo computerized network se na jude ho tab unhe road\nside quota mein rakh kar ticket reserve ki jati hain. Isme waiting list bhi\nhoti hain.\n\n\n\nPH :- Parliament House Quota\n\n\n\nFT :- Foreign Tourist  Quota\n\n\n\nDP :- Duty pass quota\n\n\n\nHP :- Handicap Quota\n\n\n\nSS :- Senior Citizen Quota\n\n\n\nUp-Down ko bhi samajhe :- \n\n\n\nJis station se train shuru hoti hain\naur apne destination ki taraf jati hain, veh Up train kahlati hain. Vapis aane\nki yatra ko down journey kahte \nhain.  Agar koi Train Amritsar se\nMumbai ke liye shuru ho rahi hain toh ise Mumbai ki aor UP train kahenge, Jab\nyeh train  Mumbai se vaapis aayegi to ise\nDown kahenge. Down train ka number Up se \nek jyada rahta hain. Jaise agar \nUp train ka number 15707 hain toh down ka number 15708 hoga.\n\n\n\n\n\nSeat bhi jaan le :- \n\n\n\nAgar aapki ticket confirm hain toh\nSeat number ke alawa ticket dekhkar  aap\nuski position bhi jaan sakte hain. Agar aapki birth number ke samne LB likha\nhain toh iska matlab Lower yani neeche wali birth , Agar likha hain MB toh iska\nmatlab Middle Birth, UB ka matlab Upper \nBirth. Iske alawa agar SU aur SL likha hain toh iska matalab Side Upper\naur Side Lower birth hain. Railway Senior Citizen ko Lower seat dene ki koshish\nkarta hain, Agar aisi seat available  ho\nto hi. Online reservation karwane par availability ke hisaab se aap khud hi\nseat choose kar sakte hain.  \n\n\n\n\n\n\nPosted by\n\n\nHinglishpedia\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nShare to:\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nNextNewer Post\nPreviousOlder Post\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPost a Comment\n\n\n\naprieztmkrdezign\n218168578325095\n\n\n\n\n\nDebosmita Roy12 April 2016 at 20:47Nice information.
