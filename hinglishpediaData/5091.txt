Apna sar hamesha uncha aur awaaz buland rakhe.\n\nApne pahnaave ka dhyaan rakhe :- Aap logo ko apni dressing skill ke jariye apni aor attract karti hain. Koshish kare ki backpack ya trolley bag hi le jaye. Jyada jewellery aur paise apne saath nahi rakhe.\n\nApne purse ko hamesha apne paas rakhe :- Paise ko hamesha kai jagaho par rakhe jaise aapki pant ka pichla pocket ya phir bag ka kinara. Agar aapke saath koi anhoni ghatati hain toh oos waqt aapko kisi se paise nahi mangne padenge.  \n\nApne saamaan ka dhyaan rakhe ;- Apne samaan ke paas rahe aur usay apni nazaro se door na jane de. Yeh India hain. Aapke namastey kahne se pahle hi aapka saamaan gayab ho sakta hain. Isliye aapko har waqt saavdhaan rahna chahiye.\n\nKisi vyakti se koi cheez udhaar na le :- Agar koi vyakti aapko kuch deta hain toh bilkul accept na kare.
