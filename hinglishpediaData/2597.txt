Vah apni left eye ko band karke ek andhere kamre mein gaye aur right eye ki madad se lagatar 20 minutes tak Smartphone ki screen par dekhte rahe. Iske baad unhone screen ko off kar diya. Jab room mein halki roshini ki gyi toh Right eye jo ki screen ko dekh rahi thi ne room ke nayi light setting ke hisaab se adjust hone mein kafi jyada time lagaya, Matlab ki vah aankh thodi der ke liye blind ho gyi thi.
