Mana jaata hain ki kele  ke phoolo se Polycystic Ovary Syndrome (PCOS) se joojhe rahi mahilaon ko madad milti hain. \n\n\n\n\n\nDoodh pilaane wali maaon ka doodh badhaata hain :- \n\n\n\nAyurveda ka sujhaav hain ki stanpaan karwane  wali mahilaon ko Kele ke phool khane chahiye, Isse unme  jyada  doodh  banta hain. Ek research mein yeh paya gya ki iske sewan se sugar  ka level kam  kiya ja sakta  hain, halaki yeh abhi tak clinical tested  nahi hain. \n\n\n\n\nYeh pachan kriya ko durust karta hain :- \n\nKele ka phool khane mein bahut hi halka hota hain aur pachan tantra ko aaram deta hain. Agar pet mein dard ho ya acidity ki wajah se sujan ho jaye toh Kele ke phoon ko khane se yeh door ho jati hain. \n\n \n\n\n Mood ko happy bananta hain :- \n\nKele  ke phool mein Magnesium bharpoor  matra mein  paya jata hain, Jisse yeh  aapke mood  ko sahi karta hain aur aapke stress ke level ko kam karta hain. Isliye  yeh  depression aur tension aadi  ko  door  karne mein bahut faydemand mana jata hain. \n\n \n\n\n Khoon ki kami ko door karta  hain :- \n\nBanana Flower mein bahut hi acchi matra mein Iron hota hain.  Kele  ke phool ke sewan se sharir ka hemoglobin badhta hain aur yeh khoon ki kami ko door karta hain aur aapko anemia aadi se bachata hain. \n\n\n\n Free Radicals se ladta hain :- \n\nKele ke phool mein  Anti-Oxidants  bahut hi acchi matra mein paye jate  hain. Yeh  Anti-Oxidants  Free Radicals  se  ladta hain, Free  Radicals  healthy cells par hamla karke unhe damage kar dete hain, Jisse  aapko dil ki bimari, Cancer aur Skin Aging  jaisi dikkate  hone lagti hain. In phoolo mein Anti-Oxidants  mauzood hote  hain jo free  radicals  se  ladkar unhe  body  ko damage  hone  se bachate  hain. \n\n \n\n\nInfection se bachata hain :- \n\nYeh phool Pathogenic bacteria ki growth ko rokta hain.
