Desi ghee prachin kaal se hi Indian sub-continentalsmein khane mein istemal hota aaya hain. Ghee ko baalo ki samsyaon ke ilaj mein bahut hi faydemandmana gya hain. Isebalo mein lagana jitna asaan hota hain, Utna hi zyada mushkil isechhudana hotahain. Hair experts ki mane toh Ghee mein thoda sa Badam ka tel milane se usme Vitamin E ki matra kafi badh jati hain. Isse baalo ki growthbhi acchi hoti hain. Saathhi isse hud sejyada chipchipahatbhi nahi hoti hain. \n\n\n\n\nAaiye jante hain Gheehamarebaalo ke liye kaise faydemandhota hain ? Baalo ki acchi sehat ke liye Ghee se jude huyekuch gharelu nuskhe (Home Remedies of Ghee forhealthy hair in Hindi/Urdu.) :- Baalo mein ghee kaise lagana chahiye ? Ek bartan mein 4-5 chammach ghee le. Jab yeh halka gunguna ho jaye toh isme 5 gram Badam ka powder mila de aur phir 3 chammach Badam ka tel milaye. Is mishran ko acche se mila le aur dhuley huye baalo par lagaye. Halke haatho se massage kare aur 5 minutes rook kar phir se massage kare. 30 minutes tak laga rahnede aur baad mein baal dho le. Isse yeh fayde hote hain :- • Baal Safed hone se roke :- In dino baalo ka safedhona aam samsya ban gyi hain. Agar aapko bhi darr lagta hain ki kanhi aapke baal safed na ho jaye toh gheelagana shuru karde. Ghee lagaye, massage kare. Tauliyese baalo ko 15 minutes ke liye lapet de. Baad mein baalo ko gungune paani se dho le. \n• Do-moohe baalo se bachata hain :- Agaraap do-moohe baalo se preshan hain toh baal dhone se pahle baalo mein ghee ko gunguna karke lagaye. 15 minutestak lagaye rakhe, baad mein gungune paani sebaalo ko dho le. \n• Baalo ki growth badhane ke liye :- Mahine mein 3 se 4 baar ghee baalo mein lagane se baalo ki growthacchi hoti hain. Aap chahe toh Ghee ke alawa Pyaaz ya Amle ka ras bhi laga sakte hain. Isse baal ghane hone lagte hain. \n• Dandruff doorbhagaye :- Baalo mein kai wajaho se dandruffho jati hain, Lekin agar dandruff hone ki wajah rookhapan hain toh ghee se accha upay aur koi nahi ho sakta hain. Ghee aur Badamke tel ko mila kar lagaye aur 15 minutes tak laga rahne de aur phir Paani mein Gulab jal mila kar, is paani se baalo ko dhoye. Aisa niyamit karne se baalo mein se dandruff door ho jayegi. \n• Baalo ko shiny banata hain :- Ghee lagane se baal bahut silky ho jate hain. Isko gunguna kare. 20 minutes tak massage kare aur seer par thoda nimbu paani lagaye aur chhod de. 10 minutes ke baad baal dho le. Ghee baalo ko natural chamak deta hain.\n• Seer ki skin ke infection ko door karta hain :- Agar aapke seer ki skin mein kisi bhi tarah ka infection hain toh ghee lagaye.
