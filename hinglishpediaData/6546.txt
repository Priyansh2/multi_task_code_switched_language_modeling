Ganne ka juice Glucose ka bahut achcha source hota hain, Jisse yeh body ko carbohydrate rakhne ke saath energy booster ka kaam bhi karta hain. To kisi bhi packaged Juice ko peene se behtar hain rojana ek glass ganne ka ras peena.\n\n\n\n \n\n \n\nDiabetes se rakhe door \n\n\n\nGanne ke juice mein pahle se hi Chini (sugar) ki bahut jyada matra hoti hain. Lekin phir bhi yeh Diabetes ke rogiyo ke liye bahut hi faydemand hota hain, Kyonki iski meethas ka karan natural sugar hain jo Glycemic Index ke saath hi blood mein glucose ki matra ko bhi control karta hain. Type-2 Diabetes ke mareezo ko ganne ke ras ka sewan jaroor karna chahiye.\n\nCancer se bachaye\n\nGanne ke juice mein mauzood calcium, magnesium, potassium, iron aur meganize body mein alkaline ke roop mein kaam karta hain. Jisse cancer hone ki sambhavna kafi had tak kam ho jati hain. Ek research mein bhi yeh mana gya hain ki Ganne ka juice peene se Cancer ka virus hone ka khatra kafi kam ho jata hain. aur Mahilaon mein sabse jyada hone wale breast aur prostate ka cancer hone ki sambhavna kafi hud tak kam ho jati hain.\n\n\n \nKidney Surkashit rakhta hain. \n\nGanne ke juice body mein maujoo protein level ko control karta hain. Jo kidney ki sehat ke liye bahut jaroori hain. Garmiyo ke mausam mein mahilaon ko hone wale Urinary Tract  Infection , STD bimari aur jalan jaise samsyaon ko Ganne ka ras peekar door kiya ja sakta hain. Aur to aur kharaab khane-peene ka sabse jyada asar Kidney par padta hain, Jisse kai prakar ki bimariya gher leti hain. In sabse chhutkara pane ke liye ganne ka ras bahut hi labhdayak hota hain.\n\n\nAnti-Oxidants \n\nGanne ka juice Anti-oxidants se bharpoor hota hain , jo kai prakar ki bimariyo se ladkar body ke immunity system ko sahi rakhta hain. Liver ke infection se bachaane ke saath hi bilirubin level ko control karta hain. Piliya rog ke mareez ko doctor ganne ka ras peene ki salah dete hain.\n\n\n \n \nPachan sahi rakhta hain. \n\nGanne ke juice mein potassium ki matra pachan shakti ko sahi rakhne mein madad karti hain. Pet ke kai prakar ke infection se bachaane ke saath usay cancer , Gas, Apach, Pet ki jalan, Marod aadi se bhi door  rakhta hain.\n\n\nDaanto ki samsya se door rakhe \n\nSaanso ki badboo ki samsya ho ya masoodo mein dard , Yaha tak ki daanto se sambandhit kisi bhi tarah ki bimari ko door karne mein ganne ka juice bahut hi kargar hota hain. Daanto ki sehat ke saath uski chamak barkarar rakhne ke liye iska juice peena bahut hi faydemand sabit ho sakta hain.\n\nDaag-dhabbo se chhutkara \n\nHealthy skin ke liye Alpha Hydroxy acid bahut hi faydemand hota hain.
