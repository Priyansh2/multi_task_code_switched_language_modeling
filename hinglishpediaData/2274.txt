Aaiye jante hain Lavang ke fayde.\n\n\n\n\n\n\n1. Pregnancy :- Laung ko mishri ke saath pees kar chatne se pregnancy mein ultiya, jee ghabrana aur bhojan mein aruchi door hoti hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n2. Pachan :- Khane ke baad 2 Laung munh mein rakhkar chuste rahne se pet ka tanav kam hota hain. Cavity hone par rui ke fureri bana kar daant dard mein aaraam milta hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n4. Saans ki Badboo :- Laung khane se Daanto ki badboo, Saans ki durgandh aur Pyria rog mein rahat milti hain. \n\n\n\n5. Khansi :- Khansi, Jukam, Sardard mein laung, Tulsi ke patte aur adarak wali chay fayda karti hain. Lavang ko moonh mein rakh kar choosne se khansi aani band ho jati hain. \n\n\n\n\n\nPryog :- \n\n\n\nLaung ka istemal manjan, paste, goliya, churan aur chutney aadi ke roop mein kiya jata hain.  Pulaav, khichadi, Kesariya Miththa Bhaat, Kadhi, Mathri, gatte ki sabzi aadi pakvano mein swaad aur khusboo badhane ke liye iska prayog kiya jata hain. Paan mein bhi laung daali jati hain. Moonh par hone wale keel-muhanso par Laung ko saaf jagah par paani mein ghees le.
