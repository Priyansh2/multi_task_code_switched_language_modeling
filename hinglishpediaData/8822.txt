(Benefits ofDrinkingPotato Juice in Hindi/Urdu.)\n\n\n• Aaloo ka juice baal badhane mein madad karta hain. Aapke baalo ko jaldi bada karne ke liye Aaloo ke ras ka aap mask ke roop mein istemal kar sakte hain. Ek aaloo lekar iska chhilka nikaal le. Iske slices kaat le aur phir ise pees le. Ab isse juice nikaal le aur isme Shahad aur Ande ka safed bhaag mila le. Ab is paste ko sar aur baalo par lagaye.Ise 2 hours ke liye laga rahne de aur phir uske baadshampoo se baaldhoye. \n\n• Agar aap Diabetes se peedit hain toh yeh aapke liye faydemand hain. Blood mein Sugar level ko kam karne ke liye aaloo ka juicelabhkari rahta hain. \n\n• Heart ke disease aur stroke sebachne ke liye aaloo ka juicesabseaccha upay hain. Yeh naso ki rukavat, heart attack aurtumours ko badhne se rokta hain. \n\n• Research se yeh baat saabit ho chuki hain ki Kacche aaloo ka ras aapko cancer, hypertension aur kidney ke alawa kai tarah ki samsyaon se bacha sakta hain. \n\n• Har din 2 glass Aaloo ka juice peene se kai tarah ke swasthyavardhak labh hote hain, Jaise Cancer, Gastric Ulcer, Diabetes, Kidney, Heart ki problems, Hypertension aur shoulders mein ghaav se bacha ja sakta hain. \n\n• Subahuthakar breakfast se pahle aur sone se 2-3 ghante pahle aaloo ka juice piye. Isseaapki bhookh par control rahta hain aur aapka wajan ghatata hain.\n\n•Yeh aapki immunity ko badhata hain aur saath hi liver se related problems se aapko rahat dilata hain. Aapko healthy banaye rakhne mein madadgar hain. \n\n• Kidney ki bimariyo ka ilaj karne ke liye Aaloo ka juice peene ki aadat dale. Yeh Blood pressure aur Diabetesko control karne mein help karta hain. Aaloo ka juice mutrashya mein Calcium ko patthari bananenahi deta hain. \n\n• Hepatitis Liver aur Gall Bladder ki gandagi ko nikaalne ke liye Aaloo ka juice kafi faydemand saabit hota hain. Japani log Hepatitis se nijat paane ke liye Aaloo ka juice ka istemal karte thay. \n\n•Aaloo ka juice peene se aap asaani se apne cholesterol ke level ko control mein rakh sakte hain. Yeh aapke sabhi swasthya sambandhi samsyaon ka samaadhaan hain. \n\n• Aaloo ka ras niyamit roop se lene se aapki skin se related sabhi tarah ki problems khatm ho jayengi. Yeh aapki skin ko ander se saaf karke usay poshan deta hain aur saath hi usay chamakdaar banata hain. Ise tavcha par lagane se moohaso se bhi chhutkara milta hain. \n\nYaad rakhe yeh baate :- \n\n1.
