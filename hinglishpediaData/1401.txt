Screening ghar ko sahi roshini deti hain, jisse makkhiya ghar mein nahi aati hain. \n\n\n\n• Tulsi sirf apne aushadiya guno ke liye hi nahi jani jati hain, balki yeh makkhiyo ko bhagane mein bhi bahut kargar hain. Ghar mein tulsi ka paudha lagaye aur makkhiyo ko ghar se door bhagaye. Iske alawa aap taksaal, Lavenderj aur gende aadi ke paudhe bhi laga sakte hain. \n\n\n\n• Research batate hain ki Green Apple Liquid soap makkhiyo ko attract karta hain. 2 chammach green apple liquid  soap ko ek jar mein daale aur uske upar kuch inch tak paani bhar le.
