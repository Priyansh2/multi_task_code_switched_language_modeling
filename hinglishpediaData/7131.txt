Kam umar mein baal safed hona aaj kal ek aam samsya ban gyi hain. Is problem ka sabse main reason Fast life culture mein baalo ki sahi dekh-bhaal na ho pana aur pollution aadi hain. Aise mein kam umar mein baalo mein aayi safedi ko chhupaane ke liye log baalo ko dye ya color karne lagte hain. Lekin kuch asaan gharelu nukho ko apna kar baalo ko kala kiya ja sakta hain. Hum aapko batane ja rahe kuch aise hi simple home remedies aur tips jisse aapke baal naturally black (kaale) ho jayenge. In tips aur nuskho ko ajma kar aap kam umar mein safed huye baalo ko phir se natural tarike se kala kar sakte hain.\n\n\nBaalo mein Aloe Vera  Gel lagane se bhi baal girna band ho jata hain aur jaldi safed bhi nahi hote hain. \n\n\n\n\nMehndi ko Nariyal tel mein mila kar paste bana le. Is paste ko baalo mein lagane se baalo ka rang attractive Dark-Brown hone lagta hain. \n\n\n\n\nNariyal Tel mein Meethe Neem ki pattiyo ko itna ubaale ki pattiya kali ho jaye. Is tel ko halke haatho se baalo ki jado par lagaye. Baal ghane aur Kale ho jayenge. \n\n\n\n\nGaay ke doodh ka makkhan lekar haatho se baalo ki jado mein lagaye. Jald hi fayda dikhne lagega. \n\n\n\n\nNimbu ka ras aur Nariyal tel milakar scalp par niyamit roop se lagane par baal kaale hote hain. \n\n\nAmla powder mein Nimbu ka ras mila kar ya taze hare Amle ko peeskar seer mein lagane se baal thick aur black ho jate hain. \n\n\nEk cup Chaai ka paani ubaalkar usme 1 chammach Namak milaye. Is mishran ko baal dhone se 1 ghanta  pahle baalo mein lagaye. Baal kaale hone lagenge. \n\n\n\n\nRojana Khali pet Amle ka Juice piye. Baal lambi umar tak Kaale rahenge. \n\n\n\n\nAam ki guthali aur Amle ko peeskar usay Seer mein lagane se safed baal kaale ho jayenge. \n\n\n\n\nAap ne apne buzurgo ko seer par Desi Ghee se malish karte huye dekha hoga. Ghee se seer ki tavcha ko poshan milta hain. Daily Ghee se seer ki malish karke bhi baalo ke safed hone ki samsya se chhutkara milta hain. \n\n\n\n\nSeer dhone ke liye Shikakai powder ya Mild Shampoo ka istemal kare. \n\n\n\n\nRojana subah thodi matra mein Amla juice lene se bhi Baal kaale hote hain. \n\n\n\n\nBaalo mein Neem ka Tel ya Rosemary ka Tel lagane se baal safed nahi hote hain. \n\n\n\n\nNariyal ke tel mein Amle ko itna ubaale ki veh kala ho jaye. Is mishran ko thanda karke raat ko sone se pahle baalo mein laga le aur phir subah uthkar baalo ko dhoye. \n\n\n\n\nBaal dhone ke liye Nimbu paani ka istemal kare. Isse baal naturally brown hone lagte hain aur safed nahi hote hain. \n\n\n\n\nKam umar mein Baal safed hone par 1 Gram Kali Mirch mein thoda sa Dahi mila kar Seer par lagane se bhi fayda hota hain. \n\n\n\n\nPyaaz ka ras nikaal kar usay baalo ki jado mein halke haatho se lagaye, baal ghane aur kaale ho jayenge. \n\n\n\n\nTil ka Tel baalo ke liye bahut hi accha hota hain. Saath hi ise khane se bhi bahut fayda milta hain. Agar aap apne khane mein Til ko shamil karte hain, toh isse aapke baal lambe samay tak ghane aur kaale bane rah sakte hain. \n\n\n\n\nAmla Juice, Badam Tel aur Nimbu ka ras mila kar baalo ki jado mein lagaye, Baalo mein chamak aayegi aur veh safed bhi nahi honge. \n\n\nAdrak ko pees kar usme thoda Shahad mila kar paste banaye aur apne seer par lagaye. Is upaay ko apnane se safed baal phir se kaale hone lagte hain. \n\n\nTurai ko kaat kar Nariyal tel mein ubaale, jab tak Turai kaali na ho jaye, Tab usay chhankar kisi bottle mein bhar le. Rojana is tel ko baalo mein lagaye. Dheere-dheere baal kaale hone lagenge. \n\n\n200 Gram Amla, 200 Gram Bhangra, 200 Gram Mishri, 200 Gram Kaale Til in sabhi ka churan bana kar rojana 10 gram ki matra mein lene se kam umar mein safed huye baal phir se kaale hone lagte hain. \n\n\n\n\nBaalo mein rojana Sarso ka tel lagane se baal hamesha kaale rahenge. \n\n\n\n\n2 Chammach Heena powder, 1 Chammach Dahi, 1 Chammach Methi, 3 Chammach Coffee, 2 Chammach Tulsi powder, 3 Chammach Pudina paste mila kar baalo mein lagaye. 3 Ghante baad shampoo kare. Kam umar mein safed huye baal phir kaale ho jayenge. \n\n\nSookhe Amle ko paani mein bhigo kar uska paste bana le. Is paste mein 1 Chammach Nilgiri ka tel milaye.
