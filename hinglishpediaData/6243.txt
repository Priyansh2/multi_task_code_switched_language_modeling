Home Remedies of Salt in Hindi/Urdu. Aaiye jante hain Namak se jude in behtarin gharelu nuskhe aur upay ke baare mein aur isse aapko kaun kaun se fayde hote hain.\n\nNamak ke gharelu nukshe aur unse hone wale fayde :- \n\n• Kabz door kare \n\nEk glass Chhacch (lassi) mein chutki bhar namak mila kar peene se digestion system behtar hota hain. Is nuskhe ko ajmaane se aapko kabz ki problems se aaram milega.\n\n• Muscles pain mein faydemand \n\nGungune paani mein 4-5 chammach Namak mila le. Isme cotton ka kapda bhigokar dard wali jagah par dheere-dheere se senkaai kare. Isse muscles pain mein rahat milegi aur dard door hoga.\n\n\n• Daanto ko safed banane ke liye \n\nEk Chammach Namak, ek chammach soda aur ek chammach Nimbu ka ras mix kare. Isse regular daanto par halke haathon se maalish kare. Isse daant majboot, chamkdaar aur bacteria free ban jate hain.\n\n\n• Digestion ke liye faydemand \n\nEk glass lassi mein thoda sa namak mila kar peene se digestion behtar hota hain.\n\n\n• Dandruff door kare \n\nAadha katori Khatte dahi mein ek chammach namak milaye. Phir is mixture ko apne baalo mein lagaye. Kareeb 30 minutes baad apne baalon ko dho le. Isse aapko dandruss se chhutkara milta hain.\n\n\n• Healthy skin ke liye \n\n5 Chammach Namak ko 1 chammach Olive oil ke saath mix kare aur phir ise halke haatho se skin par massage kare. 10 minutes ke baad naha le. Isse skin soft aur healthy ban jati hain.\n\n• Soojan kam kare \n\nPairo mein sujan hone par gungune paani mein namak mila kar 10 minutes ke liye pairo ko isme duboye. Phir pairo ko towel se saaf karle, Isse pairo ki soojan kam karne mein madad milegi.\n\n• Chamakdaar naakhoono ke liye \n\nNamak ko baking soda aur Nimbu ke ras mein mila le. Ise dheere-dheere apne naakhoono par lagaye. Hafte mein 1 baar yeh nuskha ajmaane se aapke naakhoon soft aur glowing banenge.\n\n\n• Joints pain ke liye \n\nEk balti paani mein 2 ya 3 chammach namak milaye. Regular is paani se nahane par jodo ke dard se aaram milega.\n\n• Aankhon ke dard ke liye \n\nGulab jal mein chutaki bhar namak milaye. Ise 2 cotton balls mein bhigo kar 5 minutes ke liye aankhon par rakhe. Aankho ka dard aur Sujan door hogi. \n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPosted by\n\n\nHinglishpedia\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nShare to:\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nNextNewer Post\nPreviousOlder Post\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPost a Comment\n\n\n\naprieztmkrdezign\n218168578325095\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nSubscribe to:\nPost Comments (Atom)\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nLoading...\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPopulars\nComments\nArchive\n\n\n\n\nFeature Post\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPopular\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nRukhe baalo ko Silky banane ke gharelu upay aur nuskhe (Tips for Dry Hair to make Silky)\nKya aap Rukhe baalo (Dry Hair) se preshan hain?
