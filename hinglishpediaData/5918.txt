Lekin kya aapko pata hain ki in perfume ya Cologne mein 50 se lekar 90 percent tak alcohol paya jata hain. Is tarah ke perfume ka istemal dhyaan se karna chahiye. \n\n\n\nVanilla extract :- Sweets, Cake aur dusri meethe pakwano mein istemal kiya jane wala Vanilla satv (Extract) bhi alcohol se bhara  hua hota hain. Darasal isme paye jane wali alcohol ka level, Vodka  aur Gin  ke barabar  hota hain. Yahi wajah hain ki Vanilla satv  ki kuch boondo ka hi istemal  kiya jata hain. \n\n\n\nCough Syrup :- Cough, Khansi ya Seene ki jakdan ke liye  aap jis cough syrup yani ki khansi ki dawaa ka istemal karte hain, Darasal usay banane ke liye  kuch alcohol ka istemal kiya jata hain. Yahi wajah hain ki aksar khansi ki dawa peene ke baad  nasha aane ke saath hi kai baar neend bhi aa jati hain. \n\n\n\nMouthwash :- Mooh ko bacteria aur mooh ki badboo se bachane ke liye aap har subah mouthwash ka istemal karte hain, Usme lagbhag 30% tak Alcohol paya jata hain. Galti se bhi agar aap mouthwash nigal le toh yeh aap par nashila asar daalta hain. \n\n\n\nSanitizer :- Daily aap apne haatho ko saaf rakhne  ke liye jis Sanitizer  ka istemal  karte hain, usme lagbhag 60 se  85% tak alcohol paya jata hain, jo ki bacteria ki behtar safaai karne mein  helpful hoti hain.
