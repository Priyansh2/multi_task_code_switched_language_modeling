Aaiye jante hain Fansi se jude kuch general knowledge. \n\n\nJaniye accused ko death punishment milne par subah ke time hi fansi ke fandey par kyon latkaya jata hain? \n\n\n\nAaiye jante hain ki fansi ke waqt kya-kya hota hain? \n\n\n\nSubah ke waqt hi di jati hain fansi :- Fansi dene ka waqt subah hi mukararr isliye kiya jata hain kyonki jail manual ke tahat jail ke baaki kaam suraj nikalne ke baad kiye jate hain. Fansi ki wajah se jail ke baaki kaamo par asar na pade, Isliye suraj nikalne se pahle subah-subah fansi di jati hain. \n\n\n\n\nFansi dene se pahle jallaad kya kahta hain? \n\n\n\nFansi dene se pahle jallad kahta hain, "Mujhe maaf kar diya jaye. Hindu bhaai ko Ram-Ram, Musalman bhai ko salam, Hum kya kar sakte hain, Hum to Hukm ke gulam hain."\n\n\n\n\nKitni der ke liye fansi dene ke baad  dead body fande par latke rahti hain? \n\n\n\nDead body kitni der ke liye rassi par latkaye rakhna hain, iska koi rule nahi hain. Lekin fansi dene ke 10 minutes baad medical team dead body ki jaanch karti hain. \n\n\n\n\nFansi ke waqt kin logo ka hona jaroori hain? \n\n\n\nFansi dete waqt vaha par Executive Magistrate, Jailer aur Jallad ka mauzood rahna bahut hi jaroori hota hain. Inme se kisi bhi kami mein fansi nahi di ja sakti hain. \n\n\n\n\nFansi ki punishment dene ke baad judge apne pen ki nib kyon tod deta hain? \n\n\n\nHamare law mein fansi ki sazaa ko sabse badi sazaa mana gya hain. Isliye judge is death punishment ko likhne ke baad pen ki nib tod deta hain, taki uska istemal dobara se na ho sake. \n\n\n\n\nFansi dene se pahle criminal ki aakhiri khawaaish kya kuch hoti hain? \n\n\n\nJail Administration Fansi se pahle aakhiri khwaahish puchhta hain, Jo jail ke andar aur manual ke tahat hota hain, Isme veh apni family se milne, koi special dish khane ke liye ya phir koi religious holy book padhne ki iccha kar sakta hain.
