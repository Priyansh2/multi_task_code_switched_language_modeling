Aayiye jante hain ki kaise hain yeh smartphones:-\n\n\nKaisa hain HTC One M9+ \n\n\nHTC One M9+ mein 5.2 inch ki Quad HD display hain jiska resolution 1440X2560 p hain. Design ki baat karein to screen ko corning Gorilla glass 4 protection diya gya hain. Fronts & dual speakers diye gye hain aur device ke niche vale hisse mein Bio-metrical Authentication system diya gya hain. Device mein 2.2 GHz ka MediaTek processor laga hua hain, jiske saath 3 GB ki RAM lagi hui hain. Internal Memory 32 GB hain, Jise 128 GB tak badhaya ja sakta hain. HTC One M9+ mein 5.0.2. lollipop Android diya gya hain, Jiske saath UID 7 skin di gyi hain. Agar Camera ki baat ki jaye to rear Camera 20.7 Megapixel ka hain aur front camera 4 ultra-pixel ka hain. Company ne iss device ko 52500 rupay mein launch kiya hain. \n\n\n\nKaisa hain HTC One E9+\n\n\n\nHTC ne HTC One E9+ ko bhi announce kiya hain. Iss smartphone mein 5.5 inch ki quad Hd  Screen display di gyi hain. Jismein 1440X2560p ka resolution hain. Camera ki baat ki jaye to yeh phone aapko 20 Megapixel ka rear camera deta hain. Front camera ultra-pixel hain. Device ka processor pichli device ki tarah hain. RAM bhi 3 GB ki hain. Internal Storage 16 GB ki hain jise 128 GB tak badhaya ja sakta hain. Battery ki baat ki jaye toh isme 2800 mAh ki battery hain. Iss device mein aap voice command se bhi selfie click kar sakte hain. Iss Device ke price ko abhi disclose nahi kiya gya hain. \n\n\n\nKaisa hain HTC Desire 326G\n\n\n\nAb baat karte hain HTC ki teesri Device HTC Desire 326G ki.  Yeh ek budget device hain jo dual Sim feature uplabadh karvaati hain. Camera ki baat karein toh iss phone mein 8 Megapixel ka rear aur 2 Megapixel ka front facing Camera hain. Processing speed ke liye quad core processor ke saath 1 GB ki RAM di gyi hain. Internal Memory 8 GB ki hain, jise 32GB tak badhaya ja sakta hain. Battery 2000 mAh ki hain jo badhiya standby time de sakti hain.
