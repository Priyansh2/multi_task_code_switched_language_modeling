Why should not keep eggs in refrigerator? \n\nAnde ke chhilke par mazood bacteria sehat ke liye nuksaandeh saabit ho sakte hain. Ande ko fridge mein rakhne ke baad phir usay room temperature par rakhne par condensation hone ka khatra kafi badh jata hain. condensation se ande ke chhilke mein mauzood bacteria ki speed badh sakti hain aur uske ander daakhil hone ke chances bhi badh jate hain. Aise mein ande ko khana sehat ke liye khatarnaak saabit ho sakta hain.\n\nAnde ko fridge mein rakhne se iske jaldi kharaab hone ka bhi khatra rahta hain. Room temperature par rakhe gye Ande jyada dino tak tikate hain. Fridge mein rakhe gye eggs boil karte waqt jaldi toot jate hain. Cake banane ke liye bhi fridge mein rakhe huye eggs sahi nahi hote hain. Ando ko beat karne mein dikkat aati hain. Ando ki safedi sahi tarike se wipe nahi hoti.\n\nKai baar ande ke upari bhaag mein gandagi rah jati hain, toh yeh fridge mein rakhi dusri cheezo ko bhi kharaab ya ineffective kar sakti hain. Aise mein ando ko fridge mein rakhne se kai tarah ke infection hone ka khatra ho sakta hain. Fridge ki cooling se Eggs mein paye jane wale kai poshak tatv bhi khatm ho jate hain.
