Kaha ja raha hain ki yeh smartphone Motorola aur Lenovo ke smartphone se takkar lega.\n\n\n\n\nSmartphone se takkar lega :- \n\n\n\nSutro ki maane to Panasonic Eluga Icon Smartphone market mein maujood kai smartphones se takkar lega. Jisme Motorola ka Moto G 3rd gen, Lenovo ka K3 Note, Xiaomi ka Redmi Note 4G aadi smartphone shamil hain. Company ne isme camera ke saath anya features par vishesh dhyaan diya hain. Jisme UI features jaise iSense, Smart Awake aur Blink play aadi shamil hain. Jo users ke liye behad jaroori hain.\n\n\n \n\n \n\nCamera bhi hain kafi behatrin :- \n\n\n\nPanasonic Eluga Icon abhi 2 rango mein hi available hain. Yeh Gold aur Slate color mein available hain. Iski 5.5 inch HD display hain. 4G LTE connectivity par based yeh phone ki sabse khas baat iski socking price hain. Itne High tech Features se less hone ke bawjood iski price sirf 10,990 Rupaye hain. Is smartphone ka camera bhi kafi behtarin hain. Isme LED flash ke  saath 13 Megapixel ka rear aur 8 Megapixel ka front camera diya gya hain. Yeh Android 4.4 Kitkat par chalta hain. Iske alawa isme 1.5GHz Octa-Core processor, 2GB RAM di gyi hain. Iski storage capacity 16GB tak hain, jise 32GB tak expand kiya ja sakta hain. Iske alawa isme Wi-Fi, GPS, GPRS, Micro-USB, Bluetooth aadi ki connectivity hogi. Is phone ki battery bhi kafi jaandaar hain.
