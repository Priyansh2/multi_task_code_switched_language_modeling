Home remedies for burn tongue in Hindi/Urdu. \n\nKai baar hum kuch garma-garm khana ya drinks pee lete hain, Jisse hamari jeebh jal jati hain. Jeebh jalne par hame bahut hi jyada bura mahsoos hota hain. Jeebh ke jal jane par kuch khane peene par hame jalan hoti hain aur khana ka taste bhi mahsoos nahi ho paata hain. Isliye hame kabhi bhi jaldbaazi mein garma-garm khana ya chai-coffee nahi peeni chahiye, kyonki jab jeebh jal jati hain, to kuch dino tak hame kafi takhleefo ka saamna karna padta hain.\n\nAaj ke article mein hum aapke liye kuch aise gharelu nuskhe aur upay lekar aaye hain, jo aapki jali huyi jeebh ko kafi rahat dilayenge.\n\n\n\nJeebh ke jalne par jaroor apnaye yeh gharelu nuskhe aur upay :- \n\n• Moonh se saans lena chahiye\n\nJab aap moonh se saans lenge to moonh mein thandi hawa daakhil hogi, jo jeebh ko chhukar jayegi, Jisse aapki jali hui jeebh ko kafi aaram milega.\n\n• Aloe Vera Gel lagaye \n\nJeebh ke jalne ke baad taaza Aloe Vera ke pattiyon se nikaala hua gel jeebh par lagaye. Iske alawa aap Aloe Vera gel ko ice cube mein jama kar bhi jeebh par laga sakte hain.\n\n\n• Dahi se kha kar paye aaram \n\n\nJeebh ke jalne ke baad ek chammach Dahi jeebh par kuch der tak rakhe. Isse aapke mooh mein thandak aur araam mahsoos hoga. Iske alawa aap ek glass thanda doodh bhi pee sakte hain. Isse bhi jeebh ki jalan door hoti hain.\n\n\n• Baking Soda kare kamaal\n\n\nIske istemal se mooh ki acidity kam hoti hain aur yeh garmahat ko door karta hain. Jeebh ke jalne ke baad paani mein baking soda mila kar kulla kare. Isse aapko kafi jaldi aaram mil jayega.\n\n• Jeebh par Shahad lagana chahiye \n\n1 Chammach Shahad ko jeebh par rakhe aur kuch der baad usay kha le. Honey mein aise natural properties payi jati hain jo burn hui tongue ke dard ko kam kar deta hain.\n\n\n• Vitamin E ka istemal \n\nVitamin E wale tel ki kuch boonde jeebh par lagane se jalan se bahut jaldi chhutkara milta hain.\n\n\n• Saada Khana hi khaye\n\nAgar aap spicy aur masaledar khana khane ke shaukin hain to Jeebh jal jane ke baad aapko Simple khana khane ki salah di jati hain. Kyonki Plain aur Ubala hua khana khane se jeebh ko kafi aaram milta hain.\n\n• Jeebh par Chini lagaye \n\nJali hui jagah par ek chutki safed cheeni lagaye aur usay kuch minutes aisa hi laga rahne de. Dheere-dheere karke chini ghulne lagegi, isse jalan aur dard se kafi aaram milta hain.\n\n\n• Chewing gum chabana chahiye \n\nJeebh ke jalne par aapko Peppermint wale chewing gum chabane ki salah di jati hain, Isse aapki jeebh ko kafi thandhak aur aaram milta hain. Chewing chabane se moonh mein thook kafi jaldi-jaldi banne lagta hain. Jisse mooh hamesha geela rahta hain aur dard se aaram milta hain.\n\n\n• Ice cube lagaye \n\nJeebh jal gyi hain to fridge mein rakhi hui ice cube ko moonh mein rakhe. Burf ke tukde ko choosne se jeebh ki jalan aur dard dono mein aaram milta hain.
