Rohu fish India ke alag-alag states jaise Odisha, Bihar, Uttar Pradesh, West Bengal aur Assam mein bade hi chaav ke saath khayi jati hain. Machhli ke saath-saath iske ando ko bhi tal kar khaya jata hain. Isliye apni diet mein Rohu Fish ko jaroor shamil karna chahiye. Rohu Machhali khane se kya kya fayde aur benefits hote hain, Aaiye inke baare mein jante hain.\n\nDimaag banaye tez :- Machhali aur dimaag ka ek accha mel dekha gya hain. Agar aap niyamit roop se Rohu machhali ko apne bhojan mein shamil karenge toh aapka dimaag majboot banega.\n\nKam Fat wali machhali :- Rohu Fish mein Fat bahut kam hota hain. Ise kha kar aapko shakti milegi par Fat nahi.\n\nDil ke liye faydemand :- Machhali mein Omega3 Fatty Acid hota hain Jo dil ko healthy banane ke kaam aata hain.\n\nSkin ki dekhbhaal :- Machhali khane se jhurriya der se padti hain, Jisse umar kam lagne lagti hain. Iske saath hi suraj ki dhoop se hone wale nuksaan se bhi rahat milti hain.\n\nProtein se bhari :- Machhali mein kafi saara protein paya jata hain aur Rohu machhali mein aur bhi jyada protein hota hain. Rohu machhali ko baccho aur bado sabhi ko protein ke liye khana hi chahiye.\n\nCancer hone se roke :- Logo ko aajkal Cancer jaisi janlewa bimariya hone lagi hain, Jiska ilaj rohu machhali ke paas hain. Mana jata hain ki Rohu machhali mein Anti-Oxidant kafi acchi matra mein paye jate hain jo ki cancer se ladne mein madadgar hota hain.\n\nVitamin C se bharpoor :- Rohu macchali mein kafi saara Vitamin C hota hain, Jisse health se related bimariya door hoti hain. Cough Sardi aadi rohu khane se door ho jate hain.\n\nMinerals ka khazana :- Iron, Zinc, Iodine, Potassium,Calcium aur Selenium aadi kuch aise minerals hain, Jo sharir ko rohu machhali ko khane se mil sakte hain.\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPosted by\n\n\nHinglishpedia\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nShare to:\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nNextNewer Post\nPreviousOlder Post\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPost a Comment\n\n\n\naprieztmkrdezign\n218168578325095\n\n\n\n\n\nAdmin19 September 2016 at 14:03Macchali khana mujhe bahut pasand hain, Khas karke mujhe Rohu Fish khana jyada accha lagta hain.
