Dancing se blood circulation accha rahta hain aur body bhi tone ho jati hain. Is dance mein body ka har hissa khichta hain, Iska jyada pose yoga ki tarah lagta hain. Isse dhyaan lagane ki power badhti hain. Sharir mein lacheelapan aata hain aur jami huyi charbi galtihain. \n\n\n• Pole Dancing :- Pole par chadhna aur latakna body ki badhi huyi calorie ko kam karta hain. Daily 30 minutes tak pole dancing ka matlab hain rojana gym mein 20 minutes bitana. Is dance se body tone, shape aur weight kam hote hain. . \n\n\n• Belly Dancing :- Is dance mein body ka har hissa move karta hain. Isme kulhe ka istemal sabse jyada kiya jata hain. Kamar ki charbi kam hone ke saath saath jaangh aur pet ki charbi bhi kam hoti hain. Is dance ko aap YouTube par Video dekh kar ya phir kisi dance class ko join karke seekh sakte hain. Is dance mein body mein lacheelapan aata hain aur body bhi fit rahti hain. . \n\n\n• Freestyle :- Yeh dance aapko free hokar naachne mein madad karta hain. Isme na kisi step ki tension hoti hain aur na hi body movement ki. Is dance se sharir ka motapa kam hota hain aur body mein lacheelapan bhi aata hain. Is dance ko karne ke liye fast music beat ki jaroorat padti hain, jo non-stop bajti rahe. Rojana 30 minutes tak freestyle dance karne se wajan kam hota hain. \n\nDo der kis baat ki hain aap bhi in dance form ko apnayiye aur Dance kijiye aur apna weight kam hote huye dekhe.
