Isliye Dengue ke bukhaar mein Kiwi phal ko khane se fayda milta hain. Is pahadi phal mein Fiber, Vitamin E, C, Folic Acid aur Carotenoids paye jate hain jo blood mein girate huye plates ko badhaane mein madad karte hain. Isliye dengue ke patient ko Kiwi fruit jaroor khana chahiye. \n\n• Palak :- Apni diet mein 1 time 1 Katori palak ka saag jaroor khaye. Kyonki Palak mein Vitamins bharpoor matraa mein paye jate hain jo khoon mein girte huye plates ko increase karne mein help karte hain. \n\n•Haldi :- Turmeric mein Curcumin naam ka Antioxidant hota hain jo body Metabolism Rate ko increase karta hain, jisse dengue se bachne mein madad milti hain. \n\n• Kaddu :- Kaddu mein Vitamin A aur Vitamin C bharpoor matra mein paye jate hain, Jisse ise khane se body ki immunity strong banti hain aur dengue se bachaav hota hain. \n\n• Methi ke patte :- Methi ke patte fever ko kam karne mein help karte hain. Methi ka saag khane se neend bhi acchi aati hain. Methi ki pattiyo ko paani mein bhigo kar uska paani bhi piya ja sakta hain. Methi ke powder ko paani mein gholkar peene se bhi fayda hota hain. \n\n• Adrak :- Dengue se bachne ke liye aap apni diet mein Ginger ka istemal jaroor kare. Adrak mein Anti-Bacterial properties payi jati hain jo Dengue ke asar ko kam karti hain. \n\n\n• Orange aur Amla :- Raat ko sone se pahle Mitti ke bartan ya kanch ke bartan mein sookha hua Amla paani mein bhigo de.
