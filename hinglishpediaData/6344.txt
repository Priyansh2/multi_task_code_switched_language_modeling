Experts ke mutabik 2 se 3 tarah ke fruit jaroor hi khane chahiye, kyonki isse aapko energy milegi. Kela aur Papita jaise phalo ko subah khali pet khana chahiye. In dono mein Sodium aur Fiber bharpoor  matra mein paye jate hain jo aapke pure din ko active banaye rakhte hain. \n\n\n\nDusri aor kuch aise fruits bhi hote hain jinhe khana khane ke baad khane chahiye. Aise phal pachan (Hajme) ko durust banate hain. Aaj hum aise phal-fruits ke baare mein janenge jinhe khane se digestion sahi hota hain, Matlab ki yeh phal digestion system (Pachan kriya) ko sahi rakhte hain. In fruits ko khane se pachne mein mushkil paida karne wale food ko divide kar unse protein nikaalne mein madad milti hain. \n\n\n\nAcchi pachan kriya (Hajma) ke liye aayiye jante hain aise phal aur fruit jinhe khana khane ke baad khaya jata hain.\n\n\nBest fruits for digestion system (Khana khane ke baad yeh phal jaroor khaye, isse aapka hajma durust rahega.) \n\n\n\n1. Ananas :- Ananas (Pineapple) jeebh par alag sa taste chhodta hain, jiski wajah se kai log ise khana pasand nahi karte hain. Lekin pachan ke liye yeh bahut hi accha phal hain. Isme Bromelain Enzyme hota hain jo khane ko hajam karne mein madad karta hain. \n\n\n\n\n2. Kela :- Yeh aapki aanto ke kaam karne ko sahi banata hain. Kele ko aap khana khane ke pahle ya baad mein kabhi bhi kha sakte hain. Dono hi tariko se yeh pet ke liye faydemand hota hain. \n\n\n\n\n3. Apple :- Apple mein Fiber bharpoor matra mein paya jata hain. Agar aapko pachan se related problem hain to Seb digestion ke liye sabse best fruit hain. Khane ko sahi tarah se  pachane ke liye khana khane ke 15 minute baad 1 apple jaroor khaye. \n\n\n\n\n4. Avocado :- Is phal mein nutrients, Vitamins aur Energy bharpoor matra mein hoti hain. Heavy food ko pachane ke liye khana khane ke baad aadha Avocado phal ko khana accha rahega. \n\n\n\n\n5. Nashpati :- Nashpati pet ke liye bahut hi accha phal mana jata hain. Ise aap hafte mein ek baar jaroor khaye. Haal hi mein huye ek research se pata chala hain, ki naashpati mein Fiber hota hain jisse potty sahi tarah se hoti hain. Nashpati Sodium free, cholesterol free aur Fat free hota hain aur 190 milligram Potassium paya jata hain, jisse pachan tantra majboot banta hain. \n\n\n\n\n6. Anjeer :- 1 Cup sukhe Anjeer mein 15 gram Fiber hota hain jo khane ko tod kar usay hazam karne mein madad karta hain. Khana khane ke baad ek cup anjeer ka sewan karne se pachan kriya sahi rahti hain. \n\n\n\n\n7. Rasbhari :- Agar aapko Diabetes aur pachan se related problem hain to rasbhari aapke liye accha option saabit ho sakta hain. Is phal mein sugar kam hota hain aur Fiber zyada paya jata hain. Isme Calories bhi kam hoti hain, Yeh behtar digestion ke liye bahut hi accha phal hain. \n\n\n\n\n8. Papita ;- Agar aap 24 ghato ke andar apni pachan shakti ko sahi karna chahte hain to kaccha papita aapki madad kar sakta hain. Agar aapki Aant kamjor hain aur Aapke sharir mein Vitamins jama nahi ho pa rahe hain to aap isse vitamin C pa sakte hain. Isme Papain hota hain jo protein ko todta hain aur khane ko hajam hone wala banata hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPosted by\n\n\nHinglishpedia\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nShare to:\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nNextNewer Post\nPreviousOlder Post\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPost a Comment\n\n\n\naprieztmkrdezign\n218168578325095\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nSubscribe to:\nPost Comments (Atom)\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nLoading...\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPopulars\nComments\nArchive\n\n\n\n\nFeature Post\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPopular\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nRukhe baalo ko Silky banane ke gharelu upay aur nuskhe (Tips for Dry Hair to make Silky)\nKya aap Rukhe baalo (Dry Hair) se preshan hain?
