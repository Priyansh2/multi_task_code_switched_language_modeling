Noodles mein kisi bhi tarahka protein aur mineralnahi paya jata hain, Jisse hamari sehat acchi ho. Isiliye hum noodles ko junk food bhi kahtehain. \n\n\n\n2. Agar aap non-vegetarian hain toh noodles mein Anda, Meatya chicken daal kar usay tasty aur healthy bana saktehain. \n\n\n\n3. Noodles mein bahut saara carbohydrate hota hain, jisse sharir ka sugar level badh jata hain. Isliye yeh sehat ke liye kharab hain. \n\n\n\n4. Agar noodles ko acchi tarah se nahi banaya gya toh veh hamare sharir ke liye nuksaandeh saabit ho saktahain. Veh isliye kyonki yeh taiyaarkarke pack kardiye jatehain. Isko khane se foodpoisoning aur pet mein infection ho sakta hain. \n\n\n\n6. Noodles maide se bana hotahain. Jisse yehsharir mein dehydration paida karsaktahain. Saath hi ise khane se kabz ki samsya paida hoti hain. \n\n\n\n7.
