Medicine ke roop mein iska istemal kiye jane ki history kafi  purani hain. Iska bura asar dusri sharab ke mukable kafi kam hota hain. Agar aap Vodka ko sirf ek alcoholic drink ke roop mein pasand karte aaye hain toh aaj hum aapko batane ja rahe hain Vodka ke kuch aise gharelu upay aur nuskhe Jinhe jaankar aap ise aur bhi upyogi manne lagenge. Aaiye jante hain Vodka ke kuch jaroori Home-remedies voh hi simple Hindi/Urdu mein :- \n\n• Mouth wash ki tarah :- Vodka ka istemal aap mouth wash ki tarah  bhi kar sakte hain. Dalchini powder mein Vodka mila kar 2 hafte tak ek bottle mein rakh kar mouthwash ke roop mein upyog  kiya ja sakta hain. Ise nigle nahi thoda gargle karke thook de. Mooh saaf ho jayega\n\n• Hair Conditioner ki tarah :- Vodka  gajab ke hair  conditioner ka kaam karta hain. Apni shampoo ki bottle mein thoda sa vodka mila le aur phir is shampoo ko use kare, Isse scalp healthy hone lagenge. Vodka P.H. Ko level kar deta hain. Sath hi iske upyog se baal oil free, shiny aur silky ho jate hain. \n\n• Flowers ko fresh rakhne ke liye :- Agar aapko apne ghar mein decoration ke liye lagaye gye flowers ko lambe waqt tak taro-taza rakhna ho toh ek bottle paani ke bottle mein vodka  ki kuch boonde aur 1 chammach chini mila kar ise acche se ghol le aur is ghol ko phoolo par spray kare. Lambe waqt tak phool fresh rahenge. \n\n• Shoes ki chamak badhaane ke liye :- Agar aapke shoes bahut zyada gande ho gye hain aur aapke paas polish karwane ka time nahi ho toh thoda sa vodka shoes par spray kare aur kapde se saaf kare, Jootey chamakne lagenge. \n\n• Haatho ko saaf  karne ke liye :- Agar aap apne haatho ko bacteria free banana  chahte hain toh hand sanitizer ki jagah ek boond vodka ka bhi istemal kar sakte hain. Ji haan ek boond vodka lekar usay hand sanitizer ki tarah use karenge toh haath bacteria free ho jayenge. \n\n• Dard se rahat ke liye :- Agar aapki body mein kisi bhi tarah ka pain ho raha ho toh thande paani mein thoda sa vodka  mila kar Ice cube jama  le. Vodka wala burf ko dard par lagane se dard door ho jata hain. \n\n• Glass saaf karne ke liye :- Ek cup vodka mein ek cup paani mila kar isse Jewellery saaf karne par yeh magic ki tarah work karta hain. Isi tarah agar  aap glass clean karna chahte hain toh vodka aur paani ko mila kar ghol bana le aur  glass ko saaf kare. Glass chamakne lagenge. \n\n• Skin ko glowing  banane ke liye :- Agar aapko apni skin ko healthy aur glowing banana hain toh vodka ko cotton mein lekar chehra saaf kare. Rojana aisa karne se skin healthy rahti hain. \n\n• Daant ke dard mein :- Daant mein dard ho toh vodka ki kuch boonde dard wali jagah par laga kar chhod de. Isse daant dard mein turant aaram milta hain. \n\n• Bandage nikaalne ke liye :- Agar kanhi chot lag gyi hain aur aap bandage lagane ke baad usay dard ki wajah se change karne se bach rahe hain toh ek cotton mein thoda sa vodka lekar usay bandage par halke haatho se lagaye. Bandage asaani se nikal jayega. \n\n• Bukhaar mein kargar :- Kisi ko agar tez bukhaar ho toh Vodka ko paani mein daal kar saaf tauliye ko usme bhigo kar nichod le. Is tauliye ko seer par rakhne se bukhaar  kam ho jata hain. \n\n• Kaan mein dard hone par :- Kaan mein dard hone par Vodka ki kuch boonde kaan mein daale aur 5 minutes ke liye chhod de, Phir ise saaf karle.
