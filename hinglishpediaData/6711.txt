Isliye papite ke patto ka ras dengue ke ilaj ke liye kargar mana jata hain. \n\n\n\n\n2. Yeh blood mein white cells aur plates ko increase karne mein help karte hain. \n\n\n\n\n4. Blood plates badhane mein kargar :- Papite ke patto ka juice peene se khoon mein plates ki kami ko pura kiya ja sakta hain. Is chamatkari patto mein khoon mein blood plates ko badhane ke gun paye jate hain. Iske liye Papite ke patto ka ras rojana 2 chammach kuch dino tak peena chahiye. \n\n\n\n\n5. Pimples khatm kare :- Agar aap pimples ki problems se preshan hain toh papite ki sukhi pattiyo ko lekarpaani ke saath mix karke paste bana le.
