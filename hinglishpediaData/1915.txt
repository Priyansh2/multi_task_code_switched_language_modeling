Prostate, Grabhashya, Mooh, Gala, Pet, Colon aur bhi kai tarah ke cancer ke khatre ko aap sirf tamatarkha kar kam kar saktehain. Tamatar mein mauzoodAnti-Oxidants, Free Radicals se ladte hain, jo hamare body cells ko damagekarneka kaam karta hain. \n\n• Aankho ke liye accha :- Tomato mein Vitamin A bahut hi acchi matra mein paya jata hain, Jo ratondhi ko rokne mein madad karta hain aur aapke aankho ki roshini ko badhata hain. Gajar aur Chukander mein paya jane walaBeta-Carotene tamatar mein bhi paya jata hain, Jo Suraj ki ultra-violet rays se tavcha ki raksha karta hain. Tamatar mein paya jane wala Lycopene Ultra-Violet rays ko kam sensitivebanata hain, Jo skin par lines aur wrinkles hone ki ahem wajah mani gyi hain. \n\n• Kidney Stoneaur Pitt ki patthari ko rokta hain : - Tamatar gurde aur pitt ki patthari ko rokne mein madad karta hain. Lekin iske liye aapko bina beej wale tamatar khane padenge. \n\n• Haddiya banaye majboot :- Tamatar mein Vitamin K aur Calcium dono hi haddiyo ko majboot banane ke saath hi unki murramat bhi karte hain. Lycopene Haddiyo ko sudhaarta hain, Jo osteoporosis se ladne mein madad karta hain. \n\n• Dard Door kare :- Agaraap bhi purane dard se preshan hain toh tamatar ise khatm kar sakta hain. Tamatar mein high Bioflavonoids aur Carotenenaam ka tatv hota hain, Jo pain ko door karne mein help karta hain. \n\n• Balo ko healthy banaye :- Tamatarmein paya jane wale vitamins baalo ko majboot banate hain. Lekin yeh patlehoteja rahe baalo mein fayda nahi pahuchata hain but ise khane se aapkebaal healthy rahenge. \n\n• Madhumeh mein faydemand :- Tamatar aapke blood sugar level ko control mein rakhta hain. Tomato mein chromium naam ka tatv paya jata hain jo blood sugar ko control karne mein madad karta hain. \n\n• Kaccha Tamatarkhana hota hain faydemand :- Tamatar mein Lycopene, Vitamin A aur C hote hain. Yeh Anti-Oxidants ka khazana hain. Vitamin C jo kacche tamatar se milta hain veh aagpar pakaaye gyetamatarse nahi milta hain.
