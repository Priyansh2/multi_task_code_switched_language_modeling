Agar galti se bacche ne kapoor ko nigal liya hain to aapko lene ke dene pad sakte hain.\n\n4. Kapoor aapke pachan tantra ko nuksaan bhi pahucha sakta hain, Kyonki yeh aapke Gastrointestinal ko puri tarah se block bhi kar sakta hain.\n\n5. Kapoor aapke sharir mein asaani se soak ho jata hain, Jisse aapko saans lene mein problem ho sakti hain.\n\n6. Kapoor se nervous system aur kidney ko nuksaan pahuchta hain, Isliye agar aapko kidney ki bimari hain ya phir aap kidney ki bimari se bachna chahte hain to iska istemal na kare.\n\n7. Agar aap Kapoor ko skin par direct apply karte hain to aapko skin allergy ya skin ki serious bimari ho sakti hain.
