Iski wajah se body mein mauzood khatarnaak bacteria ka khatma hota hain. \n\n\n\n• Digestion ko sudhaarta hain :- Namak wala paani Mooh mein lar wali granthiyo ko active karne mein madad karta hain. Jiski wajah se hamare sharir ki haddiya kamjor ho jati hain. Namak wala paani peene se khoye huye Minerals ki purti hoti hain. Jisse aapki haddiya phir se strong ho jati hain. Isliye roj subah kale namak ka paani jaroor peena chahiye. \n\n\n\n• Skin problems :- Namak mein mauzood chromium acne (pimples) se ladta hain. isme mauzood sulfur skin ko soft aur clean banata hain.
