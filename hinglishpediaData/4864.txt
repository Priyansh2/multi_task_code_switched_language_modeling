Body mein adrooni garmi hone par aapko sardi kam lagegiaur kai bimariya bhi nahi hongi. Sardiyo ke dauran sharir ki suraksha jaroori hoti hain. Iske liye aapko sahi bhojan lena jaroori hota hain. Sardiyo ke dauranaisi phal aur sabziya khaye, Jissesharir ko garmi mile. Bhojan health ko healthy bana kar rakhta hain. \n\n\nSahi khana khane se sharir mein BMI balance rahta hain. Kuch food jaise:- Lahsun, Methi aadi sharir ko garmi pradaan karte hain. Aisehi kai dusri khane wali cheeze aisi hain jo body ke temperatureko banaye rakhti hain aur immunity ko bhi strong karnemein madad karti hain. Janiye kuchaisi hi khane wali cheezo ke bare meein :- \n\n\n\nBadam :- Badam kai guno se bharpoor hota hain. Inhe sardiyo ka sabseaccha food mana jata hain. Iske sewan sekabz ki samsya door hoti hain, jo sardiyo mein sabse badi dikkat hotihain. Badam mein diabetes ko control karne ka gun hota hain. Isme Vitamin E bharpoor matra mein paya jata hain.\n\nOmega-3 Fatty Acid food :- Sardiyo mein Omega-3 Fatty Acid food sabse accha food hota hain. Yeh aamtaur par fish mein paya jatahain. Sardiyo ke dino mein machhali ka sewan jaroorkare, isse sharir ko garmi milti hain. Ismezinc bharpoor matra mein hota hain aur yeh white blood cells ko activate kar deta hain jo ki body ki immunity ko badhane mein madad karti hain. Body ki immunity power hi sharir ko bimariyo se door rakhne mein madad karti hain.\n\nMoongfali :- Sardiyo ke dino mein environment mein oxygen ki kami ho jatihain. Agar aap thand ke mausam mein koi aisa food khana chahtehain jo sharir ko garmi de aur sahi matra mein sharir ko oxygen lene mein madad kare toh moongfali ko jaroor khaye. Yeh sardi ke dino mein body ko warm rakhta hain.\n\nWarm up kare:- Sardiyo mein kuch aise food khaye jo aapko warm-up kar de. Jaise :- Til ke daane, Ramdaana aadi. Inke sewan se body ko energy milti hain aur vehgaram rahta hain. Iskealawa metabolism bhi accha rahta hain.\n\nAnaaz :- Kuch anaaz sharir ko sabsejyada garmi dete hain Jaise – Bajra. Sardi ke dino mein bajre ki roti bana karkhaye. Chotebaccho ko Bajre ki roti jaroor khani chahiye. Isme kai swasthyavardhakgun paye jate hain.\n\nAdrak :- Bachpan se hi hum daadi maa ke gharelu nuskhe mein adrak ka naam jyada sunte aa rahehain. Sach mein Adrak kafi gunkari hotahain. Sardiyo ke dino mein Adrak ki chai ya uska achaar bahut hi faydemand hota hain. Isse sharir ko garmi milti hain aur pachan kriyabhi durust rahtihain.\n\nRaseele phal na khaye :- Sardiyo ke dino mein raseele phalo ka sewan na kare. Santra, Rasbhari ya mosambi aapke sharirko thandakpradan karte hain aur aapko sardi lag sakti hain. Agar aap inhe khana chahte hain tohdhoop mein baith kar khaye.\n\nHari sabziya :- Apni diet mein hari sabziyo ko jaroor shamil kare. Sabziya sharir ki pratirodhak shamta ko badhaati hain aur garmi pradan karti hain. Sardiyo ke dino mein Methi, Gajar,Chukandar, Palak, Lahsun, Bathuaaadi ka sewan kare. Issedhersaari energy milegi.\n\nShahad :- Sardiyo ke dino mein apni diet mein shahad ka istemaljaroor kare.Isse pachan kriya mein sudhaar hoga aur aapki immunity par bhi asar padega.\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPosted by\n\n\nHinglishpedia\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nShare to:\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nNextNewer Post\nPreviousOlder Post\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPost a Comment\n\n\n\naprieztmkrdezign\n218168578325095\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nSubscribe to:\nPost Comments (Atom)\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nLoading...\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPopulars\nComments\nArchive\n\n\n\n\nFeature Post\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPopular\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nRukhe baalo ko Silky banane ke gharelu upay aur nuskhe (Tips for Dry Hair to make Silky)\nKya aap Rukhe baalo (Dry Hair) se preshan hain?
