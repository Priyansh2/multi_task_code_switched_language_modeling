How Keep fruits fresh in home?\n\n\n\nPhal-Fruitsko taza rakhne ke tips (phalo ko ghar mein kaise fresh rakhe) :-\n\n\n• Jab aapko fridge mein kafi matra mein phal rakhne ho toh unhe acchi tarah se jaanch le ki unme se koi phal kharab ya phir chotlaga hua toh nahi hain. Sade huye phal dusre acche phalo ko turantkharab kar sakte hain. \n\n\n\n• Citrus wale phal jaise Nimbu, Santra ya Mosambi aadi fridge mein rakhne par asaani seapna paani chhod dete hain. Isliyekoshish kare ki unhe kas kar panni (polythene) mein lapet kar rakhe. \n\n\n\n• Kuch fruitspar temperatureka bahut hi jyada asarpadtahain. Isliye unhe freshrakhne ke liye fridge ka istemal kiya jatahain. Lekin agar aapki fridge bhari huyi hain aur unme jagah nahi hain toh oon phalo ko ghar ki aisi jagah par rakhe jaha ka environment cool ho. \n\n\n\n• Isi tarah Strawberry ko agar fridge mein rakhna ho toh usay bina dhoyeaur bina kaate hi rakhe. Varnayeh bahut jaldi paani sokhne lagtahain aur sadh jata hain. Saath hi iska taste bhi bilkul paani jaisa lagane lagta hain. \n\n\n\n• Aisephal jinhe aap pakne se pahle hi khareed laye ho oon phalo ko pakne ke liye fridgeke baharhi rakhe. Aise phalo mein Pineapples, Banana, Mango aur pear shamil hain. \n\n\n\n• Kuch phal jaise kela ya ananas par thand ka bahut jyada asar padtahain. Isliye unhe fridgekechilled temperaturemein na rakhtehuye bahar hi rakhe. \n\n\n\n • Seb, Nashpati, strawberry, Ber aadi phalo ko fridge ki almariyo mein ek chhed wali panni mein rakhna chahiye, Jisse jama hua paani bahar nikal sake. \n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPosted by\n\n\nHinglishpedia\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nShare to:\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nNextNewer Post\nPreviousOlder Post\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPost a Comment\n\n\n\naprieztmkrdezign\n218168578325095\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nSubscribe to:\nPost Comments (Atom)\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nLoading...\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPopulars\nComments\nArchive\n\n\n\n\nFeature Post\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPopular\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nRukhe baalo ko Silky banane ke gharelu upay aur nuskhe (Tips for Dry Hair to make Silky)\nKya aap Rukhe baalo (Dry Hair) se preshan hain?
