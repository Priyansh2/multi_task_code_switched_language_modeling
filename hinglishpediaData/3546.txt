Yeh sharir mein mauzood saari gandagi bahar nikal deta hain, Jisse pet se sambandhit kisi bhi prakar ki koi samsya bhi nahi hoti hain. Saath hi pachan kriya bhi sucharu roop se chalti hain. \n\n\n\n \n\n \n\nBaalo ki majbooti ke liye :- Methi ke daano ko apni diet ka hissa banaye. Usay raat bhar paani ya nariyal ke tel mein bhigo kar subah iska paste bana kar baalo par lagane se baal kale aur chamakdar hote hain. Saath hi, Dryness aur Roosi ki samsya bhi door hoti hain. \n\n\n\n\n\nCholesterol ko kam kare :- Methi ke dane badhte cholesterol ko kam karte hain. Gharelu nuskho dwara cholesterol ko kam karne ka isse behtar ilaj koi dusra nahi hain. Methi Bad cholesterol ko bhi kam karta hain. \n\n\n\n\n\nDil ki bimariya rakhe door :- Methi ke daano aur pattiyo mein paya jane wala Galactomannan Potassium ek bahut accha source hain. Yeh sodium ki matra ko control karke dil ki dhadkan ke utar-chadhav ko maintain karta hain, Jisse blood pressure normal rahta hain. \n\n\n\n\nSeene ki jalan se rahat :- Ek chammach Methi ke dano ka sewan karne se Masaledar aur Oily khane ke karan ho rahi seene ki jalan se chhutkara paya ja sakta hain. Aamtaur par garmiyo mein Gas ki samsya ho jati hain. Ise door karne ke liye Methi kargar hain. Bas khane se kuch der pahle Paani mein bhigo dena chahiye, Jisse iske upar ka laslsapan thoda kam ho jaye. \n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nDiabetes mein faydemand :- Methi ka istemal Diabetes mein bahut hi faydemand hain. Galactomannan asaani se ghulne wala fiber hain, jo blood mein sugar ko observe karne ki capacity ko kam karta hain. Iske saath hi Methi mein Amino Acids ki mauzoodgi Insulin ke production ko bhi badhati hain, Jo diabetes ke rogiyo ke liye jaroori hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nBukhaar ke saath Gale ki kharash ki samsya mein rahat :- Kisi karan se ho rahe bukhaar ki samsya se nipatne ke liye Methi ke daano ko ek chammach Nimbu aur Shahad ke saath lene se turant fayda milta hain. Yeh body ko ander se poshan deti hain. Aur to aur Methi ke daano se lekar iski pattiya Sardi-Khansi jaisi problems se nipatne ke saath hi gale ki kharash aur dard se bhi rahat dilati hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nWajan kam karne mein sahayak :- Raat bhar paani mein bhigoye huye Methi ke dane subah khali pet chabane se motapa kam hota hain. Ghulansheel Fiber pet mein jakar phoolta hain. Isse pet bhara-bhara lagta hain, Jisse bhookh kam lagti hain aur over dieting ki samsya se bacha ja sakta hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nDelivery mein asaani :- Prasav ke dauran hone wale dard se nipatne ke liye Methi ke daane bahut hi gunkari hain.
