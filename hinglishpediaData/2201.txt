Jyadatar log chaai ko sirf energy dene wala hi mante hain, lekin aisa nahi hain, Iske aur bhi kai upyog hain. Aaiye jante hain tea ke aise hi kuch gharelu nuskho ke baare mein :-\n\n\n\n► Kaanch (Glass) ko saaf karne ke liye :- Kaanch ko saaf karne ke liye chaaye ka paani banaye. Usay kaanch par thoda sa chidke aur saaf kapde se poche. Kaanch ke daag-dhabbe saaf ho jayenge aur kanch chamakne lagega. \n\n\n\n► Daanto ko saaf karti hain :- Yadi aap apne daanto ko hamesha healthy aur White banaye rakhna chahte hain to Rojana ek Cup green tea jaroor piye. Yeh daanto ke liye behatrin medicine ki tarah kaam karti hain. \n\n\n\n► Baalo ki conditioning ke liye :- Baalo ki conditioning ke liye 2 glass paani lekar usme 4 Tea bag dale. Is paani ko thanda kar le. Paani thanda ho jaye to shampoo karne ke baad is paani ko seer mein daale, Baal silky aur shiny ho jayenge. \n\n\n\n► Period mein hone wale dard se nijat :- Period ke samay Pet mein ainthan hoti hain to usay theek karne ke liye Green Tea piye, Kyonki isme Anti-oxidants paye jate hain, Jo colon ko saaf karte hain. \n\n\n\n► Kabz door rahti hain :- Pet kharaab ho to Green Tea mein Dalchini powder aur Nimbu ka ras mila kar pijiye. \n\n\n\n► Lakdi ka furniture ko saaf karne ke liye :- Ladki ka furniture saaf karne ke liye thode paani mein 4-5 tea bags dalkar ubale. Uske baad is chaay ke paani mein saaf kapda dubo kar furniture ki safaai kare. Furniture chamakne lagega. \n\n\n\n► Mouth Wash :- Mooh ki badboo door karne ke liye Pudine ki chaai bana kar usse kulla kare. Bahut rahat milegi. Pudine ki tea banane ke liye paani mein pudine ki pattiya dal kar ubale. Phir chhankar upyog kare. Yeh mouth wash ka kaam karta hain. \n\n\n\n► Pet ki jalan door karti hain :- 1 Cup Green Tea mein 2 chammach pudina mix kijiye, Isse Pet ki jalan door ho jayegi.\n\n\n\n► Toner ka kaam karti hain :- Tea-Bag ek natural Toner ki tarah kaam karta hain. Tea bag ko paani mein bhigo kar chehre par ghooma le. Yeh toner ki tarah kaam karta hain. Iske lagatar upyog se chehra glow karne lagta hain. \n\n\n\n►Daant ka dard bhagane ke liye ;- Yadi aap daant ke dard se preshan hain to ek bowl mein thanda paani lekar tea bag ko bhigo le. Oos Tea bag ko daant dard wali jagah par nichod le. Dard se rahat milegi. \n\n\n\n► Pet dard mein dawaa ka kaam karti hain ;- Pet dard ho raha ho to Doodh wali Chaaye ki jagah Green Tea mein thoda sa Adrak mila kar pijiye. \n\n\n\n► Jale ka nishan mitane ke liye :- Jalne par Chaay ek medicine ki tarah kaam karti hain. Yadi aap jal jaye to Tea bag ko bhigo kar uska paani jale huye jagah par daale. Jale ka nishan nahi rahega. Yadi jyada jal gye ho to ek bath tub mein Tea bag daal kar paani mein let jaye. Jale ke nishan nahi rahenge. \n\n\n\n► Ulcer ke rogiyo ke liye faydemand ;- Pet mein ulcer ki wajah se dard ho raha hain, to thandi Green Tea pijiye. Isme bahut saara anti-oxidants hote hain, jo pet ke liye faydemand hain. \n\n\n\n► Safed baalo ko phir se kala banaye :- Aap safed baalo se preshan hain aur dye aur color use nahi karna chahte to ek cup paani mein 3 tea bags dale. Phir oos paani mein Mehandi daale. Raat bhar is mixture ko aisa hi rakha rahne de. Phir subah is paste ko baalo mein lagaye. Ek ghante tak is paste ko baalo mein laga rahne de. Uske baad Seer ko paani se dho le. Baalo ko dye karne ki jaroorat nahi padegi. \n\n\n\n► Keeda kaat jaye to :- Yadi koi keeda kaat jaye to Chaay ke paani mein cotton ko bhigo kar dard wali jagah par lagaye, rahat milegi. \n\n\n\n► Gas ki problem se chhutkara :- Gree Tea mein agar Peppermint mila kar piya jaye to Gas nahi banti hain. \n\n\n\n► Pairo ki badboo door kare :- Kai baar lagatar shoes pahne rahne ki wajah se pairo se badboo aane lagti hain. Aisi badboo ko door karne ke liye chaay ka paani bana kar pairo ko oos paani mein kuch der ke liye bhigo kar rakhe. Badboo door ho jayegi.\n\n\n\n► Thakaan door karne ke liye ;- Aankhon ki thakaan aur sujan ko door karne ke liye 2 tea bags ko gungune paani mein kuch der ke liye rakhe. Phir tea bags ko aankho par rakhe, Kuch der tak ise aisa hi rahne de. Aankho ki thakaan aur sujan door ho jayegi. \n\n\n\n► Behtrin Scrub bhi :- Green Tea mein thoda White Sugar aur Paani mila kar chehre par massage kare. Yeh scrub ki tarah kaam karta hain. \n\n\n\n► Injection ke dard ko khatm karne ke liye :- Injection lagne ke baad kai baar dard bahut preshan karta hain.
