Bhojan aur niyamit exercise kare. Upvaas ke baad subah uthate hi ek glass garm paani mein Nimbu nichodkar piye. Nashte mein Angoor, Papita, Nashpati aur genhu ka daliya le. \n\n\n\n\n\n4. Rogi ko roj garam paani ka enema de. Isse aanto mein mauzood jahreele tatv bahar nikal jate hain aur khoon saaf hota hain. \n\n\n\n5. Bhojan mein ubali huyi palak, methi, gajar, genhu ki 2 chapati aur 1 glass chhachh de. \n\n\n\n6. Dophar ko Nariyal paani de. \n\n\n\n\n7.  Raat ke bhojan mein ek cup ubali huyi sabziyo ka soup, Genhu ki 2 chapati, ubale huye aaloo aur Hari pattedar sabziya jaise Palak, Bathuaa, Sarso aadi khaye.\n\n\n8. Raat ko sone se pahle 1 glass fat free doodh mein 2 chammach shahad milakar le.\n\n9. Bharpoor matra mein hari pattedaar sabziya aur phalo ka juice piye. Nashpati khane se fayda hoga.\n\n10.  Daalon ka upyog bilkul naa kare. Liver cells ki safety ke liye din mein 3-4 baar Nimbu ka ras paani mein milakar pina chahiye.\n\n\n11. Muli ke hare patte piliya mein faydemand hain. In patto ko pees kar inka ras nikal le aur chhankar piye. Inse bhookh badhegi aur aante saaf hongi.\n\n12. Tamatar ka ras piliya mein labhkari mana gya hain. Is juice mein thoda sa namak aur kali mirch mila kar piye.\n\n13.  Swaasthya sudharne par 1 se 2 kilometer ghumne jaye aur kuch samay dhoop mein rahe. Paani saaf aur ubaal kar peena chahiye.
