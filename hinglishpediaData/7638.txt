Isliye healthy khana khaye. \n\n• Paani bhi hain jaroori :- Hamare sharir ko paani ki jyada matra ki jaroorat hoti hain. Isliye jyada matra mein paani piye. Jisse agar aap exercise kare toh aapke body mein bharpoor matra mein paani ho. \n\n• Calorie par dhyaan de :- Agar aap chahte hain ki aapka wajan badhe toh iske liye apni diet mein calorie ka sewan jyada kare.
