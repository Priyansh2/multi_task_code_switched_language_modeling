Is fermented liquid se hi Apple CiderVinegartaiyaarkiya jata hain. Ise hi Apple Cider Vinegar kaha jata hain. Paseene ki badboo khatm ho jayegi.\n2) Apple Cider Vinegar mein Potassium ke sath hi calcium aur phosphorus paya jata hain. Isliye thoda Apple Cider Vinegar lene se haddiya healthy rahti hain. \n3) Agar mooh aur saans se badboo aati ho toh Apple CiderVinegar kisi dawa ki tarah kaam karta hain. Agar aap saans ki badboo se preshan hain toh Apple CiderVinegar segararekare, Fayda hoga. \n4) Multani Mitti mein thoda Shahad aur Apple Cider Vinegar mila kar paste banaye aur ise chehre parlagaye. 15 Minutes ke baadapna chehra paani se dho le. Yeh aapke chehreko softaur glowing banata hain.\n5) Agaraap digestion se judi problem se preshan hain toh Paani mein thoda Shahad aur Apple Cider Vinegar mila kar le, Fayda hoga. Apple CiderVinegar mein Pectin zyada quantity mein paya jata hain, Jo kabzke saath hi Gas aur Acidity jaisi samsyaon ko bhi khatm kar deta hain. \n6) Applecidervinegar mein Anti-Septic Properties hoti hain. Iske niyamit sewan se fungalaur bacterialinfection door hota hain. Yeh pachantantra mein sudhaar lata hain. Yeh Diabetes ko bhi control karta hain. \n7) Shampoo karne ke baad 1 chammach Apple cidervinegar ko 1 glass paani mein mila le. Is paani ko balo mein dale. Dast mein iska sewan paani mein mila kar karna chahiye. \n9) Ek glass mein thoda Adrak crush karle, Usmethoda Shahad aur Apple Cider Vinegar mila le. Paani mein thoda vinegar mila kar naak mein daalne se naak turant khool jati hain. \n12) Chehra dhone se pahle paani mein thoda sa apple cidervinegarmilale. Iss paani se chehradhone par daag-dhabbe mittehain aurrangbhi nikharjata hain. \n13)Isme Acetic Acidhota hain jo wajan ko kam karne mein madadkarta hain.
