Pet ke infection se bachne ka sabse best ilaj hain gharelu nuskhe aur upay. \n\n\n\nPet ke infection ke yeh gharelu nuskhe pet ke infection ko jhat se door kar dete hain aur inka side-effect bhi nahi hota hain. Aaiye jante hain Pet ke infection ko door karne wale upyogi gharelu nuskhe aur upay ke baare mein. Home remedies for Stomach Infection in Hindi/Urdu. \n\n\n\nPet ka infection ka gharelu ilaj aur nuskhe :- \n\n\n\n• Neem :- Purane zamane se hi Neem ko infection door karne ke liye istemal kiya jata raha hain. Aap neem ki 5-6 pattiyo ko din mein ek baar jaroor khaye. Isse body ki saari gandagi bahar nikal jati hain. \n\n\n\n• Chawal ka paani :- Agar aapko diarrhoea ho gya hain ya phir aapka pet kharab ho gya ho toh chawal ka paani pijiye. Ek pan mein aadha cup safed chawal daal kar 6 cup paani mein ubaale. Is paani ko chaan kar thanda karle. Phir isme aadha chammach Shahad aur dalchini powder mix karle. Is paani ko dheere dheere har ghante mein piye. \n\nYou may also like :- Chawal ka paani (Maand) peene ke fayde. \n\n\n\n• Lahsun :- Rojana aapko khali pet subah lahsun ki 2-3 kacchi kaliyan khani chahiye. Saath hi khane mein lahsun ka istemal jaroor kare. Yeh pet ke infection ko door karne mein bahut hi faydemand hota hain. \n\n\n\n• Kela :- Kele mein natural sugar aur Potassium hota hain jo easily digest ho jata hain aur pet ko sahi rakhta hain. \n\n\n\n• Dahi :- Apni diet mein dahi ko jaroor shamil kare. Chahe toh aap Dahi mein Kela mix karke bhi kha sakte hain. Din mein 2 katori dahi khane se pet ke infection se aaram milta hain. \n\n\n\n• Laung :- Daily 1 ya 2 laung khana sehat ke liye faydemand hota hain. Laung pet aur Aanto mein hone wale chote-chote bacteria ko jad se khatm karne mein maahir hain. Yaha tak ki Laung khane se parasites ke saath saath unke eggs bhi khatm ho jate hain. \n\n\n\n• Nimbu :- Nimbu Acidic tatvo se bhara hota hain jo pet ke infection kodoor karne mein faydemand hota hain. Ek glass paani mein 2 chammach Nimbu ka ras mix kare aur khana khane se 30 minutes pahle ise pee le. Aisa tab tak kare jab tk pet ke infection se chhutkara nahi mil jata hain. \n\n\n\n• Haldi :- Pet ke bacteria ko maarne ke liye 1 chammach Haldi powder mein 6 chote chammach Shahad ko mix karke ek air tight jar mein rakh de. Phir ise din mein 2 baar aadha chammach khaye. Aisa karne se pet ka infection theek ho jata hain. \n\n\n\n• Adrak :- Pet ki problems se rahat paane ke liye aap adrak ka chota sa tukda Sendha namak aur Kali Mirch ke saath mix karke khaye. Ise mila kar kha lene ke baad upar se ek glass paani pee le. \n\n\n\n• Honey :- Honey body ki immunity ko boost karti hain. Shuddh shahad ko dalchini powder ke saath mix karke khane se pet ka infection door ho jata hain. \n\n\n\n• Hing :- Subah Khali pet Hing ko khane se pet ke keede mar jate hain, Isliye ise regularly khane mein shamil karna chahiye. \n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPosted by\n\n\nHinglishpedia\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nShare to:\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nNextNewer Post\nPreviousOlder Post\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPost a Comment\n\n\n\naprieztmkrdezign\n218168578325095\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nSubscribe to:\nPost Comments (Atom)\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nLoading...\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPopulars\nComments\nArchive\n\n\n\n\nFeature Post\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPopular\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nRukhe baalo ko Silky banane ke gharelu upay aur nuskhe (Tips for Dry Hair to make Silky)\nKya aap Rukhe baalo (Dry Hair) se preshan hain?
