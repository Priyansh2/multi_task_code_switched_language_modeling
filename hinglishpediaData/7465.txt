Saath hi Aam ki guthali se sharir se extra charbi aur Fat nikaal deti hain. \n\n6) Aam se sewan karne se aapko pachan se related bimariyo se bhi nijaat mil jati hain, Kyonki isme prayapt matra mein fiber paye jate hain.
