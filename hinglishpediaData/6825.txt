Janiye CTET examke baremein :-\nKab hota hain aayojit ? \n\n\nCTET exam saal mein 2 baar aayojit kiya jata hain. CBSE CTET offlinehi conduct karati hain. \n\n\n\n Kaun kar sakta hain CTET clear ?\nCentral TeacherEligibility Test mein 60% ya usse jyadamarks laane wale candidateshi clear kar sakte hain. \n\n\n\n\n Kya hain exam ?\nExam clear karne ka matlab naukri milna nahi hain. Iska matlab hain ki applicant Government school mein Teacher banne ke liye yogya (Eligible) ho gya hain. Result declare hone ke baad marks certificate ko 7 saalo tak valid mana jata hain. Yeh exam candidateschahe jitni baarde sakte hain. Ek baarqualify kar chuke candidatesapna score better karne ke liye dobara bhi yeh exam de sakte hain. \n\n\n\n \nKaha mil sakti hain naukri ? \n\nCTET seaap Kendriya Vidyalaya, Navodaya Vidyalaya, Sarvodaya Vidyalaya , Central Tibetan Schoolsaur sabhi GovernmentSchools mein padhane ke yogyaho jate hain. Yahi nahi acche private schools mein bhi CTET ki demand hain. \n\n\n \n\n Kaun de sakta hain yeh test ?\n\nIs exam mein vahi participate kar sakta hain, Jisne B.Ed kiya ho ya uske paas Diploma in alimentary education ho. \n\n\n \n\n Kaisa hota hain exam ?\n\nCTET exam mein pahle paper mein 5 section hote hain. Har section mein 30 question hote hain.
