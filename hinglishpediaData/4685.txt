Is fruits ke daane lal lal moti ke jaise chamakte hain. Anar ka ek naam Raktbeej bhi hain jiska matlab yeh hain ki yeh Khoon ko paida karne wale beej hain, Jaisa ki naam se hi clear hain ki Anar blood ko banane wala fruit hain. Anar ke niyamit sewan se aadmi ke bimar padne ki sambhavna kafi kam ho jati hain aur iske churan se bhi bimariya nazdeek nahi aati hain.\n\n\n\n\nAnar ke nuskhe :- \n\n\n\nIske lagatar istemal se hum kai saari bimariyo ko door bhaga sakte hain. \n\n\n\nPliha (Tilli) aur Liver ki kamjori, Pet dard Anar khane se theek ho jate hain. Iska Juice Acidity ko door karta hain. \n\n\n\nSharir mein khoon ki kami yani ki Anemia hone par Anar ka juice aur Muli ka Juice barabar matra mein mila kar piye. Isse aapko bahut hi jaldi fayda milta hain aur Anemia door ho jata hain. \n\n\n\nDast aur Pechish mein 15 Gram Anar ke sookhe Chhilke aur 2 Laung le. Dono ko ek glass paani mein ubaale. Phir jab Paani aadha rah jaye to din mein 3 baar le. Isse dast aur pechish mein aaram milta hain. \n\n\n\nDama, Khansi mein Jawakhar aadha tola, Kali Mirch ek tola, Peepal 2 tola, Anardana 4 Tola, In sabka churan bana le. Phir 8 tola Gud mein mila kar chutney bana le. 4-4 ratti ki goliya bana le. Garam paani ke saath subah, dophar, shaam ek-ek goli le. Is nuskhe se khansi khatm ho jati hain aur Dama mein aaram milta hain. Baccho ki khansi ko door karne ke liye Anar ke chhilko ka churan aadha-aadha chhota chammach Shahad ke saath bacche ko chataane se khansi door ho jati hain. \n\n\n\nYadi aapko der raat ki party se apach ho gya hain to Pakke Anaar ka Juice 1 chammach, Aadha Chammach Senka hua Jeera peeskar aur Gud mila kar din mein 3 baar le. \n\n\n\nDiarrhea mein Anar ke Juice ke saath Saunf, Dhaniya aur Jeera inko barabar matra mein mila kar churan banaye aur Anar ke juice ke saath is churan ko khaye. Iske alawa aap Diarrhea ko door karne ke liye Anar ke juice mein Pakka hua Kela mash karke kha sakte hain.\n\n\n\nKabz door karne ke liye Anar ke patto ko ubaal kar uska kadha bana kar piye, Jisse aapki kabz door ho jayegi.
