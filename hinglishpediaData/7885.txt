Aise breakfast se bhi aapko jaroori poshan milega.\n\n• Fruits aur Dry Fruits :- Isebhi aap raat ko taiyaar kar saktehain. Apne kuch pasandida phal jaise anjeer, cherry, berry, mosambi aadi ko le le. Subah aap ise nashteke roop mein le saktehain.\n\n• Salad :- Aap nashte mein protein ke saathvegetables ko bhi le sakte hain. Palak ki patti ke saath Anda aur chicken ko mila le.
