Log machhar ki wajah se kahi bhi sukoon se nahi baith pate hain aur raat ko jee bhar ke so bhi nahi pate hain. In bade dank wale machharo se kai bimariya bhi kafi tezi ke saath phailti hain. Laung ke tel ko Nariyal ke tel mein mila kar lagane se machhar nahi katate hain. \n\n\n\n\n\nSoyabean ka tel ;- Soybean oil ko khane ke liye bahut hi tezi ke saath istemal kiya jata hain, Lekin kya aapko pata hain hain isse machar bhi door bhagte hain. Soyabean ka tel lagane se machhar bilkul bhi nahi kat te hain. Yeh Tel bazaar mein kafi asaani ke saath Soyabean Oil ke naam se mil jata hain. \n\n\n\nNilgiri ka tel :- Nilgiri ka tel body par lagane se oos jagah par machhar nahi baithate hain. Iske alawa eucalyptus (Nilgiri) ki pattiyo ko jalane se bhi macchar bhagte hain.\n\n\nNeem ka tel :- Neem ke baare mein kaha jata hain ki Neem kai rogo ki nashak hain, Lekin bahut kam logo ko pata hoga ki Neem ka tel machchhar bhagane ke liye kafi asardar hain. Is baat ki pushti American Research of Council ne apni research mein ki hain. Neem ka tel lagane se machhar katna to door najdik bhi nahi aate hain.\n\n\nGende ka ped :- Gende ke phool do rang narangi aur peele rang ke hote hain halaki inki kai varieties hain. Gende ke ped ghar mein lagane se inki khusboo se machhar nahi aate hain.\n\n\nKali Mirch ka tel :- Kali mirch ka roj-marra ki zindagi ko sahaz banane mein kafi yogdan hain. Iska tel bhi kafi faydemand hota hain. Kali Mirch ka tel lagane se machhar paas tak nahi aate hain.\n\n\nLahsun ki kaliya :- Sabziyo ka swaad badhane ke liye kargar Lahsun machchhar ko bhi badi tezi ke saath bhagata hain. Lahsun ki kalian chabane se macchar nahi kat-te hain.\n\n\nAjwain ka powder ;- Ajwain bhi macchar bhagane ke liye ek asardayak nuskha hain. Ajwain ko khoob maheen sa pees le.
