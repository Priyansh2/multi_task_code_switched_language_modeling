Diabetes ke liye :- Laung ke tel mein khoon ki safai ke sath sath blood sugar level ko control mein rakhne ka gun paya jata hain. \n\n3. Infection mein :- Antiseptic guno ki wajah se yeh chot, jakhm, khujali aur infection aadi mein kafi faydemand saabit hota hain. Daant mein dard hone par laung ke tel ki kuch boonde rooi mein laga kar daant dard wali jagah par rakhne se daant dard mein aaram milta hain. \n\n5. Kaan ke dard ke liye :- Laung ke tel ko til ke tel ke saath mix karke kaano mein daalne se kaan ke dard se rahat milti hain. \n\n6.Skin ki dekhbhaal ke liye :- Laung ke tel ka istemal skin ke liye bahut hi faydemand hota hain. Iska istemal pimples ko door karne ke liye bhi kiya jata hain. \n\n7. Matli :- Laung ka tel ulti ya matli ke dauran kafi labhkari hota hain. Yeh pregnancy ke dauran hone wali matli mein faydemand hota hain. \n\n8. Tension ke liye :- Apne special guno ki wajah se yeh mental pressure aur thakaan ko kam karne ka kaam karta hain. Yeh aneendra aur mentally disorder jaise weak brain memory, Depression aur tension aadi ko khatm karne mein madad karta hain. \n\n9.Saans ki bimariyo mein :- Laung ke tel ka istemal Jukaam, Khansi, Asthma, T.B., Fefdo mein sujan aadibimariyo ke ilaj mein kiya jata hain.
