Isse pyaas bhi turant boojh jati hain. Vaise to thade-bulbule soda yukt drinks kuch samay ke rahat to jaroor de sakte hain, Lekin sharir par inke Bad Effects padne ka pata baad mein lagta hain. Aise mein agar aap Garmiyon mein chilchilati dhoop se preshan hain to santre ka sewan kare. Isse aapke sharir mein paani ki kami door hogi, Aap infection se bhi bache rahenge aur jahir hain ki santra khane ka koi side-effect bhi nahi hain.  Aap ise Juice kitarah le sakte hain ya chhilka utaar ka kha sakte hain. Agar aap apne routine mein healthy fruits ka juice shamil kare to dhoop se hone wali bimariyo se aap bach sakte hain. Aaj hum santre ke guno ke baare mein charcha kar rahe hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n\nMakkhi machchharo se bachata hain \n\n\n\nAadiwasi santre ke sukhe chhilko ya uske powder ko jalte huye koyale par dalkar pure ghar mein dhooan karte hain. Aisa karne se Makkhi, Machchhar aur khatmal aadi bhag jate hain. \n\n \n\n \n\nSantra ke jabardast energy dene wala phal hain. Santre ka vanaspatik naam Citrus reticulata hain. Santre mein Glucose aur dextol jaise tatv bharpoor hote hain, Jo sharirik shakti ke liye jaroori hain. Santre ke ras mein Vitamin C, Vitamin B complex, Vitamin Aur tarah ke minerals paye jate hain. \n\n\n\n\n\nTavcha ki safaai karta hain\n\n\n\nRaat ko sone se pahle Santre ke 2 chammach ras mein 2 chammach shahad mil kar 20 minutes tak chehre par lagaye. Baad mein saaf rui se doodh mein dubokar chehre ko saaf karna chahiye. Rojana aise karne se kuch hi dino mein fark dikhai dene lagega.\n\n \n\n \n\n\n\nLooh se bachata hain. \n\n\n\nGarmiyo mein looh lagne par din mein 4 baar santre ka ras (Orange Juice) peena bahut hi faydemand hain. Ghamauriyo ke ilaj ke liye aadiwasi Santre ke chhilko ko sukhakar powder bana lete hain aur isme thoda sa tulsi ka paani aur gulabjal mila kar sharir par lagate hain. Aisa karte hi turant aaraam mil jata hain. \n\n\nBawasir mein aaram\n\nBhojan karne ke baad yadi 1/2 glass Orange Juice roj piya jaye to pet ke ulcer theek ho jate hain. Chhav mein sukhaye huye santre ke chhilko ko pees le aur ghee ke saath barabar matra mein milaye. Ise 1-1 Chammach din mein 3 baar peene se bawaseer mein aaram milta hain. \n\n\n \n Apach door karne ke liye\n\nSantre ke ras ko garm karke usme kala namak aur sonth powder mila le. Aadiwasiyo ke anusaar aisa karne se apachan ki samsya door ho jati hain. \n\n\n \nJuto se aane wali durgandh door hoti hain\n\nAisa har baar hota hain ki aap jab bhi santra khate hain, iske chhilko ko asaani ke saath kachra peti mein daal dete hain. Santre ke chhiko ko raat ko badboo maar rahe juto ke ander rakh de. Agli subah in chhilko ko juto mein se nikal kar phenk de. Juto se durgandh door ho jayegi. \n\n\n \n \n \nPairo ki badboo se nijat\n\nJinke pairo ke talve se badboo aati hain, Usay door rakhne ke liye santre ke chhilke behad faydemand hain. Santre ke taze ya sukhe huye chhilko ko 2 Ltr. gungune paani mein ubalkar isme pairo ko doobo kar rakhna chahiye. Kareeb 2 se 4 minutes baad pairo ko paani se nikalkar towel se saaf kar liya jaye. Aisa hafte mein 2-3 baar karne se jald hi problem solve ho jati hain. \n\n\n\n \nSkin ko glow karne ke liye \n\nKoi bajaaroo cream aapko gori nahi banayegi, Lekin yeh daawaa hain ki is desi nuskhe ko ajmaakar aap apni tavcha mein nikhar jaroor pa sakti hain. Santre ke  chhilko ko sukhne ke liye rakh de. Jab yeh puri tarah se sukh jaye, to inhe pees kar powder bana le. Is powder ki 3 gram matra le aur ise 3 chammach dahi mein ghol le. Ise chehre par lagakar sukhne de. Kareeb 15 se 20 minutes ke baad jab yeh sukh jaye, to hatheli se halka-halka ragadte huye ise utaar le aur baad mein paani se chehre ko saaf kar le. Tauliye se saaf kar lene ke baad thoda moisturizing Cream laga le. Roj nahane se pahle 15 dino tak aisa kare.
