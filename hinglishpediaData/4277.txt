Lekin phir dubara aur baar-baarghaas phir ug aati hain. Aise mein aapko kuch aise tarike apnane chahiye, Jo grass ko jad se hi khatm kare. Grass ke roots bahut hi strong hote hain, Jinhe khinch kar nikaalne par bhi yeh jaldi se khatm nahi hote hain aur phir se ugg jate hain. \n\nLekin agar aap kuch gharelu nuskhe aur upay apnayenge to in ghaas ka safaya kiya ja sakta hain, Nahi to yeh ghaas aapke garden ke paudho ko nuksaan pahucha sakte hain. Aaj hum aapko Garden se ghaas ka safaya karne ya khatm karne ke liye natural tarike ke baare mein bata rahe hain. \n\n\nGarden se ghaas hatane ke 9 natural tarike :- \n\n1. Sharab :- Ghaas ko khatm karne ke liye oos par Alcohol ka chhidkaaw karde, Vah puri tarah se jhulas jayegi aur dubar se nahi ugg payegi. \n\n\n2. Garm paani :- Paani ko acchi tarah se khaula kar ghaas par daale. Isse aapke lone ki ghaas jhulas kar saaf ho jayegi. \n\n\n\n3. Baking Soda :- Ghaas ke upar baking soda daalne se ghaas jhulas kar khatm ho jati hain aur mitti ko bhi koi nuksaan nahi pahuchta hain. Garmiyo ke dino mein ugne wali ghaas par aap hamesha baking soda ka istemal karke isse chhutkara paye. \n\n\n4. Zameen ko dhakne wale paudhe lagaye:- Ground cover plant, mitti ko upjaao banaye rakhte hain aur ghaas ko bhi hata dete hain. Yeh ghaas ki jad ko kaat dete hain. \n\n\n\n5. Namak :- Agar kisi paudhe ke aas-paas ghaas ug aati hain to usay hatane ke liye uski jado mein namak daal de, jisse ghaas ki jade gal jayenge aurghaas apne aap hat jayegi. \n\n\n6. Newspaper :- Aapko kuch ajeeb sa lage, lekin yah baat sahi hain ki agar aap garden se ghaas ko remove karna chahte hain to akhbaar ko fold karke moti si layer bana kar ghaas ke upar rakh de, isse usko suraj ki roshini nahi milegi aur vah sookh jayegi. \n\n\n7. Sirka :- Agar aap gharelu Sirke ka istemal ghaas ko khatm karne ke liye karenge to yeh ghaas ko jad se khatm kar deta hain. Iske liye ek jug paani mein ek cup sirka daale aur ghaas ko ukhaadne ke baad daal de. Iske baad ghaas nahi nikalegi. Aisa 2 baar vaha par karne se kharpatvaar se bhi mukti mil jati hain. \n\n\n8. Corn Gluten meal :- Kya aapko apne garden se hamesha ke liye ghaas nikaalna hain toh aap corn gluten ka istemal kare. Yeh lone ko kharpatvaar free bana deta hain. \n\n\n9. Bleach :- Bleach ka istemal aap ghaas ko hatane ke liye bhi kar sakte hain.
