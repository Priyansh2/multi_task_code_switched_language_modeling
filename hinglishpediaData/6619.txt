Jismein paani ki matra dheere-dheere cells ke dwara bahar nikalne lagti hain aur bahar se body ko paani ki utni matra nahi mil pati hain, Jitni usey jaroorat hoti hain. Jyadatar garmiyon ke mausam mein yeh samsya dekhi jati hain. Aur iski andekhi kai tarah ki bimariyon ko paida karti hain. Hamare sharir mein 70% paani ki matra hoti hain. Isliye din mein kam se kam 8-10 glass paani peene ki salah di jati hain. Dehydration ke chalte paseena aana, gastrointestinal, Bukhar, Looh lagna jaisi problem hone lagti hain. Sharir mein paani ki kami se kidney par bhi asar padta hain aur pet se related kai prakar ki bimariya hone lagti hain. \n\n\n\n\n\nBado mein Dehydration hone ke karan\n\n\n\n\n\nBukhaar, Dhoop ke prabhav aur jaroorat se jyada exercise karne se.\n\nUlti, dast aur urine ke infection ke karan , khane aur paani peene ke matra mein balance na hona. \n\nSkin Infection, Bimari aur moonh ki kisi bhi tarah ki bimari hone par dehydration ki problem ho sakti hain. \n\n\n\n\n\nBado mein Dehydration ke lakshan\n\n\n\n1.
