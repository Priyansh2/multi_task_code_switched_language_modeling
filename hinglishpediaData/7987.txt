Rojana sirf 1 Anaar aapki sehat ko bilkul durust banaye rakh sakta hain. Isme Vitamin A, C aur E ke saath Folic Acid bhi hota hain. Isme Anti-Oxidants aur Anti-viral tatv hote hain. Anar mein Anti-oxidant hone ki wajah se Free Radicals (Suraj ki roshini se aur Pollution se hone wale radicals) se bachata hain. Yeh radicals skin mein dheelapan late hain, jiski wajah se vyakti samay se pahle budha dikhai dene lagta hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n2. Yeh haddiyon ko majboot karne, Blood circulation ko sahi rakhne aur Wajan kam karne mein bhi bahut labhkari hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n3. Anar khane se khoon ka pravah theek rahta hain. Iske saath hi yeh Heart attack aur heart stroke ko bhi theek kar deta hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n4. Anar mein Anti-Oxidants kafi hone ke karan Yeh kharab cholesterol ko shuruvati avastha mein hi rok deta hain. Is prakar Dil ki dhamniyo mein khoon ke thakke nahi bante hain. Anar khoon ko patla banane ki khasiyat rakhta hain. Isse khoon ke thakke nahi bante hain. \n\n\n5. Anar ka juice adhik umar mein hone wale Alzheimer bimari se bachata hain.\n\n\n6. Khoon ki kami ko door karne ke liye Anaar ka juice sabse achha mana jata hain.\n\n\n7.  Anar skin ke liye bahut hi accha hota hain, Yeh skin ko mulayam aur glowing banata hain.\n\n\n8. Yeh pregnant woman ke liye bhi bahut badhiya hota hain. Ise khane se baby healthy paida hota hain.\n\n\n9. Anar khane se daant bhi majboot hote hain, aur unme chamak aa jati hain.\n\n\n10.
