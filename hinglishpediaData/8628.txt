Tension ko door karti hain. \n\n\n\nChocolate mein mood theek karne aur tension (tanav) ko door karne wala gun paya  jata hain. Jab bhi aapka mood kharab ho to chocolate khaye. Aapko Good Feel hoga.\n\n\n\n\n\n2. Mansik bimariyon mein.\n\n\n\n\n\nChocolate mein Flavonol hota hain. Flavonol khoon sanchar behtar tarike se karta hain. Isliye Chocolate khane se mansik rogo mein labh hota hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n3. Dard bhi bhagati hain chocolate\n\n\n\n\n\nChocolate khane se Sharir mein   Andofijin Harmon paida karti hain. Isliye chocolate khane se dard kam hota hain. Yeh ek Natural Pain killer ki tarah kaam karti hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n4. Diabetes mein bhi .\n\n\n\n\n\nDark Chocolate mein maujood Flavonoids aur Glycemic rasayan diabetes ko control karne mein help karte hain. Dil ke liye faydemand\n\n\n\nChocolate mein copper aur potassium paya jata hain. Isliye yeh dil se judi bimariyon ko door rakhti hain. Chocolate blood circulation ko behtar banati hain. Saath hi iska niymit sewan se cholesterol bhi hamesha control mein rahta hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n6. Blood pressure mein \n\n\n\n\n\nChocolate khane se Blood pressure hamesha control mein rahta hain. Dark Chocolate mein badi matra mein Flanovoids aur Anti-oxidant  hote hain. Chocolate ki thodi-thodi matra hafte mein 2-3 khane se blood pressure ko control karne mein madad milti hain. \n\n\n\n7. Khansi mein kare yeh upay\n\n\n\n\n\nKya aap jante hain, ki chocolate khansi mein faydemand hoti hain. London ke Health researchers ne Cocoa mein milne wali theobromine tatv se bharpoor aisi chocolate taiyaar ki hain. Jise kha kar khansi se chhutkara paya ja sakta hain.\n\n\n\n\n\n \n\n \n\n8.
