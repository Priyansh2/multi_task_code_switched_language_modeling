Isse bhi jyada Talcosis ya fefdo meinjalan bhi paida hoti hain.\n\n3.\tTalcum powder zehareela hain :- Talcum powder mein tall naam ka mineral hota hain. Yeh nuksaandeh hain jab iski matra saans ke jariyeanderchali jatihain. Yeh anti-septic, baby powder aur talcum powder mein istemal hone wala khas mineral hain aur yeh sadakchhap heroine hain.\n\n4.\tFefdo ka cancer :- Jo log jyada powder istemal kartehain, veh saans ke jariye iske kano ko ander le jate hain. Jisse fefdo serelated problems paida hoti hain aur aage chalkar yeh fefdo ke cancermein bhi badal sakti hain. Isliye jyadatar log cancer ki roktham ke liye cornstarch yukt body powder istemal karne ki salah dete hain.\n\n5.\tGarbhashya ki jalan :- Bahut si ladies ise khusboodaar beautyproduct ke roop mein iska istemal karti hain. Veh vaginapar bhi iska istemal karti hain. Jiske karan iske kan garbhashya mein enter kar jatehain. Yeh kan pahle felopene tube mein jatehain, phir garbhashya tak pahuch jate hain. Yeh kan asaani se nahi nikalte hain aur lambe waqt tak rahtehain. Isse grabhashya mein jalan aur cancer jaisi gambhir bimariya bhi ho sakti hain.\n\n6.\tGarbhashya cancer :- Cancer Prevention coition ka manana hain ki is tarah ke beautyproducts ke istemal se garbhashya ka cancer ka khatrabadh jata hain. Har 5 mein se 1 mahila inka istemalkarti hain. Powdermahila ke yaun ang dwara grabhashya tak pahuchtahain. Vagina (yoni) mein powder ka istemalkarne wali mahilaon mein garbhashya ka cancer hone ka khatra powder na istemal karne wali mahilaon ke mukable kanhi jyada hota hain.\n\n7.\tSaans se sambandhit bimariya :- Talcum powder se saans se related problemsshuru ho jati hain, khastaur par shishuon mein.
