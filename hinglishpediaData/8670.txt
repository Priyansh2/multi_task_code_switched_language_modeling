Saath hi isse bana butter bhi bahut hi swadisth hota hain aur usme kai fayde bhi chhupe hote hain. Garmiyo se lekar sardiyo tak milne wali moongfali ke kheti jyadatar South America aur Mexico mein hoti hain. Lekin Bharat mein bhi iski paidwaar bahut jyada matra mein hoti hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n\nMoongfali ke per 100 Gram 166 calorie, 7.8 gram protein, 17.1 ml calcium, 203 ml Potassium, 49.3 ml Magnesium, 111ml phosphorus, 89.6 ml Sodium, 4.3 Gram carbohydrate, 2.6 gram Fiber aur 14.7ml Fat maujood hota hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n1. Healthy Heart ke liye :-  Moongfali mein monosaturated aur polysaturated fat paya jata hain. Jo healthy heart ke liye bahut hi jaroori hain. In dono ki balance matra cholesterol level ko control karti hain. Yeh kharab cholesterol ko kam karke sharir ke liye jaroori cholesterol ke production ko badhati hain. Isse dil ki kai prakar ki bimariyo se bacha ja sakta hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n2. Protein ka khazana :- Cells ke banane mein protein ki sabse jyada jaroorat hoti hain. Protein naye cells ke banane aur purane cells ko theek karne ka kaam karta hain. Moongfali mein Protein  ki sabse jyada matra payi jati hain. Isliye rojana kuch matra mein moongfali ka sewan karna bachhon ke saath saath bado ke liye bhi bahut hi faydemand hota hain. Vegetarians bhi Moongfali kha kar apne sharir mein protein ki kami ko pura kar sakte hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n3. Anti Oxidant se bharpoor :- polyphenols ek bahut hi accha anti-oxidant hota hain. Jo moongfali mein bharpoor matra mein paya jata hain. Iske alawa isme p coumaric acid aur oleic acid bhi paye jate hain. Jo na kewal healthy heart ke liye jaroori hain, balki Free Radicals ko door karke kai prakar ke infection se bachate hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n4. Minerals ka khazana :- Moongfali mein Magnesium, phosphorus, Zinc, potassium, Calcium aur Sodium sabhi prakar ke minerals paye jate hain. Jo body ko active rakhne ke liye jaroori hain. Sharir mein in sabhi minerals ki maujoodagi pachan kriya ke saath hi liver aur heart ko sahi rakhti hain. Isse kai prakar ki bimariyo se bacha ja sakta hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n5. Vitamins ka source :- Sharir ke sahi vikas ke liye protein ki sabse jyada jaroorat hoti hain. Iske saath hi Vitamins cells aur tissue ke ban ne mein bhi utne hi jaroori hote hain. Sharir ke baaki Ango ke sahi function ke liye bhi yeh jimmedar hain. Metabolism ka level sahi rakhe ke saath Fat aur carbohydrate ko energy mein badalne ka kaam vitamins hi karte hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n6. Folate bhi jaroori :- Pregnant women mein uchit matra mein Folate ka hona, Unhe shishu ke janm ke samay mein hone wali kai prakar ki samsya se chutkara dilati hain. Pre-Mature Birth ki sambhavna ko bhi ise kha kar kafi had tak kam kiya ja sakta hain, Kyonki Moongfali mein folate ki matra bahut jyada payi jati hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n7. Dimaag ki shakti badhata hain :- Moongfali mein vitamin B3 paya jata hain. Jo brain ke liye bahut hi jaroori hota hain. Ise brain food ke taur par jana jata hain. Isme mauzood Niacin tatv dimaag ke kaam karne ki power ko badhata hain. Jisse bhoolne ki bimari, depression, tension aadi ki problem door hoti hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n8. Cancer se bachav :- Mahilaon mein jyadatar hone wale colon cancer ko bhi moongfali kha kar door kiya ja sakta hain. Hafte mein do baar moongfali se bane butter ka istemal kar mahilaon mein 58% aur purusho mein 27% colon cancer ki sambhavna ko kam kiya ja sakta hain. \n\n\n\n\n\nMoongfali se hone wale Nuksaan :- \n\n\n\n\n\nAllergy :-  Moongfali ki jyada matra se allergy bhi ho sakti hain, jo kai baar maut ka karan ban jati hain. Sensitive Skin ke liye bhi moongfali bahut ki ghatak hoti hain. Moonh mein khujali , chehre aur gale mein sujan aadi iske allergy ke hi result hain. Aur to aur kai baar saans lene mein preshani, Asthma attack bhi ho sakta hain.
