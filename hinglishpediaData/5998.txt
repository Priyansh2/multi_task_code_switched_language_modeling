Agar Pudine ki chutney saath ho to kya baat hain. Samosa, Kachauri jaise tali-bhuni cheezo se jitna aap door rahenge , Utna hi accha hain. \n\n \n\nBazaar mein Chiwda, Poha samet bahut se oil free options mauzood hain, Unhe nashte ke liye istemal mein laya ja sakta hain. \n\n\n\n\n\nPhalo se rakhe dosti \n\n\n\nTarbooz mein 90% tak Paani to hota hi hain, Saath mein dher saara Potassium aur Fiber bhi hota hain. Phalo ka raja Aam, Kela sharir ko thandak pahuchane mein bhi sahayak hote hain. Seb, Santra, Mosambi, Malta Vitamin C se bharpoor hain, Jo sharir ki immunity ko badhane ke saath hi Anti-Aging ki problem se bhi door rakhte hain.\n\n\n \n\n \n\nOil Free bhojan \n\n\n\nBreakfast mein parathe aadi se parhez kare. Protein aur Potassium ka accha source hain. Ulcer mein bhi kargar hain. \n\n\n\n\n\nChhachh - Kala namak aur Peesa hua bhuna Jeera mila kar Chhachh peene se body ke liye jaroori proteins ki purti ho jati hain. \n\n\n\nAam Panna - Garmiyo ke liye sabse khas drink aur saath hi vitamin ka sabse accha khazana hain, Jo looh se bachane ke saath hi bahut hi healthy hota hain. \n\n\n\n\n\nVegetables Juice - Ghiya, Kheera, Amla, Tamatar aadi ko Juice banakar pee sakte hain. Ha, Nariyal, Soyabean aur Canola aadi ke tel  ka istemal kiya ja sakta hain. \n\n\n\n\n\nJunk Food -Burger, Pizza, Tandoori Chicken jaise junk food ko garmiyo mein bye kahna hi behtar hain.
