Benefits& Side effects of Pickle in Hindi/Urdu. \nKhana khane ke saath achar ka sewan na kewal India mein balki puriduniya mein hota hain, Isse health ko kai saare fayde hote hain, Lekin iska zyada sewan karna bhi health ke liye accha nahi hain, Kyonki yeh fayda dene ki bajaye tab nuksan dene lagta hain. \nSirf Bharat hi nahi duniya bhar mein khane ke saath aachaar ka sewan kiya jata hain. Agar India ki hi baat ki jaye toh yeh hamare bhojan ka ahem hissa hain. Hamare yaha kai tarah ke phalo aur sabziyo mein Telaur masale mila kar achaar banaya jata hain. China, Japan, Korea mein Sabziyo ke saath Ande, Masale aur Soya Sauce mila kar Pickle banaya jata hain. Jabki Western Countries mein Sabziyo aur phalo ko Sirke mein doobo kar achaar banaya jata hain. Yani ki duniya bhar mein khane ke saath iska sewan kiya jata hain. Phalo aur Sabziyo ke saath masale aur oil wala achaar khane ke bahut fayde hote hain, Lekin isse hone wale nuksaan ko bhi nazarandaaznahi kiya ja sakta hain. \nAachaar khane ke fayde :-\n• Free Radicals se bachaye :- Achar banane ke liye fruits aur vegetables ka istemal hota hain, Iske liye phalo aur sabziyo ko bina pakaye huye istemal kiya jata hain. Jisse phaloaur sabziyo mein mauzood nutrientskhatm nahi hote hain. Yeh Anti-Oxidantsse bharpoor hota hain. YehAnti-Oxidantsachar ke jariyebody mein pahuchate hain aur mukt kano yani ki Free Radicals se sharir ko bachate hain. Jo kai tarah ki bimariyo ki wajah hain Free Radicals.\n\n\n• Pachan kriya sudhaare ;- Achar khane se pachan kriya durust rahti hain. Darasal pet mein good aur bad dono tarahke bacteria hote hain, jab aap kisi antibiotic medicine ka sewan karte hain, tab good bacteria bhimar jate hain.Isse digestion system par effect padta hain aur khana asaani se pachta nahi hain. Jabki achaar mein namak hota hain, joProbiotics ke vikas mein helpful hain. Isse digestion mein sudhaar aata hain. \n\n• Liver ko majboot banaye: - Amle se bane achar liver ke liye faydemandhain, Amle mein paya jane wale guno ki wajah se Liver majboot banta hain. Amla Ulcerjaisi bimari se bachata hain. Saath hi yeh pachan kriya ko bhi sudhaarta hain. Isliye Amle ka achaar ka sewan jaroor karna chahiye. \n\n• Dimaagke liye faydemand :- Aachaar pet ke sath-sathdimaag ke liye bhi faydemand hain. Achaar mein paya jane wala Probioticsbacteria dimaag ke liye bhi faydemand hain. Yeh baatresearches mein saabit ho chuki hain ki kendriyatantrikapranaliaurdimaagke beech gahra relationhota hain , agarpet sahi toh dimaag bhi sahi tarah se kaam karta hain. \n\n• Baccho ke liye faydemand :- Baccho ko khatte achaar ka taste bahut pasandaata hain. Achaar baccho ke liyefaydemand hain, Agar bacchaacharkhaneke liyekahe toh usay mana nahi karna chahiye. Achar ko puribody keliye faydemandmanajata hain. Yeh baccho ki prati-raksha pranali ko majboot karke unhe bimariyo se bachata hain. Achar khane se baccho ko cold aur flu nahi hota hain. Garbhvati mahilaon ke liye bhi yeh faydemand hain. \n\nAachaar hamarebhojan ko swadist banata hain. Yeh bhartiya rasoi ka ahem hissa hain. Bhojan ke saath Achaarkhane se khane ka tastebadal jata hain. Achaar kai tarah ke hote hain Jaise Nimbu ka achaar, Aam ka achar, Amla ka achar, Gajar ka achar aur bhi kai tarah ke acharhote hain. Bharat mein aam taur par bahut zyada tel aur masalo ke saath acharbanaya jata hain. \n\n\n\nAchar ko lambe waqt tak sahi rakhne ke liyeisme jyada matra mein namak, tel aur sirke ka istemal kiya jata hain. Yeh sabhi sehat ke liye nuksandeh hote hain.Bhale hi inhelambe waqt mein kam matra mein hi khaya jata ho. Achar sehat ke liye vahi accha hain jismekam tel aur paani ki matra zyada ho, Lekin aise achar jyada der tak sahi nahi rahte hain aur jaldi hi kharab ho jate hain, Lekin sehat ke liye yeh behatarin hote hain. Toh kya sach mein achar khana bura hain, yeh janne ke liye achar khane ke nuksan jaroorpadhe.  \n\nAchar khane ke nuksan (Side effects ofPickle in Hindi/Urdu.) :-\n1. Jyada Tel hota hain :- Achar banate waqt jyada tel aur masalo ka istemal kiya jata hain. Tel ke jyada istemal se karne se body mein cholesterol ki matra badhti hain, iski wajah se wajan badhneki problem ho sakti hain. Iske karan heartse related problems bhi ho sakti hain. \n\n2.Jyada namak :- Achar faydemand honeke sath nuksandeh bhi hain. Achar mein namak ki matra jyada hoti hain, jiski wajah se yeh health par buraasar daalta hain. Bazaar mein milane wale achaar toh aurbhi zyada nuksaandeh hote hain, Kyonki unme Sodium ke saath chemical bhi hota hain. Isme paya jane wala Sodium benzoate ko jyada khane se cancer ki samsya ho sakti hain . \n\n3. Pachan samsya :- Bahut zyada matra mein achaarkhane se, achaar ka ras pachan samsyaon ki wajah ban jata sakta hain. Pet ki preshani, pet dard aur pet phoolna bahut zyada achar khane ke side-effects hain. Yeh diarrhoea ke karan pachan tantra ki parat ko prabhavit karta hain. Ek research mein yehbaat saamne aayihain ki achaar jyada khane wale logo ko gastric ki problem ho sakti hain. \n\n4. Cancer ka khatra :- Achar ke jyadasewan se cancer jaisi janlewa bimari ho sakti hain. Ek research mein yeh baat samne aayi hain ki jo log achar ka zyada istemal karte hain, unhe gastric cancer ki problem jyada hoti hain. Iskealawa iske jyada sewanse gale mein kharash aur dard ki bhi shikayat rahti hain. Yeh esophagealcancer ka karan bhi ban sakta hain. \n\n5. Ulcer :- Achar mein bahut zyada masale ki wajah se ulcer ki samsya ho sakti hain. Niyamit roop se achar ka sewan karne wale logo mein yeh problem dekhne ko milti hain. Isliye jitna ho sakekam acharkhana chahiye, Ghar ka bana achar khana chahiye aurachar ko banate waqt usmedher saare masale nahi milana chahiye. \n\n6. Diabetes :- Ji haan Diabetes ke mareezo ke liyeachar khana accha nahihota hain. Achar mein preservatives ke roop mein Chini ke saathbhi banaya jata hain. Isliye diabetes se peedit logo ke liye yeh sahi nahi hain. Chini se bane achar ka sewan bilkul nahi karna chahiye. \n\n7. High blood pressure :- High blood pressure , jyada matra mein namak ka sewan karne ki wajah se hota hain. Isliyehigh bloodpressure ke mareezo ko achar se doori banaye rakhni chahiye. \n\n8. Triglyceride ka high level :-Aachar mein mauzoodsabhi tarah ke preservativeski wajah se yeh health ke liye bura hota hain. Preservatives mein aam-taurpar telki matra jyada hotihain. Halaki tel ki wajah se achar zyada der tak tikta hain. Lekin Iski mauzoodagi se sharir mein Triglyceride ke level mein badhotarihoti hain. Achar mein kisi bhi tarah ke tel ke istemal se yehproblem hoti hain. \n\n9. Sharir mein Sujan :- Achar mein preservatives ke istemal ki wajah se namak ki matra zyada hoti hain. JisseismeSodium bahut zyada hota hain. Sodium ki wajah se sharir Paani ki matra ko banayerakhne ka reaction karta hain jo sharir ke osmotic balance ke liye jaroori hain. Isse sharir mein sujan ki samsya hoti hain.
