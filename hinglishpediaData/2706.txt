Agar kisi ko Allergy, Asthma ya Sukhi hawa se problem hoti hain toh usay cough ki samsya kafi jyada hogi. \n\n\n\nTurant rahat paane ke liye Allopathic upchar sahi hota hain, Lekin cough ek aisi problem hain jo kafi dino tak chalti hain. Isse cough asaani se nikal jayega. \n\n\n\nSpice Tea :- Chai ko Tulsi, Kali Mirch aur kai tez aur garam masale daal kar pakaye aur garma-garam pee jaye, Isse rahat milegi.\n\n\n\nMedicine wali Toffee :- Yeh ek accha gharelu nuskha hain, Jisse aapko koi side-effect bhi nahi hoga. Ise choosne se jakadan door hogi aur gale ko rahat milegi. Ek chammach Brandi lene se fayda hota hain. \n\n\n\nAmla :- Sardi ko door bhagane ke liye sabse accha tarika hain Amla. Ise aap dhoop mein baithkar khaye. \n\n\n\nDry Air se bache :- Sardiyo ke dino mein sukhi hawa chalti hain jo cough ki samsya hone par aur bhi dukhdayi hoti hain. Isliye aap aisi jagah par baithe jaha aapko thodi nami mile aur sookhi hawa se bachaav ho. \n\n\n\nHydrate kare :- Agar aapke sharir mein paani ki kami rahti hain toh cough sharir se nikalne mein problem paida karega. Cough ki samsya hone par garam paani piye.\n\n\n\nGaram paani se nahaye :- Agar gale mein cough bahut jyada hain aur aapko jakadan bhi mahsoos hoti hain toh Garam paani shower le le. \n\n\n\nNozzle spray :- Agar aapko bahut jyada samsya ho rahi hain to nozzle spray ka istemal kare. Isse aapko turant rahat milegi. \n\n\n\nMasala chaay :- Cough ki samsya hone par masala chaay ka sewan karne se aaram milta hain. \n\n\n\nSaline Garlic :- Yeh cough ko door karne ki prachin vidhi hain.
