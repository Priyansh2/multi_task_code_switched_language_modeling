Mood ko badalne ke liye kaun si cheezo ko khana chahiye? Mood ko sahi rakhne ke liye kya khana chahiye? Happy hone ke liye kya khaye? Mood ko sahi karne wale food kya hain? In swaalo ka jawaab janne ke liye yeh post jaroor padhe.\nAgar aapka mood kharab hain ya phir aapka dimaagkuch sust aur slow sa ho raha ho toh aur aap chah kar bhi usay theek nahi kar pa rahe hain toh khane ki kuch cheeze aapke mood ko khushnuma bana saktihain. Kuch researches ke mutabik Serotonin, Endorphin, Dopamine aur dusre Neurotransmitter Hormones hote hain jo Dil aur dimaag ko accha aur khush rakhte hain. Isliye agar aapka mood kharab ho gya ho toh in cheezo ko jaroor khaye, Inse bigda mood bhi sudhar jata hain. Happy rakhne ke liye yeh cheeze jaroor khaye. Aap ekdum fresh feelkarenge toh jante hain kuch aise foods ke bare mein jo aapka mood badal sakte hain. .\n1. Agar aapka dimaag soost hain toh black tea, green tea ya orange lemon tea lene se mood ekdum change ho jata hain. Ji haan Journal of Nutrition ki ek research ke mutabik is tarah ki chai mein ek tarah ka amino acid paya jata hain. Jo bigde mood ko khushnuma aur soost dimaag ko ekdum sharp bana deta hain. .\n2. Strawberry Potassium ka bahut accha source hain, Jo ki tantrikaonko uttejit karne mein madad karta hain. Yeh vitamins ka bhi bahut accha source hain. Ise khane sehamara mood accha hota hain. .\n3. Agar aapka mood kharab hain to, thoda sa Shahad kha le. Darasal, Shahad se niyamit sugarki matra milti rahtihain. Isme Kaempferol aurQuercetinaadi paye jate hain, Jo ki mind ko stress freerakhne ka kaam karte hain. .\n\n4. Jyada matra meinProtein lene se bhi stresskam hota hain. Isliye jab aap jyada tension aur stress feel kare toh Macchli, Anda aur doodh se bani cheezo ka sewan kare. Isse sharir ko protein aur amino acid milte hain. Tyrosineek special kism ka amino acid hain jo Dopamine,Norepinephrineaur Epinephrine aadi Neurotransmitter ke production ko badhata hain. In rasayano se sharirik alertness aur energy badhne mein madad milti hain. Isliye agar aap accha feel karna chahte hain toh protein se bhare khadya padarth, Jaise Macchali, Anda, Meat, Phaliya, Paneer, Doodh aur Dahi aadi ka sewan jaroor kare. .\n\n\n5. Carbohydrates se bharpoor food ko khane se kharab mood sahi ho jata hain. Carbohydrate wale food Serotonin ka production badhate hain. Jo ki body ke liye feel – good hormone hain. Research se pata chala hain ki jin depression ke mareezo ne carbohydrate ka lagatar sewan kiya, 1-2 hafte mein hi unka depressionkam hua. .\n6. Jab aap stress mein ho toh nariyal jaroor khaye. Nariyal khane se heartbeat turant control mein aa jati hain. Colombia University dwara kiye gye research mein yeh paya gya ki agar kisi ka blood pressure badha hua hain aur veh nariyal soonghta hain toh uska blood pressure bahut tezi se control mein aata hain. Darasal Nariyal mein medium size triglycerides hote hain. Isme fat ki special quantityhoti hain jo mood ko accha karte hain aur dimaag ke liye healthy bhi hain. .\n\n\n7.Kele ko ek poshak tatvo se bharpoor phal mana jata hain. Isme kai tarah ke poshak tatv bharpoormatra mein hote hain. Potassium aur Magnesium kafi matra mein paye jate hain. Keleke andernatural sugar jab aapke blood mein milti hain toh isse aapmeenergy ka circulation hota hain. Kele mein Starch wala Carbohydrate hota hain jo aapke mood ko accha banaye rakhne mein madad karta hain..\n8. Dark Chocolate khane se dimaagstress free hota hain. Kai researches se yeh baat saabitho chuki hain. Saath hi Chocolate mein Anti-Oxidants paye jate hain, Jo aapke mood ko hamesha accha banayerakhti hain. .\n9. Tamatar sirf tasty aur swaad badhane wala nahi hotahain. Yeh dimaag ke liye accha hain. Isme Lycopene naam ka Anti-Oxidant paya jata hain jo dimaag ki sujan ko door karta hain.
