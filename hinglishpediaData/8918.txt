Kai prakar ke rango aur size wala kharbooja , Kheera aur kakdi ki tarah hota hain. \n\n\n\nIski paidawaar India, Iran aur Africa mein jyada hoti hain. Khas taur par garmiyo ke mausam mein April se lekar August tak. \n\n\n\n\nMithe swaad ke saath Garmiyo mein kharbooje ka sewan Sharir ko thandak pahuchata hain. Saath hi ismein mauzood paani, Vitamins aur Minerals sharir ko poshak tatvo ki kami ko pura karte hain. \n\n\n\n\n\nKharbooze ke fayde \n\n\n\nGarmiyo ke mausam mein aksar sharir mein paani ki kami ho jati hain, Jise Kharbooza kha kar kafi had tak kam kiya ja sakta hain. Saath hi yeh Looh se bhi bachata hain. Kharbooja khakar aapko kitne fayde ho sakte hain , Aaiye jante hain.\n\n\n \n\n \n\n 1. Pachan Kriya mein hone wale kharbooje se fayde\n\n\n\n\n\nKabz mein aaram :-  Isme kafi matra mein paani aur fiber paya jata hain, jo khane ko asaani se pacha kar kabz jaisi samsyaon ko door kar dete hain.\n\n\n \n\n \n\nAcidity :- Masaledaar bhojan khane ke baad agar pet mein jalan aur acidity ki samsya ho to kharbooja kha kar ise door kiya ja sakta hain. \n\n\n\nDiarrhea se rahat :- Diarrhea ki samsya se preshan hain to kharbooje ko kali mirch, sendha namak, bhune huye jeere aur adarak ke saath mila kar khaye, turant rahat milegi. \n\n\n\n\n\n2. Kharbooje se skin ko hone wale fayde \n\n\n\nKharbooje mein mauzood ulfa aur beta  carotene , folates aur folic acid, potassium, Vitamin C, E, K ka khazana skin ko healthy rakhne ke saath usay chamakdaar bhi banaye rakhte hain. Kharbooze ke niyamit sewan se in faydo ko dekha ja sakta hain. \n\n\n\nSkin Texture ko banaye rakhta hain.\n\n\n\nKharbooje mein paye jane wala collagen tatv Skin ke natural glow aur banavat ko banaye rakhta hain. Saath hi yeh chhoti-moti chot aadi ko bharne mein bhi kafi faydemand hota hain. \n\n\n\n\n\nDaag-dhabbe mitata hain. \n\n\n\nKeel-muhanse, Chehre ke kale dhabbo aur bhi kai prakar ke skin ke problems ko kharbooje ki sahayta se door kiya ja sakta hain. \n\n\n\n\n\nMoisture banaye rakhe\n\n\n\nIsmein mauzood paani aur vitamin K, B aur E ki matra tavcha ki khoi hui nami ko lauta kar usay healthy aur fresh banata hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n3. Kharbooje se mahilaon ko hone wale fayde\n\n\n\nDoodh badhata hain.\n\n\n\nDelivery ke baad iske sewan se pryapt matra mein doodh banta hain, Jo shishu ke vikas ke liye bahut jaroori hain.\n\n\n\n \n\nPeriods ke dauran\n\n\n\nIsmein mauzood Potassium ki matra sharir ke extra sodium ko bahar nikal kar blood pressure ko control mein rakhta hain. Iske saath hi periods ke dauraan mahilon mein bleeding aur blood clotting ke banane ki samsya ko bhi khatm karta hain  \n\n  \n\n\n\n4. Motapa kam karne ke liye faydemand hain kharbooza\n\n\n\nCalorie ki bahut kam matra aur fiber ki acchi khasi matra samete huye kharbooje mein paani, minerals, vitamins aur kai tarah ke jaroori poshak tatv paye jate hain. Ise khane se 100% motapa kam kiya ja sakta hain.\n\n\n\n \n\nWajan kam karta hain.\n\n\n\nRojana thoda-thoda kharbooja kha kar bhi wajan ko control kiya ja sakta hain. Khane se pahle ise khane se bhookh shant ho jati hain. Jisek chalte aap jaroorat se jyada aahaar nahi le pate hain. Aise mein wajan dheere-dheere kam ho jata hain.  \n\n\n\n\n\n5. Pregnancy mein hain faydemand \n\n\n\nFolic Acid aur Iron \n\n\n\nYeh Garbhvati mahilon ko bahut hi fayda pahuchata hain. Iski bachche ke vikas bhi aham bhoomika hain.\n\n\n\n \n\nJanm ke samay hone wali samsya\n\n\n\nKharbooja khane se na sirf bacche ka vikas hota hain, blki janm ke saath hone wali kai prakar ki samsyaon se bhi bachata hain.\n\n\n\n \n\n \n\nJee machlana door karta hain\n\n\n\nPregnancy ke pahle mahine mein Jee machlane aur Ulti ki samsya  ko kharbooja kha kar kafi had tak kam kiya ja sakta hain.\n\n\n6. Dil ke liye hain faydemand\n\nDil ki bimariyo se bachata hain\n\nKharbooja heart attack aur kai prakar ki dil ki bimariyo se bachata hain. Saath hi isme mauzood kai prakar ke tatv jaise Adenosine blood Cloting nahi hone deta hain.\n\nBlood pressure ko control karta hain. \n\nPotassium ki adhik matra aur cholesterol ki kam matra blood circulation ko banaye rakhti hain jo Heart pulse ki sahi chalaane ke liye kargar hain.  \n\n\nArtriz ki jakdan ko kam karta hain. \n\nIske rojan sewan se Artriz ki ainthan nahi hoti hain. Jisse heart attack ki sambhavna kafi kam ho jati hain.\n\n\n7. Cancer se bachata hain\n\nIsme paye jane wala Flavonoids aur Carotenoids   jo ki cancer ki cancer jaisi bimari se ladte hain, aur usay banane wale cells ko rokte hain.\n\nAsamay budhape ko rokta hain\n\nColon Cancer, Breast cancer, uterus, Lung aur pancreatic Cancer aajkal hone wale aam cancer hain. Inhe door rakhne ke liye is mausam mein kharbooja khana chahiye.\n\nDiabetes\n\nKharbooze mein fructose aur  Glucose ki matra diabetes ke rogiyo ke liye theek hain.Yani ki ve bhi iska sewan kar sakte hain.\n\n8. Kharbooje se balo ko hone wale fayde\n\nBaalo ka vikas karta hain \n\nNutrients aur Minerals ka source hain Kharbooza, Jo ki baalo ke vikas ke liye bahut hi important hota hain. Saath hi isse Seer ki tavcha bhi healthy rahti hain.\n\nBaalo ke girne ki samsya ko door karta hain\n\nIske rojana sewan se Balo ke asamay girne aur jhadne ki samsya ko door kiya ja sakta hain.\n\nScalp ko poshan pradaan karta hain\n\nIske gude ko scalp par lagakar baalo ki acchi conditioning hoti hain.
