Jab bhi aap Carbohydrates wale food khate hain toh yeh sharir mein jakar glucose ya blood sugar mein break ho jata hain. Iske baad blood sugar iss body, dimaag aur nervous system ko energy deta hain. Toor daal fiber ka accha source hain, Jisse kabz se chhutkara milta hain. Fiber wali diet lene se ChronicDiseasenahi hoti hain.
