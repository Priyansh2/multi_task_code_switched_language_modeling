Jiskelakshan khas karke Sardi, Khujali, Naak bahna, Seerdard jaisi problems ho sakti hain. Agar aap allergy se peedit hain toh aap baar-baar Sardi-Jukam ke shikar ho sakte hain aur kai baar Asthma aur Saans lene mein dikkat bhi ho sakti hain.\nAllergy hone ki wajah :-\nAllergy ki problem hone ki kai wajah ho sakti hain, Jisme dhool, dhooaan, mitti ke kan, paudhe ki wajah se, phoolo ke soonghne seaur kai baar janwaro ke contact mein aane se bhi aur Medicine ke side-effect se bhi Allergy ho sakti hain. Zyadatar hum allergy ko seriously nahi lete hain. Lekin kai baar asaan sa dikhne wali allergy ki problem bade rogoko bulawa de sakti hain. Iske liye sabse pahle yeh janna bahutjaroori hain ki allergy kis cheez se huyi hain aur phir uske baadkhan-pan aurlifestyle mein kuch jaroori changes karke is samsya se asaani se bacha ja sakta hain. \nAllergy ke lakshan :- \nAllergy ke neeche likhe lakshan ho sakte hain.\nKhan-pan mein allergy :- Bahut se logo ko khane peene ki cheezo se jaise Macchli, Ande, Chocolate,Doodh aadi se bhi allergy hoti hain. \nSaans ki allergy :- Isme khansi, saans lene mein takhleef aur asthmajaisi samsya ho sakti hain.\nPuri body ki allergy :- Kabhi-Kabhi kuch logo ko allergyseserious situation ka saamna karna padta hain aur puri body mein ek saath gambhirlakshan paida ho jatehain. Aisi situation mein turant hospital mein jana chahiye.\nAankh ki allergy :- Aankho ka lal hona, Paani aana, jalan hona, khujali hona aadi.\nSkin ki allergy :- Skin ki allergy kaficommon hain aur yeh baarish ke mausam mein bahut zyadapaida hoti hain.
