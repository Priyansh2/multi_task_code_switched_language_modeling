Aise mein agar aap dahi ko regularly khate hain to aapko acchi neend lene mein kafi jyada madad milti hain.\n\n\n• Chawal \n\nVaise to yeh dekha jata hain ki log chaawal khane se katraate hain, kyonki chaawal khane se wajan badhne ka khatra sabse jyada hota hain. Lekin agar aap thodi se matraa mein roj chawal khate hain to aapko acchi neend aati hain. Acchi neend lene ke liye aap jasmine chaawal ka istemal kar sakte hain. Is chawal mein Glycemic Index jyada matra mein hote hain. Aise food good quantity mein Amino Acid ko paida karte hain, jisse achhi neend lene mein madad milti hain.\n\n\n• Kela \n\n\nAapko niyamit roop se Kela jaroor khana chahiye. Kele mein Potassium aur Vitamin B6 hota hain jo andhere mein neend laane wale hormone Melatonin ka production karta hain.\n\n\n• Cherry Juice pijiye \n\n\nAgar aapko acchi neend chahiye to aapko Cherry Juice peena chahiye. Cherry Juice ki help se aap sleeping pattern mein sudhaar kar sakte hain. Raat ko ek acchi neend lene ke liye aapko rojana 1 glass Cherry ka juice jaroor peena chahiye.\n\n\n• Eggs khaye \n\n\nJyadatar Dietitian ek balance diet mein eggs ko shamil karne ki advice dete hain. Lekin agar aap eggs ko apne breakfast mein shamil karte hain to aapko raat mein acchi neend aati hain.
