Vitamin B ke sewan se healthy aur glowing skin milti hain. Paneerko niyamit roop se apni diet mein shamil kare, aap kuch hi dino mein fark mahsoos karenge. \n\n\n\n• Healthy teeth :- Jab majboot daanto ki baat aati hain, toh calcium sabse jaroori hain. Lactose ek aisa padarthhota hain jo khane se nikalta hain aur daanto ko kharab karta hain.Paneer salviakepravaah ko badhata hain aur daanto seacid aur sugar ko saaf karta hain. \n\n\n\n• Gathiya se rahat :- Gathiyarog honeki sabsebadiwajah body meincalcium ki kami ka hona hain. Strong bones ke liye Calcium ki jaroorat hoti hain aur paneer mein Protein aur Calcium dono hi high quality mein mauzood hote hain. Paneer mein vitamin A, Phosphorus aurZinc bhi payajatahain. Vitamin B bhi paneer mein payajatahain. Khastaurpar baccho, pregnant women aur buzurgo ki haddiyo ko majboot karne mein paneer madad karta hain. \n\n\n\n• Tension ko kam kare :- Raat koneendnahi aatiya phir aaptanaav grasthain toh sone se pahle aappaneer ka sewankare, neend acchi aayegi. Yeh isliyehota hain kyonki paneermeinTryptophan Amino Acid paya jata hain, jo tension kam karne aur neend ko badhane mein madadgar hain. \n\n\n\n•Body ki immunity badhaye :- Paneer kasewan karnese sharirki rog-pratirodhak shamta badhti hain. Rog-pratirodhak kshamtabadhti hain toh bimariyo se ladne ki sharir ki power bhi badhti hain. \n\n\nPaneerkhane ke nuksaan :-\n\n\nPaneerkhana sehat keliye faydemandtoh hain.
