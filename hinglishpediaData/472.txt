Isliye is pani ko niyamit roop se peene se body mein blood ki kami ko door kiya ja sakta hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n• Diabetes ki bimari se bachaye \n\n\n\n\n\nNiyamit roop se Dhaniye ka paani peene se body ka glucose level balance mein rahta hain. Jisse aapko diabetes ki bimari hone ka khatra kafi kam ho jata hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n• Body ki Immunity strong banaye \n\n\n\nDhaniye ke paani mein Ascorbic Acid paya jata hain. Ascorbic Acid ko Antioxidant ki category mein rakha gya hain. Yeh special Anti-Oxidant body ki immunity ko boost karta hain. Jisse aapko Sardi-Jukam, Viral infection aadi hone ka khatra kafi kam ho jata hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n• Dil ke liye faydemand \n\n\n\nDhaniye ka paani peene se sharir mein Good Cholesterol yani HDL badhta hain aur body se kharab cholesterol yani LDL kam hota hain.
