Yeh sharir ko thandak pahuchane ke saath diabetes ko bhi door karta hain.\n\n\n\n \n\n \n\nMilk Shake \n\n\n\nCalcium aur Energy ka behatrin source Milk Shake swaad mein lajawaab hota hain aur ise kisi bhi samay piya ja sakta hain.
