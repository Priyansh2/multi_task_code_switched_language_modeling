Isliye Apach, Gas aur pet mein marod jaisi samsya hone par Mosambi ke juice ko peene ki salah di jati hain. Iska juice peene se pet mein pachaane wale jaroori enzymes paida hote hain. jo bekar toxins ko bahar nikaal kar pachan kriya ko sahi rakhte hain. Iska khatta-meetha taste ulti aur dast jaisi problems ko bhi door rakhta hain. \n\n\n\n\n\nKabz se rahat dilati hain \n\n\n\nMosambi mein maujood Acids sharir ke hanikarak tatvo ko bahar nikalte hain. Saath hi ismein maujood fibers kabz ki samsya se joojh rahe vyakti ko rahat dilate hain. Iske juice mein thoda sa namak mila kar peene se kabz se rahat milti hain. \n\n\n\nImmune system sahi rakhti hain\n\n\n\nRojana iske sewan se immune system sahi rahta hain. Saath hi dil ki bimariyo ka khatra kam rahta hain. Blood circulation ko sahi rakhne ke saath ismein maujood Vitamin C ki bharpoor matra kai tarah ke infection jaise sardi khansi se ladne mein sahayak hoti hain. \n\n\n\n\n\nScurvy rog se bachata hain\n\n\n\nVitamin C ki kami se hone wala rog hain Scurvy. Iske ras mein maujood kai saare tatv rusi rokne ke saath hi lambe aur ghane baalo ke liye faydemand hote hain.\n\n\n\nDo munhe baal \n\n\n\nDhool, Pollution aur badlte mausam ke chalte lagbhag sabhi ko do-munhe baalo ki samsya ho jati hain, Lekin thodi si savdhani barat kar ise door kiya ja sakta hain. Isse chehre par hone wale keel-muhanse , Daag-dhabbo se chhutkara paya ja sakta hain. Saath hi tavcha soft bhi bani rahti hain. Isse chehre par asamay dikhne wale budhape ko roka ja sakta hain.\n\n\n\n\n \n\nHontho ke kalepan ko door karta hain \n\n\n\n\nIske ras ki 3-4 boonde rojana hontho par lagane se kalepan ki samsya kuch hi dino mein kam kiya ja sakta hain.
