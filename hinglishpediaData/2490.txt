Smartphone se log jab bhi mauka milta hain Selfie lena nahi bhooltehain. Bedroom se lekar Party event, travelling tak har jagah selfie lene ka bhoot chadha rahtahain. Jin logo ko selfie lene ki aadat ho, unhe aaj hum batayenge aise top tips jo perfect selfie khichne ke liye hain. Aaiye jante hain perfect Selfie click karne ke khas tips :- • Do Something Interesting :- Kuch interesting kaam karte huye selfie lena kafi special hotahain. Selfielete waqt kabhi seedhe ban kar na baithe, Kuch na kuch harkatejaroor kare. Aisi selfiekafi acchi mani jati hain. \n\n• Don’t EditPhoto :- Selfie ko kabhi bhi Edit nahi karni chahiye. Isse photo mein realty nahi rahti hain, Selfie jaisi bhi aayi ho apne aap mein best hain.\n\n• One Hand or Two Hand :- Selfie lete waqt aap kaun sa haath use kar rahe hain, Yeh bhi mayne rakhta hain. Ek haath se selfie lena ho ya dono haath se , dono alag-alag hain. Aise mein aap in dono tariko se selfie le sakte hain aur jo aapko acchi lageturant apne friends mein sharekare.\n\n• Adjust your shoulder placement :- Agar aapka shoulder camera ke Parallelhain toh usey side ki aor thoda aur jhuka de. Ise aap rightya left kisi bhi side jhuka sakte hain. Isse aapki selfie kafi acchi ban sakti hain. \n\n• Use Back Camera:- Aamtaur par selfie lovers hamesha se selfie lene ke liye front camera ka istemal karte hain. Halaki yeh kafi accha hain, Lekin agar aap isse bhi behtar selfielena chahte hain, toh hamesha back camera ka istemal kare. Back camera ka resolution kafi high hota hain, Jisme blurrinesske chance kafi kam hote hain. \n\n• Experiments with Angles :- Selfie lete waqt perfect angle ka hamesha dhyaan rakhe. Jaise ki aap selfie lene ja rahe hain, apne seer ko ekbaar Right Side aur phir left side rakh kar phone ki screen par nazar dale. Isse aapko andaza lag jayega ki ek side dusriside se behtar hain. Aise mein jo angle aapko accha lageturant photo click kare. Kabhi bhi screen ke beech mein face rakh kar selfiena le, Yeh acchi nahi aayegi. \n\n• Smiling Face :- Koi bhi photo acchi tabhi mani jati hain jabusme muskurahat ka alag andaz dekhneko mile. Aapko Monalisa ki veh picture toh yaad hi hogi, Jo apne smile ki wajah se puri duniya mein famous hain. Isiliye selfie lete waqt smiling face hamesha se important hota hain. \n\n• Pose with something new :- Kisi bhi perfect selfie mein pose ka role kafi important hota hain. Different poses mein selfie lene ka alag hi mazahain. Iske saath hi aap looks ke saath bhi experimentskar sakte hain. Kabhi glasses laga kar, kabhi hair style change karke, vahi ghar mein koi paaltujaanwar hain toh uske saath selfie lena best option hain. \n\n• Take a full bodyshot :- Jyadatarselfie mein aapka chehra hi dikhayi deta hain, Lekin full body shot lena ek behtaroption ban sakta hain. Kisi full size mirror ke saamne selfielena ek alag experience hota hain, toh abki baar jaroortry kare. \n\n• Standin the light :- Selfie lete waqt light adjustment jaroori hain, Jab bhi selfielene ja rahe ho toh face hamesha oos taraf rakhe, jis tarafse aapke chehreparlight aa rahi ho. Is tarah se selfie kafiacchi aati hain. Iske alawaagaraap light ke hisaab se khud ko adjust karenge, Jaise ki half face par light pade, aise mein kafi acchi selfie aa sakti hain.
