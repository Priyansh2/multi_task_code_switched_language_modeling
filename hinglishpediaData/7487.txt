Kuch aisi cheeze hain jinhe apni diet ka hissa banane se aap badhti umar ke asar ko rok sakte hain. In khane wali cheezo mein Anti-Oxidants aur Phytochemical ki bharmaar hoti hain, jo badhti umar keasar ko kam karne mein madad karte hain. Chaliye jante hain kuch aisi hi aam cheezo ke baremein jinhe khane setavcha hamesha jawan bani rahti hain. • Macchli :-Fish mein Omega-3 Fatty Acidpaya jata hain. Iske sewan se skin hamesha youngbani rahti hain. Yeh gathiya, ulcerative Colitisaadi bimariya bhi door hoti hain.• Hari sabziya :- Pattedaar Saag, Palak, Methi, Salad patta, Ajwain aadi mein Beta-Carotene bharpoor matra mein paya jata hain. Iske alawa ismeCalcium, Iron, Folic Acid, Vitamin C aur bhi kai nutrientspaye jate hain. Hari sabziyo ka niyamit sewan karne seskin hamesha young bani rahti hain. \n• Peele rang ke phal :- Papita, Gajar, Orange aur sabziya jaise Kaddu, Aam, Khubani, Shakarkand, Beta-CaroteneaurVitamin C se bharpoor hote hain. Isliye in cheezo ko apne khane mein zaroor shamil karna chahiye. Tavcha jawan bani rahegi.\n• Jayi (Oats) :- Roj aadha cup sukhi jayi ka choker ya ek cup sukha jayi ka daliya cholesterolko control karta hain. Oats (Jaii) ke sewan se blood circulation sahirahta hain. Saath hi skin bhi young bani rahti hain. • Soya :- Soya products, Soyabean, Soya ka aata, Soya milk aur tofu kam fat aur calcium se bharpoor hote hain. Soya products mein genistein ki bharmaar rahti hain. Iske sewan se cancer door rahta hain aur skin young bani rahti hain. \n• Olive Oil :- Jaitun ya sarso ka tel khane aur chehrepar lagane se chehre mein jhurriya nahi padti hain. \n• KhattePhal :- Khatte phalo meinSantra, Mosambi, Angoor, Nimbu aadi mein Vitamin C, Bioflavonoids aur Limonin acchi quantity mein payejate hain. In phalo mein kafiacchi matra mein Antioxidants hote hain, jo ki tavcha ko lambewaqt tak jawanbanaye rakhte hain. • Gobhi :- Gobhi, PattaGobhi, Broccoli, Brussels sprout aur gobhi family ki sabhi sabziya bimariyo se hame bachane mein madadkartihain. In sabziyo mein payejane wale Beta-Carotene, indole glucosinolates aur Isothiocyanates (Jo khas karke broccoli meinpayejatehain) skin ko young banayerakhtehain.\n\n\n• Dahi :- Dahi mein lactosebacteriahote hain jo pachan mein madad karte hain. Calcium ka yehbahut accha source hain. Yeh tavcha ke liye bhi faydemandhota hain.\n\n• Lahsun :- Lahsun mein Allicin naam ka tatvpaya jata hain. Yeh blood se badcholesterolaur triglyceride ko kam karta hain. Yeh body ki immunityko badhata hain. Saath hi isme Anti-Oxidants bhi bharpoor matra mein paye jate hain. Isliye lahsun ka niyamit roop se sewan karne se, skin lambi umartak jawan bani rahti hain. Saathhi yeh dil sejudi bimariyo ko bhi doorkarta hain. \n\n• Tamatar :- Tamatar mein Lycopene paya jata hain, Yeh ek tarah ka Caretonoidhain. Saath hi Anti-Oxidants bhi mauzood hote hain, Jo skin ko young banaye rakhte hain. \n\n• Apple aur Strawberry :- Seb aur Strawberry mein ghulansheel fiber bloodsugarlevelko kamkarta hain. Saath hiin phalo mein Bharpoor matra meinAnti-Oxidants payejate hain. Isliyeinke niyamit sewan seskin hamesha jawan dikhayi deti hain. Yeh Cancer se bhi ladnemein help karte hain, yeh cancer cells ko khatm karne ka kaam karte hain. \n\n• Green Tea :- Green tea mein Polyphenols paye jate hain. Saath hi isme sahimatra meinAnti-Oxidantsbhi mauzood hote hain. Isliye regularly greantea peete rahne se tavcha hamesha jawan bani rahtihain.
