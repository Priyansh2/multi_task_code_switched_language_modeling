Ismein Vitamin A, B1, B6, D, Potassium, Phosphorus, Iron aadi paye jate hain. Niyamit roop se Kheere ka juice sharir ko takatwar banata hain. Yeh kabz se mukti dilata hain. Kheera pani ka bahut hi badhiya source hain., Ismein 96% pani hota hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n\nKheere mein Erepsin enzyme hota hain, jo Protein ke pachan mein madadgar hota hain. Ise khane se bal swasth rahte hain. Iska juice daanto ke keedon ko khatm karta hain. Gas, Acidity, Chhati mein jalan jaisi samsyaon mein niyamit roop se kheera khana faydemand hota hain. Jo log motape se preshan hain unhe subah ke samay iska sewan karna chahiye. \n\n\n\n\n\n1. Kheera khane se kabz door hota hain. Yeh Pelia, Bukhaar, Sharir ki garmi aur jalan aadi ke dosho ko door karta hain. Iska ras paththari mein bhi labhdayak hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n2. Yadi subah uthne par Seer mein dard yaa khumari ki shikayat hoti hain to sone se pahle kheera khaye. Kheera mein Vitamin B, Sugar  aur electrolights mauzood hote hain. Yeh sharir ke liye paushtik aur jaroori tatv hain. Jo Headache se ubarne ke liye madadgar hote hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n3. Kheere ke karan sharir mein uric acid ka level control mein rahta hain, Jisse gurde (Kidney) apne sahi aakaar mein rahte hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n4. Kheere mein Silicon aur Sulfur mauzood hota hain. Yeh balon ko ghana aur chamakdaar banata hain. Behtar natize ke liye aap chahe to Kheere ke juice ko gajar aur palak ke juice ke saath milakar pee sakte hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n5. Swimming karne se pahle aur baad mein sharir ke jis hisse mein charbi jyada ho , vaha kheere ka tukda malne se wajan tezi ke saath kam hota hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n6. Kheere mein Silesia bahut matra mein paya jata hain. Isse jodo ko majbooti milti hain. Gajar aur kheere ka juice mila kar peene se gathiya rog door hota hain. Isse uric acid ka level bhi kam hota hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n7. 250 Gram kheere ke ras din mein 3-4 baar peena chahiye. Kheere ke ras mein 1 chammach shahad aur 1/2 chammach nimbu milakar peene se sharab ka nasha utar jata hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n8. Diabetes aur blood pressure mein kheera khana bahut hi faydemand hota hain. Kheere ka ras Pancreas ko active karta hain. Pancreas ke   active ho jane par insulin banta hain. Jisse diabetes control mein rahti hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n9. Garmiyon mein sharir ke tapman ko ek saman rakhne ke liye Kheera aur ajawain ka ras milakar piye. Bukhaar hone par kheere ka juice faydemand hota hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n10. Jin ladkiyon ko masik dharm mein bahut jyada preshani hoti hain, ve dahi mein kheere ko kaskar usme pudina, kala namak, kali mirch, zeera aur Heeng dalkar rayta banakar khaye. Aaram milega.\n\n\n\n\n\n11. Kapde par agar daag-dhabba lag gya ho to usay saaf karne ke liye kheera bahut hi labhkari hota hain. Daag-dhabbo par kheere ka ras laga kar kuch der ke liye chhod de.
