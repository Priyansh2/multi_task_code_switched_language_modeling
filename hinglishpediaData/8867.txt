Yeh pet ke tissue ki marmmat karne mein sakshm hain. Yeh body ke energy level ko badha deta hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n4. Iske beej mein pani ko saaf karne ka gun hota hain. Beej ke churan ke roop mein paani ke saath milaya jata hain. Pani mein ghulkar yeh ek natural Clarification agent ban jata hain. Yeh pani ko bacteria rahit banata hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n5.
