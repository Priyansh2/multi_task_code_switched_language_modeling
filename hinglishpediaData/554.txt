Phone par 720 x 1280 pixels resolution ke saath 5 inch ka HD AMOLED display diya gya hain. Phone ko power deta hain 1.7 GHz Quad-Core MediaTek (MT6582) processor. Iske saath hi aapko isme 2 GB ki RAM milegi. Iske alawa Selfie Click karne ke liye is par aapko milega 5 Megapixel ka front facing camera. Connectivity ke liye isme diya gya hain 3G, Wi-Fi 802.11 b/ g / n , Micro USB aur Bluetooth ki suvidha.
