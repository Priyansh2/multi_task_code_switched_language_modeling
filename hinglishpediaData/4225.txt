Lekin iske guno ke baare mein agar aap janenge to jaroor ise apni diet mein aap shamil karenge. Blood sugar, shariri kamjori aur anemia jaisi bimariyo mein Beetroot bada hi faydemand hain. Aaj hum aapko beetroots ke fayde aur kin-kin bimariyo mein ise khana faydemand rahta hain aapko batayenge.\n\n\n\n\n\n\n1. Kabz hone par \n\n\n\nBeetroot mein fiber hone se pet se judi samsyaye khatm ho jati hain. Ise roj khane se pet sahi rahta hain aur toxins bahar nikal jate hain.\n\n\n\n \n\n2. Brain power badhta hain\n\n\n\nUK ki ek university ke anusaar , Beetroot ka juice 16% tak sharirik power ko badata hain. Beetroot mein nitrate hone ki wajah se Oxigen pure sharir mein pahuchta hain. Isse Dimaag sahi kaam karta hain.\n\n\n\n \n\n 3. Pregnant mahilaon ke liye faydemand\n\n\n\nBeetroot mein folic acid  hota hain, jo pregnant mahilaon ke un-born baby ke liye sahi rahta hain. Iske alawa beetroot se pregnancy ke samay mahila ko energy milti hain. Beetroot mein maujood Folic Acid Baby ki spinal Cord ke liye accha hota hain.  \n\n\n\n4. Osteoporosis ke liye \n\n\n\nBeetroot mineral silica hota hain.Jo calcium ki purti karta hain. Calcium sharir ke liye jaroori tatv hain. Calcium se haddi aur daant majboot hote hain. Isliye rojana ek glass chukkander ka juice piye taki body mein calcuim ki kami naa ho.\n\n\n\n \n\n \n\n5. Diabetes mein faydemand\n\n\n\nJin logo ko diabetes ki dikkat hoti hain, Unko meetha khane ka bahut mann karta hain, Aise mein Beetroot meetha khane ki ichcha ko pura karne ke saath bhookh bhi badhata hain. Beetroot body ka sugar level sahi rakhta hain. Saath hi beetroot mein dusri sabziyo ke mukable calorie bahut hi kam hoti hain aur yeh fat free bhi hota hain.\n\n\n\n \n\n6. Anemia ke liye \n\n\n\nLogo ka manana hain ki Beetroot Lal rang ki hoti hain, yeh khoon ko badhata hain aur anemia ke liye sahi rahta hain. Lekin iske peeche ki sachchaai yeh hain ki Beetroot mein Iron jyada hota hain. jo Hemagglutinin ko  sahi rakhta hain. Hemagglutinin sharir mein oxygen aur nutrition\'s ki purti karta hain. Anemia vaise bhi iron ki kami ke karan hota hain, Isliye is bimaari mein Beetroot khane ki salah di jati hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n7. Thakavat hone par \n\n\n\nEk study ke mutabik , Beetroot energy badhata hain. Nitrate hone ki wajah se sharir mein oxygen sahi tarike se pahuchta hain., Jisse sharir ka energy level badh jata hain. Saath hi beet root mein Iron ki bharpoor matra hone ki wajah se sharirik kshamta badh jati hain \n\n\n\n8. Cancer se bachne ke liye faydemand\n\n\n\nBeetroot mein betacyanin bhi hota hain. jo cancer se ladne mein skasham hain. Jin logo ko tymer aur breast cancer hota hain., unhe beetroot khane ki salah di jati hain. Beetroot khane se cancer ke treatment mein madad milti hain. \n\n\n\n9. Blood pressure ko kam karna \n\n\n\nBeetroot mein Nitrate hota hain, Jo pet mein banne wali gas ko kam karta hain. Ise ham Nitric Oxide ke naam se jante hain. Healthy aur fit rahne ke liye diet mein kam se kam 500 gram beetroot ko jaroor shamil kare.\n\n\n\n \n\n10. Bad-cholesterol ko kam karna \n\n\n\nChukandar mein ghulne wala fiber, flavonoids aur betacyanin hota hain. Betacyanin ki wajah se Chukkander ka rang lal hota hain. Isme anti-oxidant hota hain. Anti-oxidants hone ki wajah se LDL Cholesterol kam hota hain.
