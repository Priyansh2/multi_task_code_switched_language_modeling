Kutte ke kaatne par aapko kai kism ke infection ho jate hain aur toh aur insaan pagal bhi ho jata hain. Kai baar kutte ke kaatne ke baad insaan ko turant treatment nahi mil pata hain, Aise mein kuch gharelu ilaj aur nuskhe aapki madad karenge. \n\n\n\nPurane zamane mein bhi log kutte ke kaatne par in upay ko ajmaate thay. Agar aapko kutte ne kaat liya hain aur aapko doctor turant nahi mil pa raha hain to turant in gharelu ilaj aur upay ko apnayiye. Home Remedies & Treatment for Dog Bites in Hindi/Urdu. \n\n\n\nKutte ke kaatne par apnayiye yeh gharelu nuskhe aur upay :- \n\n\n\n1. Dog Bites wali jagah ko kisi acche Antibacterial saabunt se dhoye aur phir Dettol laga kar patti baandhne se zehar ka asar nahi padta hain. \n\n\n\n2. Pyaaz ka juice peene se aapko fayda hota hain. \n\n\n\n3. 15 Kali Mirch aur 2 Chammach Jeere ko paani mein daal kar pees kar is paste ko kutte ke kaate jane wali jagah par lagane se kuch dino mein zehar ka asar khatm ho jata hain. \n\n\n\n4. Lal Mirch Powder ko kutte ke kaatne wali jagah par turant lagane se kutte ka zehar nahi failta hain. \n\n\n\n5. Chaulai ki jad 50 se 100 gram paani mein pees kar ghol kar baar-baar mareez ko pilane se kutte ka zehar khatm ho jata hain. \n\n\n\n6. Aam ki guthali ko paani ke sath ghees kar kutte ke kaate jane wali jagah par lagane se Dog ka poison khatm ho jata hain. \n\n\n\n7. Genhu ke atte ki kacchi roti ko kaate jane wali jagah par baandhe aur phir oos roti ko usi kutte ko khane ke liye de, jisne aapko kaata hain. Agar vah kutta roti kha le toh samjhe ki kutta pagal nahi hain aur agar na khaye to samjhe vah pagal hain. \n\n\n\n8. Aloe Vera ko ek aor se chhil kar uske gude par Senda namak daal kar dog bite wali jagah par lagaye aur kisi cotton cloth se usay baandh le aur aisa 2 se 3 dino tak lagatar kare. Yeh dog poision ko khatm kar deta hain. \n\n\n\n9. Shahad ke saath peesi huyi pyaaz ko mila kar kutta kaatne wali jagah par lagane se zehar ka asar khatm ho jata hain. \n\n\n\n10. Pyaaz ka ras, Akhrot ki giri ko barabar matra mein pees kar usme namak mila le aur phir ise shahad ke saath mila kar kutte ke kaatne wali jagah par lep kare aur patti baandh le. Aisa karne se body par kutte ke zehar ka effect nahi padta hain. \n\n\nNote :- Sach toh yeh hain ki Kutte ke kaatne par aapko Doctor ke paas jana chahiye aur Rabbis ke injection lagwaane chahiye. Yeh gharelu nuskhe sirf tab hi aap istemal kare, jab aap doctor ke paas jane mein asamarth ho. Lekin mauka milte hi turant acche se Hospital mein jaye aur Kutte kaatne ka treatment doctor se karwaaye. Kyonki Rabbis ke bacteria ko Injection ke jariye hi khatm kiya ja sakta hain. Isliye Kutte ke kaatne ke baad doctor se turant mile.
