Lekin kya aap jante hain ki kai baar chote-chote lakshano par dhyaan dene ki wajah se insaan nervous break down ka shikaarho jata hain. Aap ya aapka koi kareebi iska sheekar na ho toh iske liye hum aapko batane ja rahe hain kuch aise lakshan ke bare mein jo nervous system ki aor ishara kar dete hain. Nervous System Breakdown Symptoms in Hindi. Nervous Breakdown Signs in Hindi/Urdu. \n\n\n1. Mental Stress :- Stress ya tension bhinervous breakdown ki wajah banti hain. Stress freerahne ke liye jaroori hain ki aap samay-samay par apne friendsaur apne pasandeeda logo ke saath waqt bitaye. Mental stress jitna kam hoga, nervous break down ka khatra bhi utna kam hoga. Music soone, dance kare aur khush ho kar kaam kare, dheere-dheerestress kam hone lagega.\n\n\n\n2. Depression :- Depression ek aisi problem hain jo dheere-dheerekab gharjati hain. Insaan samajh hi nahi pata hain. Kai baar kisi insaan ke kareebi log bhi yeh samajh nahi paatehain ki veh depression mein hain. Depression mein rahne walavyakti hamesha dheemi awaaz mein baat karta hain. Veh bahut bura feel karta hain. AiselogSociety aurfriends se bhi doorhonelagte hain. \n\n3. Concentration ki kami :- Concentration ki kami ek taraf se mentally disorder ki tarafishara karti hain. Baar-baar cheezoko bhool jana. Choti-moti baato mein overemotional ho jana, Kai baar yeh problem itni badh jati hain ki insaan pure concentration ke saath koi kaam nahi kar pata hain. \n\n4. Suicide ka khyaal aana :- Kisi bhi insaan ko agar Suicide ka khyaal aata hain toh kanhi na kanhi uske peeche uske mann mein gahri nirasha chhupi hoti hain, Lekin nirasha mein suicideka khyaal aana aur baar-baar suicideka khyaal aane mein antar hain. Baar-baarmann mein suicideka khyaal aaye toh iska matlab hain ki aap kisi maansik bimari se joojh rahe hain aur aapko turant psychologist se contact karna chahiye.\n\n5. Bhaavnatmak sanket :- Kai baar insaanbahutjyadabhaavuk hota hain, Lekin choti-choti baatoparbhaavook hona bhi accha nahi hain. \n\n6. Baar-baarmood badalna :- Baar-baarmood badalna accha nahi mana jata hain. Kabhi-kabhi bahut zyada depressive rahna, kabhi bahutzyada khush rahna aur kabhi ekdum segussa aa jana yeh sabhi nervousbreakdownki tarafishaara karte hain. Aapka mood baar-baarbadalta hain toh sambhal jayiye aur kisipsychologist se consult kijiye. \n\n7. Sex life :- Sex life bhi agar theek na ho toh yeh bhi mental disorderki ek ahem wajah ban sakti hain. Kaibaar sex problems ki wajah se bhi tension badh jati hain. Aise mein insaan din mein theek se so nahi paata hain aur nahi raat ko aurusay kai tarah ki problems staanelagti hain. \n\n8.Sharirik sanket :- Nervous breakdown hone par normally sharirik sanket bahut kam milte hain, Lekin phir bhi kuch sharirik lakshan dekhe ja sakte hain jo ki is tarah hain, Aniyamit dilki dhadakna, Tension, haatho ka hamesha chip-chipa rahna, muscles mein tanaav,kabz rahna, chakkar aana aadi iske main lakshan hain. \n\n9. Nashe ki latt ki wajah :- Kai baar drugs ya Drinks ya dusre tarah ki nashe kiaadat bhi nervous breakdown ki wajah ban sakti hain. Kai baar bahut zyada gussa ya niraasha mein jab koi insaan lagatar nasha karne ka aadi ho jata hain toh veh maansik roop se aswasthya ho jata hain.\n \nKya kare jab yehproblem ho ? \n\n\n\n\n\n \nProfessional se madad le :- Aapke saathupar batayi gayi samsyaon mein se koi bhi samsya ho toh aapko psychologist ki madad leni chahiye. In problems ko choti na samajhte huye aur sharm aur jhijhak chhod kar aap psychologist se milengetoh yehsamsayein bahut asaani se sulajh jayengi. \n\nIn baatokarakhe dhyaan :- \n\n\n\n • Samay par soye. \n\n • Bharpoorneendle. \n\n • Khana samay par khaye.\n\nDepression ko kahe bye-bye ;- Apnekareebi aur dosto seapni problem ko share kare.
