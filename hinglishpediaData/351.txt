Rukhe baalo ko Silky banane ki tips ya Gharelu nuskhe ke baare mein janana chahte hain? kya aap apne ghar ya rasoi mein padi huyi aisi cheezo ke baare mein janana chahte jo aapke baalo ko silky bana de? Inhe lagane ke tarike ke baare mein janana chahte hain to yeh post jaroor padhe.\n\n\n\nBejaan aur rukhe baal (Dry Hair) kisi ko bhi acche nahi lagte hain. Sabhi yahi chahte hain ki baal silky aur shiny ho. Iske liye jyadatar log kai tarah ke cosmetics ka use karte hain, Lekin inse baal lambe samay tak healthy aur beautiful nahi bane rah sakte hain. Baalo ko sunder banane ke liye sahi khan-pan aur gharelu tarika adhik kargar hote hain. Yadi aap bhi rukhe aur bejan baalo se preshan hain to apnayiye yeh gharelu nuskhe:- \n\n\n\n1. Aloe Vera :- Iska ras (Juice) baalo mein lagane se baal mulayam honge. Ise nahane se pahle apne baalo aur jado mein laga kar thande paani se seer ko dho le. Aap aloe vera ke ark wala shampoo bhi laga sakte hain.  \n\n\n\n2. Doodh aur Shahad :- Doodh aur Shahad ko barabar matra mein mila le. Is mishran ko baal dhone se 1 ghanta pahle acchi tarah lagaye. Isse rukhe aur curly baal sahi ho jate hain. \n\n\n\n3. Beer :- Beer mein adhiktar matra mein yeast paya jata hain, Jisse baalo ko moisture milta hain aur veh mulayam ban jate hain. Seer par thodi beer girayiye aur 15 minutes tak usse seer ki massage kijiye. Phir thande paani se dho lijiye. Good results ke liye aisa 1 hafte tak lagatar kijiye. \n\n\n\n4. Dahi :- Baalo mein Dahi ko 20 minutes tak laga rahne de aur phir usay shampoo se dho le. Par agar thand ka mausam ho to, Seer mein Dahi na lagaye varna aapko sirdard aur sardi ho sakti hain.\n\n\n\n5. Ande :- Ande mein Protein aur Vitamin hote hain, Jisse baalo ko poshan milta hain. Seer mein ek anda phod kar laga le aur kuch der baad Seer ko halke garam paani se dho le. Aap chahe to Ande ko apne shampoo mein bhi daal sakte hain. \n\n\n\n                  2 Ando ka yolk (Peela bhaag) lekar acche tarah se fent le. Is mixture mein 2 chammach Olive Oil yani ki jaitun ka tel mila le. Brush ki help se baalo mein lagaye. 2 Ghante tak baalo mein laga rahne de. Baad mein shampoo laga kar dho le. Isse baal straight aur shiny ho jayenge. \n\n\n\n6. Nariyal Paani :- Nariyal Paani mein thoda Nimbu ka ras mila le. Mishran ko 1 din ke liye Freeze mein rakh de. Creamy Paste taiyaar ho jayega. Ise apne seer par acchi tarah se lagaye aur massage kare aur baalo ko bhaap de. Bhaap dene ke liye gungune paani mein towel bhigo kar ise seer par lapet le. Ek ghante baad seer ko dho le aur hafte mein 3 baar yeh nuskha apnaye. Isse baal straight aur shiny ho jayenge. \n\n\n\n7. Multani Mitti :- Ek cup Multani Mitti le. Usme ek Anda aur 5 Chammach Chawal ka Aata milaye. Is misharan ko acchi tarah fent le. Baalo ko acchi tarah se kanghi kar le. Phir mishran ko baalo mein lagaye. Baalo ko mitti lagane ke baad fold na kare aur sidha rakhe. Baalo par 40 Minutes tak laga rahne de aur puri tarah se sukhne ke baad hi dhoye. Is paste ko mahine mein 3 se 4 baar jaroor lagaye, Iske istemal se baal silky, shiny aur straight ho jayenge. \n\n\n\n8. Doodh :- Ek spray bottle mein paani aur doodh barabar matra mein le. Is mixture ko apne baalo mein spray kar le. Yeh mishran pure baalo mein barabar matra mein spray hona chahiye. Iske baad bade daanto wali kanghi se baalo ko swaar le, Taki harek baal tak doodh aur paani ka misharan asaani ke saath pahuch sake. Ise baalo mein 1 ghanta tak laga rahne de aur usay baad mein acchi tarah se dho le. Shampoo se dhone ke baad Conditioner ka istemal kare. Isse baal straight aur shiny ho jayenge.
