Neem se hone wale fayde (Benefits of Neem in Hindi/Urdu.) :- \n\nBaalo se related problems mein :- Neem ka tel baalo lamba karne aur dandruff se chhutkara dilane ke liye bahut hi faydemand hain. Iske liye halke haatho se baalo ki jado par neem ke tel se maalish kare. Isse aapko patle baalo ki problems se bhi chhutkara milta hain. \n\nAgar aapke baalo mein dandruff ki problem hain toh neem ke powder ko paani mein mila kar scalp par lagaye aur 1 ghante baad ise shampoo se dho le. Agar aapko neem ki pattiya nahi mil rahi hain toh aapko bazaar mein asaani se neem ka powder mil jayega. Aap uska istemal kare. \n\nMoohase door bhagaye ;- Agar aapko pimples ne preshan kar diya hain toh iske liye neem ka istemal behtar hoga. Neem ke istemal se aap moohaso se chhutkara pa sakti hain. Iske liye neem ke patto ko paani mein ubaal kar rooi ki madad se is paani ko chehre par jaha jaha moohase ho vaha-vaha lagaye. Isse aapke moohase bahut jaldi sahi ho jayenge. \n\nBlood sugar ko control kare :- Iske liye neem ki patti kafi faydemand saabit hoti hain. Yeh natural insulin ki tarah kaam karta hain. Iske liye rojana subah khali pet neem ka juice piye aur iski pattiyo ko chabaye.\n\n\nDry Skin ko normal banaye :- Agar aapki skin bahut jyada dry hain toh aapko neem facepack ka istemal karna chahiye. Iske liye thoda sa neem powder lekar usme kuch boonde grape seed oil ki mila le. Is pack ko chehre par lagaye aur 3 minutes ke baad thande paani se dho le. Ise hafte mein 1 ya 2 baar jaroor lagaye.\n\n\nNatural Skin tonner ka kaam karta hain :- Neem ka niyamit roop se istemal karne se aapke chehre ki jhurriyo aur jhaaiyo se nijaat mil jayega. Iske liye neem ko ubaale aur phir is paani ko thanda karke raat ko sote waqt ise chehre par lagaye. Agar aapki skin oily hain toh thoda sa gulab jal mila le aur phir subah apne chehre ko dhoye. Isse aapka chehra khubsoorat aur mulayam ho jayega. \n\nCancer se bachata hain :- Neem ki pattiyo mein Glycoprotein paya jata hain jo tumour cells ki growth ko rokta hain. Jisse aapko cancer se bachaav hota hain. Iske liye roj subah neem ka juice jaroor piye.\n\n\nWajan kam karne mein labhdayak :- Neem mein aise tatv paye jate hain jo aapke wajan ko kam kar sakte hain. Iske liye ek mutthi neem ke phoolo ko lekar paani mein ubaal le aur isme 1 chammach Nimbu ka ras aur aadha chammach shahah mila kar roj khali pet piye. \n\nChehre ke blackheads ko mitaye ;- Neem Blackheads, whiteheads aur big skin pores ko theek karne mein kafi madadgar saabit hua hain. Iske liye neem ki pattiya aur santre ke chhilko ka paste banaye, aur isme kuch boonde Shahad, Soyamilk aur Dahi dale aur infected jagaho par lagaye. Jald rahat paane ke liye hafte mein kam se kam 3 baar ise istemal jaroor kare.\n\n\nSkin infection se bachata hain :- Neem ki pattiyo mein Anti-Bacterial, Anti-Fungal aur Anti-Viral tatv hote hain. Jiski wajah se yeh skin ke liye kafi faydemand hain. Neem skin ko bina dry kiye sujan aur jalan ko bhi door karta hain. Iske liye neem ki mulayam pattiyo ko ubaale aur jab paani hara hone lage toh gas band kare aur phir is paani ko chhane aur nahane ke paani mein ise milaye.
