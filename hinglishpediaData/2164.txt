Kheere mein aise tatv paye jate hain jo pancreas ko activate karte hain. Jisse sharir mein insulin banti hain. Jo ki diabetes se aapko bachata hain. Isme jyada matra mein Calcium, Potassium paya jata hain jo aapke blood pressure ko control mein rakhta hain. \n\n\n♦ Baalo aur Skin ki dekhbhaal karta hain :- Kheere mein Silicon aur Sulphur bharpoor matra mein paya jata hain, Jo ki baalo ko badhane mein madad karta hain. Iske liye aapko Ise gajar aur palak ke juice ke saath bhi mila kar le sakte hain. Saath hi chehre par iska istemal facemask ke taur par karne se chehre mein kasaav aata hain aur tanning se nijat milti hain. \n\n♦ Mooh ki badboo door kare :- Agar aapke mooh se badboo aa rahi hain, toh kuch minutes ke liye apne mooh mein kheere ka tukda rakh le. Isliye pet ko thanda rakhne ke liye Kheere ka sewan kare. \n\n♦ Aankho ke neeche sujan se dilaye rahat :- Kheere mein Ascorbic acid aur caffiec acid naam ke 2 tatv paye jate hain, Jo aapke sharir mein paani ki kami ko pura karte hain. Jisse aapke aankho ke neeche ki sujan chali jati hain. \n\n\n♦ Masoodhe healthy rakhta hain :- Kheera khane se masoodho ki bimari kam hoti hain. Kheere ke ek tukde ko jeebh se mooh ke upari hisse par aadha minute tak roke. Aise mein kheere se nikalne wala Phytochemical mooh ki durgandh ko khatm karta hain. \n\n\n♦ Pachan ko theek rakhta hain :- Kheere mein bharpoor matra mein Fiber paya jata hain, Jiski wajah se yeh pet ki har problem se hame nijat dilata hain. Saath hi yeh aapke pet ko saaf bhi karta hain. \n\n\n♦ Periods Problems mein rahat dilaye :- Kheere ka niyamit sewan karne se yeh periods ki samsyaon se nijat dilata hain.
