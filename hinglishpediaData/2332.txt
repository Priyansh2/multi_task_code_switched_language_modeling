Bahut saare vitamins aur minerals se bharpoor khajoor ka swaad mitha hota hain. Yeh kai aakaar mein market mein available hota hain jinme \'Amir hazi\' , \'Saiyadi\', \'Khadravi\' aur \'Medjul\' kuch prakar hain.\n\n\n\n\n\n\n1. Kam kare Cholesterol \n\n\n\n\n\nKhajoor mein kisi prakar ka koi cholesterol nahi paya jata hain. Yani ki ise khane se motapa nahi badhta hain. Ise khane se sirf heart hi nahi balki puri body fit rahti hain. Per 100 Gram khajoor mein 1.6 gram fiber paya jata hain. Jo blood se cholesterol ki matra ko kam karta hain. Yeh kai prakar ki dil ki bimariyon se bachata hain.\n\n\n\n \n\n \n\n2. Protein ka khazana \n\n\n\nSharir mein balanced protein ki matra growth ke saath muscle banane ke liye jaroori hoti hain. Iski pryapt matra sharir ke function ko sahi rakhti hain. Khajoor mein protein acchi khasi matra  mein mauzood hota hain. Ise khana har tarike se sehat ke liye faydemand hota hain .\n\n\n\n\n\n3. Vitamin C ka Source\n\n\n\n\n\nKhajoor kai saare vitamins jaise vitamin B1, B2 , B3, aur Vitamin A aur C ka accha source hain. Vitamin A jise Anti-Oxidant ke roop mein jana jata hain. Aankhon aur skin ke liye bahut hi jaroori hota hain. Aankhon mein hone wali kai saari samsyaon se rahat dilane ke saath chehre ke jhurriyon ko khatm karta hain khajoor. Vitamin A ki matra Lung aur Oral cancer ko kafi kam kar deti hain. \n\n \n\n \n\n\n\n4. Body ko energy deta hain \n\n\n\n\n\nKhajoor mein glucose, sucrose aur fructose paye  jate hain, jo body mein energy banaye rakhne ke liye bahut hi jaroori hain. Doodh ke saath khajoor ka sewan karna bahut hi faydemand hota hain. Isme calorie ki matra na ke barabar hoti hain, Jo healthy rahne ke liye bahut hi jaroori hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n5. Nervous system ko behtar banata hain. \n\n\n\n\n\nKhajoor ki per 100 Gram matra mein 696 ml potassium ki matra hoti hain. jo sharir ke liye bahut hi faydemand hain. Yeh sharir ke naye cell aur fluid ko banati hain. Yeh heart ke saath hi blood pressure ko control mein rakhta hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n6. Khoon ki kami nahi hone deta \n\n\n\n\n\nKhajoor mein iron ki bharpoor matra payi jati hain, Jo sharir mein khoon ki kami nahi hone deti hain. Mahilaon mein hemoglobin ki samsya aamtaur par payi jati hain. Ise khajoor ka rozana sewan karke bahut jaldi hi door kiya ja sakta hain. Iske alawa fluorine ki matra daanto ki samsya se chhutkara dilati hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n7. Kabz se rahat\n\n\n\n\n\nFiber ke khazane se bharpoor khajoor ko kha kar kabz ki problem se jald hi chhutkara paya ja sakta hain. Iske liye khajoor ko raat mein pani mein bhigo kar rakh de aur Subah fresh hone se pahle ise kha le. \n\n \n\n \n\n\n\n\n8. Pet ke cancer se bachata hain\n\n\n\n\n\nIse khane se koi side-effect nahi hota hain, Balki fayde ke liye rozana iska sewan bahut hi labhkari hota hain. Khane ki aniyamta pet ki kai bimariyon ka karan hoti hain. Yeh dhyaan na dene par gambhir bimari ka roop le leti hain. Vaise to iske liye khane mein savdhani bartna bahut hi jaroori hain. Lekin Khajoor khane se pet ke cancer jaisi badi bimari se nipta ja sakta hain. aur to aur ratondhi , Aankhon se judi problem bhi door ho jati hain \n\n \n\n \n\n\n\n\nKhajoor se skin ko hone vale fayde\n\n\n\nKhajoor mein vitamin A, B1, B2, B3, Protein, Calcium, Phosphorus, Iron aur Magnesium skin ki andruni saaf safai karne ke saath hi uski chamak ko barkarar rakhte hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n9. Tavcha ke khichav ko banaye rakhna \n\n\n\n\n\nVitamin C aur Vitamin P ka khazana khajoor , tavcha ke khichav ko banaye rakhta hain. Iske saath hi uski komalta ko bhi barkarar rakhta hain. Rozana iske sewan se kair prakar ki skin problems, Infections aadi se bacha ja sakta hain. \n\n \n\n \n\n\n\n\n10. Anti-Aging  ka kaam \n\n\n\n\n\nFree Radicals skin cells ko damage kar deti hain. Jisse chehre par samay se pahle umr ka pata chalne lagta hain. Khajoor mein vitamin c hota hain, jo is problem se bachata hain. Sharir ko melanin ke hanikarak prabhavo se bachane ke saath hi jhurriyo, daag-dhabbon aadi se bhi door rakhta hain. Isse skin young rahti hain. \n\n\n\n\n\nKhajoor se balon ko hone wale fayde\n\n\n\nBalon ki sunderta ka sidha sambandh hamare khan-pan se hota hain. Balon ko jaroori matra mein neutrons   ki jaroorat hoti hain, jo balon ko healty banane ke liye bahut hi jaroori hota hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n11. Balon ki majbooti ke liye \n\n\n\n\n\nRozana 2-3 Khajoor khana balon ki jado ko majboot banata hain. Iske saath hi bejaan balon ki samsya ko door karke unhe lamba aur ghana banata hain. \n\n \n\n \n\n\n\n\n12. Balon ki jhadne ki samsya se rahat\n\n\n\n\n\nKhajoor mein mauzood Vitamin C Balon ke liye bahut hi labhkari hain. Iski kami hi asamay balo ka girna aur pakna jaise problem paida kar dete hain. Khoon ki kami bhi iski ek wajah ho sakti hain. To in saari samsyaon ko  jald se  jald door karne ke liye khajoor khana bahut hi labhkari hota hain. \n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPosted by\n\n\nHinglishpedia\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nShare to:\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nNextNewer Post\nPreviousOlder Post\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPost a Comment\n\n\n\naprieztmkrdezign\n218168578325095\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nSubscribe to:\nPost Comments (Atom)\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nLoading...\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPopulars\nComments\nArchive\n\n\n\n\nFeature Post\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nPopular\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\n\nRukhe baalo ko Silky banane ke gharelu upay aur nuskhe (Tips for Dry Hair to make Silky)\nKya aap Rukhe baalo (Dry Hair) se preshan hain?
